Athran Exigency (1 of 5)


Faction: Minmatar Republic
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (no gates)
Damage dealt: kin(60%), therm(40%)
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: therm, EM or exp
Recommended ships: Kestrel

Group 1

2x or 4x Frigates (Athran Agent) spawn when you are within 7 to 10 km from the station.

Tips:
  • Approach the mission objective (Abandoned Mining Station). Make it visible in the overview by enabling 'Types - Celestial - Large Collidable Object'.
  • The Station is 40km away from warp-in. The mission is flagged as complete when within 5km of the station.)


Athran Exigency (2 of 5)


Faction: Minmatar Republic
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: kin(70%), therm(30%)
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: therm, EM or exp

Look for signs of Athran Brotherhood activity; if attacked, destroy all hostiles.

Pocket 1


Group 1

Aggro on Warp-in.
Distance: 22-24km from warp-in.
5x Frigates (Athran Agent)

Structures
no loot

Pocket 2

Full Aggro on warp-in.

Group 1

Distance: 19-21km from warp-in.
2x Frigates (Athran Agent)

Group 2

Distance: 49-50km from warp-in.
6x Frigates (4x Athran Agent, 1x Athran Operative)

Structures
no loot

Tips:
  • A Battleship, the Republic Tribal Ymir, is exploding when you arrive in this pocket -leaving behind no wreckage. [Eat your heart out noob. You know that I did. ;-)]
  • Group 1 appears after the explosion of the above mentioned battleship.


Athran Exigency (3 of 5)


Faction: Minmatar Republic
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (no gates)
Damage dealt: kin(67%), therm(33%)
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: therm, EM or exp

Destroy the Athran Ammunitions Depot and any enemy ships.

Single Pocket


Group 1

Aggro on Warp-in.
Distance: 7-10km from warp-in.
4x Frigates (3x Athran Agent; 1x Athran Operative)

Group 2

Distance: 28km from warp-in.
4x Frigates (3x Athran Agent; 1x Athran Operative, or 4x Athran Agent)

Blitz
Destroy the "Amarr Starbase Control Tower", leaving the ships alone.

Tips:
  • The mission objective is the "Amarr Starbase Control Tower". Reveal it within the overview with "Entity - Large Collidable Structure".
  • As reported by CileniaBitweaver, groups 1 and 2 are on either side of the mission objective: 'Athran ammunitions depot'.
  • Aggro from both groups occurs when the control tower is attacked.

Athran Exigency (4 of 5)

Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: em(7%), exp(17%), kin(49%), therm(27%)
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: exp

Go to our Asteroid Colony Field Office and speak to our Assistant Chief Security Officer. If you get in any trouble, deal with it.

Pocket 1

No aggro on warp-in.

Group 1

Distance: 30-42km from Warp-in.
4x Frigates named "Repair Crew" (2x State Katana; 2x Imperial Sixtus)

Group 2

4x Frigates (Republic Skani; Republic Radur)
1x Cruiser (Republic Commander)

Mineable Asteroid info:
10x veldspar

Blitz
Go directly to the acceleration gate. Destruction of the "Repair Crew" is not necessary.

Tips:
  • Five 'Minmatar kamikaze pilot' frigs destroy themselves upon warp-in.
  • Group 1 wrecks drop Caldari & Amarr Navy insignia.
  • Group 2 spawns after the explosion of the above mentioned battleship.

Pocket 2

Full aggro on warp-in.

Group 1

Distance: 38-41km from warp-in.
4x Frigates (3x Republic Radur, 1x Republic Skani)
1x Cruiser (Republic Ormur)

Athran Exigency (5 of 5)

Faction: Minmatar Republic
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (no gates)
Damage dealt: kin(66%), therm(34%)
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: therm, EM or exp

Destroy all Athran ships.

Single Pocket

Full aggro on warp-in.

Group 1

Distance: 24-47km from warp-in.
9x Frigates (8x Athran Agent; 1x Athran Operative)

Group 2

4x Frigates (3x Athran Agent; 1x Cosensin Thrand)

Mineable Asteroid info:
16x scordite
16x veldspar

Structures
no loot

Tips:
  • Only 5 'Athran Agent' frigs were reported by CileniaBitweaver for 5 of 5, Group 1.
  • This 5 of 5 is a good mission to mine with large amounts of veldspare & scordite very near warp-in point. 60k per roid is estimated.
  • Got 990,202 Scordite & 895,122 Veldspar. [I] only had to make two moves to get the whole belt. The roids are 20k to 90k in size. from StevenCarter
  • [S]everal structures 'Athran proximity mine' are scattered throughout the asteroids. Be careful as they do explode. -CileniaBitweaver
