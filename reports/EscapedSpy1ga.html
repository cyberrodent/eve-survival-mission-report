Escaped Spy, Level 1


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:26 UTC


Faction: Gallente Federation (+Caldari Spy)
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: KN/TH
Recommended damage dealing: KN/TH
Recommended ships: Kestrel

Pocket #1


No aggro on warp-in. Firing on GROUP 1 aggro's GROUP 2. Acceleration gate 15km away. Structures drop loot.

GROUP 1 (~15-20km)

2x Federation Clavis (frigate)
1x Federation Kontos (frigate)

GROUP 2 (~40-45km)

2x Federation Clavis (frigate)
1x Federation Hastile (frigate)

Pocket #2


Aggro on warp-in (~10km). No loot from structures.

1x Corporate Spy (frigate)
2x Federation Clavis (frigate)
1x Federation Hoplon (frigate)
1x Federation Kontos (frigate)

Tip:
Destroying Corporate Spy completes requirement. "Prisoner" loot (1 m3) can be picked up from a floating container after destruction of Corporate Spy which is needed to complete mission.
