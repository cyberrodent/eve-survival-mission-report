Core Garrison, Class 5

Last edited by Markj2
Sat, 06 Feb 2016 12:12 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Cosmic Anomaly
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Omni
Extras: NOS, Stasis Webify, Warp Scramble
Webbers: Emergent Sentinel, Emergent Warden, Awakened Sentinel, Awakened Keeper, Sleepless Sentinel, Sleepless Guardian
Scramblers: Emergent Sentinel, Emergent Warden, Awakened Keeper, Sleepless Sentinel, Sleepless Guardian
Recommended ship classes: Capital, Battleship, T3 (7+ Remote Repair Modules)
Recommended generic setup: Multi-range, Heavy Omni-tank, Remote Repair (Spider Tanking)
Blue loot: 253.4 mill ISK / 838.4 mill ISK (Cap + Site)

All sleepers will auto aggress; Sleepers will switch targets, drones and support ships are not immune.

Single Pocket


Capital escalation waves spawn between 60 and 65km directly above the initial warp to 0, show sensor overlay to see the initial warp to 0 location.
