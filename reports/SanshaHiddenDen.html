Sansha Hidden Den


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Sat, 28 Mar 2015 20:18 UTC

Faction: Sansha
Location: Lowsec & Nullsec
Damage Dealt: EM/Therm
Web/Scramble: Centii Loyal Minion/Ravener/Scavenger/Servant
Extras: Tracking Disruption Centii Savage/Slavehunter
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM (best), Therm(secondary)

Single Pocket


On warp-in, only the missile battery will agro. Engaging anything will agro the whole room. Trigger is final kill of specified ship type.

Initial Group

1x Sansha Heavy Missile Battery
9-10x Frigate (Centii Butcher/Enslaver/Manslayer/Plague)
9-11x Destroyer (Centior Horror/Monster)
4-5x Cruiser (Centum Mutilator/Torturer)
2-3x Battlecruiser (Centatis Devi/Wraith) Trigger

Wave 1

3-4x Frigate (Centii Enslaver/Plague)
2-3x Cruiser (Centum Ravager/Ravisher) Trigger

Wave 2

3-4x Frigate (Centii Enslaver/Plague)
2-3x Destroyer (Centior Horror/Monster) Trigger

Wave 3

3-4x Frigate (Centii Savage/Slavehunter) Tracking Disruption
2-3x Cruiser (Centum Mutilator/Torturer)
3x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon) Trigger

Wave 4

3-4x Frigate (Centii Savage/Slavehunter) Tracking Disruption
4-5x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon/Specter) Trigger
Note: Sometimes the next wave will trigger on the last Daemon kill.

Wave 5

3x Frigate (Centii Savage/Slavehunter) Tracking Disruption
2-3x Cruiser (Centum Mutilator/Torturer) Trigger

Wave 6

1-2x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Ravener/Scavenger/Servant) Scramble & Web
3x Cruiser (Centum Ravager/Ravisher)
4-5x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon) Trigger

Wave 7

2x Frigate (Centii Enslaver/Plague)
2-3x Cruiser (Centum Mutilator/Torturer)
3x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon) Trigger

Wave 8

4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Minion/Ravener/Servant) Scramble & Web
2-3x Cruiser (Centum Ravager/Ravisher)
6-7x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon)
