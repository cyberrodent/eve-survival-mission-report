Pirate Intrusion, level 1


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:37 UTC

Faction: Blood Raiders
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM, Therm
Extras: Tracking Disruptions from Corpii Seeker and Corpii Collector
Recommended damage dealing: EM, Therm

Pocket 1

You can aggro one group at a time, but if you get close to a group you will aggro it.
Closing in on the acceleration gate will agro group 4.

Group 1: (30km)

2x Corpii Seeker (Frigate class)

Group 2: (40km)

2x Corpii Raider (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Seeker (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Collector (Frigate class)

Group 3: (50km)

2x Corpii Herald (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Collector (Frigate class)

Group 4: (55km)

2x Corpii Reaver (Frigate class)

Pocket 2


Group 1: (25km)

2x Corpii Collector (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Reaver (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Seeker (Frigate class)

Group 2: (30km)

1x Corpii Seeker (Frigate class)
2x Corpii Collector (Frigate class)

Group 3: (40-60km)

1x Corpii Diviner (Frigate class)
2x Corpii Raider (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Engraver (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Reaver (Frigate class)
1x Corpior Devoter (Destroyer class)
