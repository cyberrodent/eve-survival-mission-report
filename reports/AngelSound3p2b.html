Chapter 1


Chapter 2 - Heaven


Mission 6 - Serpentis Fill-In


Gated Deadspace complex, no guards. Deliver the Sealed Research Cache into the Serpentis Transport Hub structure in the only room.

Mission 7 - Ride to the Rescue


Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/Scramble: Yukiro Demense
Recommended damage dealing: Kin (best), Therm (secondary)

Gated complex, no guards on initial gate.
On warp-in there will be a lot of Angel and Guristas rats that will destroy each other leaving only a few Guristas for you to deal with.

Single Room

Initial Group:
3x Frigate (Pithi Infiltrator/Invader)
1x Frigate (Yukiro Demense) Web & Scram
1x Battlecruiser (Pithatis Executor)

Mission 8 - The Best Kind of Revenge


Faction: Mercenary
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: All
Web/Scramble: Mercenary Wingman
Recommended damage dealing: Therm (best), Kin (secondary)

Gated complex, no guards on initial gate.
Last kill triggers next wave.

Single Room

Initial Group:
2x Cruiser (Mercenary Commander)
2x Cruise Missile Battery

Reinforcement Wave 1:
7x Elite Frigate (Mercenary Elite Fighter)

Reinforcement Wave 2:
4x Frigate (Mercenary Rookie/Wingman) Wingman Scram & Web

Mission 9 - Wrath of Angels


Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/Scramble: Yukiro Demense
Recommended damage dealing: Kin (best), Therm (secondary)

Gated complex, no guards on initial gate.

Single Room

Initial Group:
2x Guristas Cruise Missile Battery
1x Frigate (Yukiro Demense) Scram & Web

First arc reward 1x Domination Target Painter

Mission 10 - Dominus


Report to Kadeja Dulche in K-QWHE (K-QWHE VII - Moon 8 - Dominations Assembly Plant).
