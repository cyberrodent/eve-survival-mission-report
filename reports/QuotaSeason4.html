Quota Season, level 4 Storyline


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sun, 29 Jul 2012 17:17 UTC

Faction: Angels
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal
Damage dealt: Explosive, Kinetic
Recommended damage dealing: Explosive, Kinetic

Pocket 1


Group 1, 55km, Aggro

2x Cruiser (Gistum Phalanx/Marauder)
2x BS (Gist Throne)
Reinforcements spawn at last enemy killed

3x Frigate (Gistii Hunter)
1x Cruiser (Gistum Phalanx/Marauder)
2x BC (Gistatus Primus)
8x BS (Gist Malakim/Throne)

Reinforcements, 55km

2x BC (Gistatis Tribuni)
2x BS (Gist Malakim)

There is a lot of DPS in this mission, so you may have to warp out. Leave one Throne from the first group active and take out the Malakims to reduce incoming DPS. Sniping is possible.

Destroy the AI Storage Unit (not the Drone Production Lines) to get the cargo container needed for mission completion.

Blitz: Destroy Drone AI Storage Unit, collect can, warp off.

Mining
1.22MM Dense Veldspar, 4k Scordite, 411k Solid Pyroxeres
