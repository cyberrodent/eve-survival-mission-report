Illegal Activity, level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:44 UTC


Part 1 of 3

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal
Damage Dealing: Kin/Therm (plus some EM)
Web/Scramble: Navy Delta II Support Frigate
Recommended damage: Kin/Therm
Recommended ships: Hawk

Warp in

Takes a couple of minutes for the ships to warp in.

Wave 1

Won't fire back.
6x Frigates (Atron) (Miners)

Wave 2

You may get one ship more or less of each kind

3/4x Frigates (Maulus) (Gallente Navy Officer )
3/4x Frigates (Tristan) (Navy Delta II Support Frigate) (Web)

Hint: Delta II Support Frigates are orbiting @ 1200m.

Blitz
Kill everything in 2nd wave. Loot the mission objective from last killed ship (1x Gallente Light Marines). Warp out. You don't need to take care of miners.

Loot
I know ship loot shouldn't be reported but this one is a loot you always get on that mission: miners drop Gallente Mining Laser.

Asteroids
17-18 Veldspar asteroids, quantity 350.000 unitys.

Part 2 of 3:

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal
Damage Dealing: Kin/Therm (plus some EM)
Web/Scramble: Navy Delta II Support Frigate, Gallente Navy Detective.
Recommended damage: Kin/Therm
Recommended ships: Hawk

Warp in


Single Group

Instant aggro. All Web/Scrambles.
3x Frigates (Maulus) (Navy Delta II Support Frigate)
3x Frigates (Celestis) (Gallente Navy Detective)

Part 3 of 3:

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage Dealing: Kin/Therm (plus some EM)
Web/scramble: Navy Delta II support frigs, Navy Detective.
Recommended damage: Kin/Therm
Recommended ships: Hawk

Warp in


Single Ship

1x Gallente Navy Vexor
It will drop a RS Officer's Keycard, required to jump through the acceleration gate.

Pocket (after gate)

Groups 2 and 3 will aggro separately.

Group 1 (20km)

Splited in three groups surounding the warp in location. Aggro after a few sec.
3x Cruisers (Gallente Navy Vexor)
3x Gallente Sentry Gun

Group 2 (90km right gate side)

You may get one ship more or less of each kind.
2/3x Cruisers (Gallente Navy Thorax/Gallente Navy Vexor)
2x Interceptors (Gallente Navy Gamma I Support Frigate) (Web)

Group 3 (90km left gate side)

90km from warp in point
3x Cruisers (Gallente Navy Thorax/Gallente Navy Vexor)

Group 4 (60km, near gate)

Warps in after a few minutes.
2x Roden Shipyards Courier (Industrial)
5x Frigates (Tristan) (Navy Gallente Officer) Courier Escort

Blitz
Pocket 2 only requires group 4 to be destroyed, so groups 2 and 3 are optional.

Asteroids
There might spawn an omber roid in the asteroid field.
