Recommended damage dealing:

After The Seven - "Ruse" (4 of 5)


A Caldari Shuttle will be granted when accepting the mission. Use the shuttle to accomplish the mission.

Pocket 1

Anakism will spawn at 35-40km. He cannot be attacked and will not be aggroed until you pick up the hostages in The Seven Holding Cells at around 20km.
Once hostages are picked up, run to the acceleration gate.

Pocket 2

Stolen CONCORD ship (a container) will spawn near you. Transfer the hostages in the cargo and your journal will update.

After The Seven - "Decision" (5 of 5)


When warp in, Locced (Destroyer) will be in your 20km. If you attack him, 2 extra Pithi Invaders will spawn at ~25km.

Locced's Bribe only contains 1 unit of "Metal Scraps." Kill him.

Some Veldspar can be found in this dead space (50+ of small asteroids).
