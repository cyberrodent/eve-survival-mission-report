Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:19 UTC

Faction: Gurista Pirates / Mercenaries
Mission type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kin, Therm

When structures are shot at the following will spawn:

7x Frigates (Pithi Arrogators/Imputors)
4x Repair Station's Point Defense Battery

When repair stations are shot at the following will spawn:

5x Frigates (Mercenary Frigates/Rookies)

Standing:
Killing the Repair Outpost incurs a -4% standing penalty.
