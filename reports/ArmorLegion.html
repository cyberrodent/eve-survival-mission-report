Legion


Role
  • Best armor cruiser for running incursions.
  • Good close range and midrange dps with pulse lasers.
  • No sniper role (typical).

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Medium Pulse Laser Specialization 3
  • Medium Beam Laser Specialization 3
  • Controlled Bursts 5
  • Amarr Engineering Systems 5
  • Amarr Offensive Systems 5
  • Amarr Electronic Systems 5
  • Amarr Defensive Systems 4
  • Amarr Propulsion Systems 4
  • Energy Weapon Rigging 4

Fitting Notes

Regular
  • Energy Locus Coordinator rigs fitted : Multifrequency/Conflagration range up to 28km, Scorch range up to 65km.
  • Heavy Pulse fitted.
  • Fuel Catalyst subsystem reduces align time and boosts afterburner speed (if fitted).
  • Tactical Targeting Network subsytem significantly reduces target lock time.
  • Ship's EM-Thermal resistance is given more priority for better defence against more damaging laser hits.
  • Typical rigs : Energy Locus Coordinator / Anti-EM Pump / Anti-Thermic Pump.

MWD
  • Energy Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator rigs fitted to maximize short range damage.
  • Heavy Pulse fitted.
  • Chassis Optimization subsystem maximizes MWD speed (if fitted).
  • Tactical Targeting Network subsytem significantly reduces target lock time.
  • Slot limitations result in slightly lower EM-Thermal resistance profile. Within acceptable limits.
  • Typical rigs : Energy Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator / Anti-EM Pump / Anti-Thermic Pump.
  • Damage Control II + EANM combination avoids triple stacked resist penalty if an OGB is present.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • Stationary fleets do not require for afterburner to be fitted.
  • Mobile fleets require either an afterburner or MWD to be fitted, plus sometimes a Code Breaker module.
  • Heavy Pulse Laser fitted (typical).

Assault/HQ
  • Afterburner or MWD fitted (typical).
  • Heavy Pulse Laser fitted (typical).
  • Last mid-slot is typically fitted with a Target Painter.
