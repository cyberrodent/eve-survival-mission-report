Angel Haven


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Fri, 04 Jul 2014 15:29 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel
Location: Nullsec
Damage Dealt: Exp/Kin
Web/Scramble: Arch Gistii Outlaw/Rogue/Thug
Extras: Target Painting Arch Gistii Nomad/Ruffian
Recommended Damage Dealing: Exp(best), Kin(secondary)

Single Pocket

Two stripes of clouds with tiny little asteroids around the warp in point (watch out if you orbit).

All waves aggress upon spawning.

Two types of site, scroll down for the second.

Initial Group

2x Turrets (types)
5x Elite Cruisers (Arch Gistum Liquidator) Trigger
3x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Legionnaire/Primus)
7x Battleships (Gist Cherubim/Seraphim/Throne)

Wave 1

4x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Ambusher/Hunter/Impaler)
3x Elite Cruisers (Arch Gistum Centurion/Phalanx)
2x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Legatus) Trigger
3x Battleships (Gist Malakim/Throne)

Wave 2

2x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Outlaw) Scram & Web
3x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Praefectus/Tribuni) Trigger
3x Battleships (Gist Malakim/Throne)

Wave 3

2x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Hijacker)
6x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Legatus/Tribuni/Tribunus) Trigger
4x Battleships (Gist Cherubim/Seraphim)

Wave 4

3x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Nomad/Ruffian) Target Painting
3x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Praefectus)
7x Battleships (Gist Cherubim/Malakim/Seraphim/Throne) Trigger

Wave 5

3x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Outlaw/Rogue/Thug) Scram & Web
4x Battleships (Gist Cherubim/Seraphim)

OPTIONAL: Domination Nephilim
