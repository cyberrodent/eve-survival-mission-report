Recon (1 of 3)

Faction: Gurista Pirates
Mission Type: Encounter
Spacetype: Deadspace with gates
Damage Dealt: Kinetic/Thermal
Web/scramble: Dire Pithi Invader
Extras: Jamming (Dire Pithum Annihilator and Nullifier)
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic/Thermal
Recommended ships: Shuttle (blitz), Rattlesnake, Raven, Drake, Ishtar, Dominix
Video: Kronos Part 1,2 & 3

Leads to: Recon (2 of 3), Level 4

Blitz
The gates are not locked, so you can blitz this mission without a single kill.

Pocket 1

Groups spawn approximately 1 minute apart, starting just after warp-in.
Will spawn approximately 30-45km away and big ships orbit around 32km.
All groups aggro on spawn, though they will aggro drones that are out as well.

Spawn 1:

1x Elite Cruiser (Dire Pithum Abolisher/Eraser)
1x Battlecruiser (Pithatis Death Dealer)
1x Battleship (Pith Usurper/Massacrer)

Spawn 2:

1x Elite Frigate (Dire Pithi Infiltrator/Invader) (Web/Scramble)
1x Elite Cruiser (Dire Pithum Nullifier/Annihilator) (Jamming)
1x Battleship (Pith Usurper/Massacrer)

Spawn 3:

1x Elite Frigate (Dire Pithi Infiltrator/Invader) (Web/Scramble)
2x Battleships (Pith Usurper/Massacrer)

Spawn 4:

1x Elite Cruiser (Dire Pithum Nullifier/Annihilator) (Jamming)
2x Battleships (Pith Usurper/Massacrer)

Bunker has chance of loot, but did not drop for author.
No other structure drops loot.

Bounty:
8,125,500.00 ISK

Pocket 2

Aggro on Warp-in, warp in at about 30km, big ships orbit at around 30-35k.
Second group did not can aggro on drones.

Spawn 1:

2x Battleships (Pith Conquistador/Destroyer)
2-3x Destroyers (Pithior Guerilla)

Spawn 2:

4x Elite Frigates (Dire Pithi Invader/Arrogator) (Web/Scramble)
3-4x Elite Cruisers (Dire Pithium Inferno/Mortifier)

Blitz-
Go to warp gate, activate it, when you reach the next pocket you are done!


Bounty:


10,486,500.00 ISK

Mining

Second pocket has 146 Veldspar Asteroids with an average quantity of 40k, totaling approx 5.8m Veldspar, all within 40k of warpin, over half within 15k (after 25k travel to first gate).
