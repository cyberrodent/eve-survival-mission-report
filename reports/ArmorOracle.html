Oracle


Role
  • Average close range and midrange dps with pulse lasers.
  • Above average sniper.

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Large Pulse Laser Specialization 3
  • Large Beam Laser Specialization 3
  • Controlled Bursts 5
  • Battlecruisers 4

Fitting Notes
  • Ability to perform at both close range and sniper roles without re-rigging is heavily emphasized.
  • Mega Pulse / Mega Beam fitted.
  • One rig slot is wasted for Vanguards to accomodate an Ancillary Current Router which allows swapping to sniper configuration.
  • Typical rigs : Ancillary Current Router / Anti-Kinetic Pump / Anti-Explosive Pump.
  • Slot limitations result in slightly lower Kin-Exp resistance profile. Acceptable with the ship's cruiser sized signature radius.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • No afterburner (typical).
  • Mega Pulse Laser fitted (typical).

Assault
  • No afterburner (typical).
  • Mega Beam Laser fitted (typical).
  • Mid slot requires 2 Sensor Booster to target 150km distance.

HQ
  • ATTENTION!! DO NOT use for HQ sites.
