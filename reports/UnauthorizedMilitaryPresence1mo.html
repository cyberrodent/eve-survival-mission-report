Unauthorized Military Presence, Level 1


Last edited by LoneGunman
Thu, 13 Sep 2012 00:38 UTC


Faction: Mordus
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kinetic, Thermal, Kinetic missiles.
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Reccomended damage dealing: Kinetic

First pocket


Initial Spawn:
1x Frigate (Mordus Sabre) spawn trigger

Second Spawn:

6x Frigate (Mordus Rookies)

Second pocket


Initial Spawn:
5x Frigates (Modus Rookies)

Second Spawn (after 2 minutes)
6x Frigates (Modus Rookies/Sabres)
1x Personnel Transport

Mineable Asteroid info
First Pocket - 80+ asteroids about 10km away: 60% veldspar, 20% Plagioclase, 20% Pyroxeres
Second Pocket - 60+ asteroids about 50km away: 60% veldspar, 20% Plagioclase, 19% Pyroxeres, 1% Omber


Standing loss
Killing the Transport Ship incurs a -4.0% standing loss

Tips:
•The gate to Pocket 2 is unlocked
•You can't just kill the Transport ship Collect the Marines and book, had to kill 4 out of 5 Frigates to complete mission.
