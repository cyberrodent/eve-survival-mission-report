Worlds Collide, level 4


Last edited by Jedi2155
Mon, 09 Feb 2015 01:49 UTC

Faction: Serpentis & Guristas Pirates. Serpentis in the last pocket.
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace, with guards at the first gates.
Damage dealt: (Kin, Therm - for Guristas) / (Therm, Kin - for Serpentis)
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic - Guristas / Kin, Therm - Serpentis
Recommended ships: Rokh, Drake, Maelstrom
Video: http://youtu.be/ZakdjTANDcc , Golem , Vargur

Warp in "Warzone stage"


You arrive in the middle of two large groups and two gates. One for your primary enemy, and one for the faction mentioned in the mission briefing (in this case, Serpentis and Gurista). They start far away from you guarding their own gate. The gates are locked until you kill the last ship. But you can unlock them by carrying Zbikoki's Hacker Card (obtained from Zor) in your cargohold. It will be consumed on activation.
The guristas/pith ships will aggro your drones after a while if they haven't aggroed anything else.
The ships are sitting 100km away from their gates, you better have some long range hitting ship or a fast one.
Do not use heavy drones! Rats will switch aggro to drones.

The overseer:
  • The overseer is a special ship that will spawn in the first room of each path.
  • The overseer has a value of between 487,500 and 4,875,000 ISK.
  • The higher value one drops faction loot.
  • The overseer spawn point is with the group that does not aggro at the beginning of the stage.
  • The low value overseer is just like an extra battleship.
  • The high value overseer has a MWD (was moving at about 600 m/s or faster), but has crazy low resists.
  • It has, in its description, "sometimes they hire a good pilot to fly their ships" and was a dominix model.
  • The highest bounty might drop a Serpentis Blueprint.
  • The overseer doesn't aggro until you shoot it.
  • Doesn't always spawn! (Edit 4-11-2014)

Serpentis Gate

4x Frigate (Guardian Veteran) Web/Scram
4x Battlecruiser (Corelatis Captain Sentry)
4x Battleship (Core Rear Admiral)

Guristas Gate

2x Cruiser (Dire Pithum Eraser/Dire Pithum Abolisher)
3x Battleship (Pith Eliminator/Pith Extinguisher)

Ships will leash back after 30-40km.

Blitz

- Kill the 4 battleships to unlock gate

Serpentis Pocket 1:

Aggro at warp-in from one group. 4 Groups total. All pretty close and can apply a lot of DPS if full room aggro occurs, which happens sometimes on warp-in. Guardian Veterans may web and scramble. Battleships in all groups. And there two angel spies in the pocket.

Gamtu discovered: The station in the Gurista pocket dropped loot once. Basic t1 stuff.

Serpentis Pocket 2: (Crash site)

You will stop in the middle of 4 groups. Great for short range weapons. No web/scramblers. Aggro from one group. If you shoot only the aggroed Group, you won't get stageaggro. After finishing the first group take a NPC near the gate and you will get aggro from the next group and so on. There's a damaged Heron in the middle as a spawn container. Open it, and grab 20 "ships crew" when you have destroyed close to everything.
In my case final room (Crash site) has guristas pirates that jamed me alot :)

Group 1: 5km
Aggro at warp in
3x Battlecruiser (Corelatis High Captain/Corelatis Captain Sentry)
3x Battleship (Core Rear Admiral)

Group 2: 5-10km
Distance aggro under 5km
3x Battleship (Core Rear Admiral/Core Vice Admiral/Core Flotilla Admiral) Sensor Damp

Group 3: 5-10km
Distance aggro under 5km
3x Battlecruiser (Corelatis Squad Leader/Corelatis Wing Leader)
1x Battleship (Core Vice Admiral) Sensor Damp

Group 4: 5-10km
Distance aggro under 5km
3x Destroyer (Corelior Trooper/Corelior Soldier)
2x Battleship (Core Commadore/Core Port Admiral)

"Gate to the warzone" leads back to the Warzone stage.

Blitz
The only group you need to destroy to "unlock" the Heron is the one that spawns first. It consists of one Core Flotilla Admiral, two Core Rear Admirals and three Corelatis Captain Sentries. When the last ship belonging to that group is destroyed, the journal button flashes. You can then grab the crew from the Heron and leave.
None of the other groups that spawn soon afterwards aggro, and neither do they object to drones.

Bounty
26,700,000.00 (full clear, both paths)
