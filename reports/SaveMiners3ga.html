Save The Miners, Level 3


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Thu, 01 May 2014 19:30 UTC

Faction: Gallente Federation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: Therm/Kin
Extras: damp (cruisers)
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Therm, Kin
Recommended ships: Hurricane, Ferox

Warp-In

Group upon warp in may vary.

3-6x Gallente Privateer (Cruiser)

Pocket 1


Destroying the last Privateer spawns the next wave. The last Privateer of the 2nd spawn drops the cargo container with the mission objective in it.

Spawn 1 (warp-in)

3x Gallente Privateer (Battlecruiser)Final kill of these is trigger
1x Gallente Privateer Leader (Battlecruiser)

Spawn 2

2x Gallente Privateer (Cruiser)Final kill is trigger

Spawn 3

2x Gallente Privateer (Battlecruiser)

Blitz
No blitz, Can drops from last ship killed.

Tags to be looted
3x Federation Navy Command Sergeant Major Insignia I (~ cruisers in Pocket 1)
7x Federation Navy Fleet Captain Insignia I
7x Federation Navy Fleet Major Insignia I
