Liberate the Miners, Level 5


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:16 UTC

Faction: Amarr
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with Gate
Web/Scramble: Stasis Towers, All Frigates
Extras: Amarr Energy Neutralizer Sentry IIIs, Tracking Disrupter
Damage dealt: EM 56% / Therm 44%
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ships: Completed in (1) Maelstrom w/ Passive Tank and (1) Maelstrom w/ Shield Transporters

Acceleration Gate into Deadspace

Single Pocket

All groups aggro within 10 seconds of warp-in



Tower Sentries

Between 25-80km away

2 x Amarr Heavy Missile Batteries
5 x Amarr Cruise Missile Batteries
2 x Tower Sentry Amarr IIIs
2 x Amarr Stasis Towers
2 x Amarr Energy Neutralizer Sentry IIIs (Destroying Neutralizers will spawn replacements 3 times, after which there will spawn 6 eWar frigates)

Group 1 – Broken Metallic Crystal Asteroid

70km from warp-in

4 x Battleships (Imperial Templar Martyr) orbits at 49k

Group 2 – Amarr Control Tower

45k from warp-in

4 x Frigates (Divine Imperial Imran/Sixtus)orbits at 4k, Web/Scramble
2 x Frigates (Divine Imperial Napat) (separate) orbits at 10k, Web/Scramble, 30% Tracking Disrupter
4 x Battleships (Imperial Templar Ultara) orbits at 49k, -Trigger 1-

Group 3 – Cloven Grey Asteroid

55k from warp-in

4 x Battleships (Imperial Martyr*/Imperial Templar Torah) orbits at 17-44k, *25% NOS, -Trigger 2-


- Spawn 1

Around Amarr Control Tower

4 x Battleships (Imperial Templar Dominator/Martyr) orbits at 17-49k

- Spawn 2

Around Cloven Grey Asteroid

4 x Battleships (Imperial Templar Dominator/Martyr ) orbits at 17-49k, -Trigger 3-

- Spawn 3

Around Cloven Grey Asteroid

4 x Battleships (Imperial Templar Dominator/Martyr) orbits at 17-49k, -Trigger 4-

- Spawn 4

Around Cloven Grey Asteroid

2 x Battleships (Imperial Templar Judgment) orbits at 49k

Tips
  • Use a Passive Tank and save time by not having to destroy Energy Neutralizer Sentries which re-spawn themselves 3 times, and then spawn frigates
  • Or plan a capacitor that receives an additional 80 units/s
  • Maximum damage dealing of initial groups are 1806 hp/s (EM 58%, Therm 42%)
  • Mission flagged complete when all ships are destroyed
