Sansha Sanctum


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Thu, 24 Mar 2016 11:15 UTC

Faction: Sansha
Location: Nullsec
Damage Dealt: EM/Therm
Web/Scramble: Centii Loyal Minion/Ravener/Scavenger/Servant
Extras: Tracking Disruption Centii Loyal Savage/Slavehunter & Centum Loyal Beast/Execrator/Juggernaut/Slaughterer
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM (best), Therm(secondary)

This anomaly has two different variants, each Sanctum will be one or the other. The 'Ring' variant will be categorized by a series of five rings of POS shield hardeners. The 'Station' variant is categorized by a station with an asteroid belt.

Ring Variant


On warp-in everything will agro. Final kill is trigger for next wave.

Initial Group

3-4x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon/Specter/Wraith)
6-8x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)

Wave 1

3-4x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon/Specter/Wraith)
3-4x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)

Wave 2

3x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Beast/Juggernaut/Slaughterer) Tracking Disruption
4x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)

Wave 3

3-4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Minion/Ravener/Scavenger/Servant) Scramble & Web
3-4x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)

Wave 4

4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Minion/Ravener/Scavenger/Servant) Scramble & Web
4x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Fiend/Hellhound/Torturer)
6-7x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)


Station Variant


On warp-in everything will agro. Final kill is trigger for next wave.

Inital Group

3-4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Enslaver/Manslayer/Plague)
4x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Beast/Execrator/Juggernaut/Slaughterer) Tracking Disruption
7x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)

Wave 1

3-4x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Hellhound/Mutilator/Torturer)
3x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)

Wave 2

3-4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Butcher/Enslaver/Manslayer/Plague)
4x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)

Wave 3

3-4x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon/Devil/Phantasm/Specter/Wraith)
3-4x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)

Wave 4

4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Savage/Slavehunter) Tracking Disruption
2x Cruiser (Centum Fiend/Hellhound/Torturer)
6x Battleship (Centus Dark Lord/Dread Lord/Overlord/Tyrant) Trigger

Wave 5

3x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Savage/Slavehunter) Tracking Disruption
4x Destroyer (Centior Horror/Monster)
3-4x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon)
4x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant) Trigger

Wave 6

2-3x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Manslayer/Plague)
3x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Fiend/Hellhound)
3-4x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Devil/Phantasm/Wraith)
3-4x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)
