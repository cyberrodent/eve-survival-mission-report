Alluring Emanations, Level 5


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:54 UTC

Faction: Rogue Drones
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Web/Scramble: None
Extras: None
Damage dealt: EM/Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Therm/Kin
Recommended ship classes: Any ship with at least some combat capabilities, even a rookie ship.

Direct warp into Deadspace. No Gate (in case you want to bring in your Capital Ship)

Warp-In

Ship at 50k



1 x Frigate – Rogue Drone (Tarantula Apis) Trigger, Objective

Destroy it and Mission Complete. Really. That’s it. It’s done, just go…

Spawn 1

Upon destroying the lone drone there spawns 43 drones between 70-130km away

5 x Arachula Apis
3 x Asmodeus Apis
7 x Beelzebub Apis
3 x Belphegor Apis
6 x Incubus Apis
3 x Malphas Apis
3 x Mammon Apis
1 x Moth Apis
3 x Scorpionfly Apis
4 x Tarantula Apis
5 x Termite Apis

You really don’t need to destroy these. Seriously. The mission is complete. Just take the Loyalty Points and run…


But for those who insist on sticking around because they must kill everything; at least make it easy on yourself and bring along a Smartbomb. It takes 2 minutes for all the drones to reach you (plenty of time for a refill…)



One smartbomb blast will finish the room and you will reign as a giant among your peers.

Tips
  • Yes, this is the exact same mission as the one assigned in levels 1-3
  • Maximum damage dealing of all drones together is 132hp/s (EM 52%, Therm 48%)
  • As with most Level 5s, bring a fleet to stave off low-sec pirates
