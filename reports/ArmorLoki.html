Loki


Role
  • Average close range dps with 425mm autocannons. Poor mid range dps if artillery fitted.
  • No sniper role (typical).
  • Web range bonus enable up to 35km webbing with Federation Navy webs (without OGB bonus).

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Medium Autocannon Specialization 3
  • Medium Artillery Specialization 3
  • Minmatar Engineering Systems 4
  • Minmatar Offensive Systems 5
  • Minmatar Electronic Systems 5
  • Minmatar Defensive Systems 5
  • Minmatar Propulsion Systems 4

Fitting Notes
  • Projectile Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator rigs fitted to maximize short range damage.
  • 425mm Autocannon fitted.
  • Afterburner : Fuel Catalyst subsystem reduces align time and maximizes afterburner speed.
  • MWD : Chassis Optimization subsystem maximizes microwarp speed.
  • Ship's low Kin-Exp profile is not a big issue. It's cruiser sized signature radius will negate a major amount of Kin-Exp missile damage.
  • Typical rigs : Projectile Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator / Anti-Kinetic Pump / Anti-Explosive Pump / Trimark Armor Pump.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • Stationary fleets do not require an afterburner.
  • Mobile fleets require either an afterburner or MWD. A Code Breaker module may also be needed.
  • 425mm Autocannon fitted (typical).
  • Targets are typically webbed down at 20km or below.

Assault/HQ
  • Afterburner or MWD fitted (typical).
  • 425mm Autocannon fitted (typical).
  • Long range webbing for cruiser sized targets such as Mara Paleo, Arnon Epithalamus and etc.
