Breeding Facility, Level 5

Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:55 UTC

Faction: Amarr
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace, no gates
Web/Scramble: All Frigates
Extras: Energy Neutralizer Sentries, Tracking Disrupters
Damage dealt: EM / Therm
Recommended damage dealing: EM / Therm
Recommended ships: Maelstrom w/ Passive Tank plus logistics.

Direct warp into Deadspace. No gates.

Warp-In

Ships at 15-100km from Warp-In



6 x Frigates (Divine Imperial Napat) orbits at 10km, Web/Scramble, Tracking Disrupter
12 x Destroyer (Imperial Templar Caius/Crusader) orbits at 8km
16 x Amarr Cruise Missile Batteries
2 x Amarr Stasis Tower Trigger 1
4 x Amarr Energy Neutralizer Sentry IIIs Trigger 2

Timed Spawn

4 minutes after warp-in there arrives at the Breeding Facilities

2 x Battleships (Imperial Templar Martyr) orbits at 49k
3 x Amarr Slave Transport Ships (Transports don’t always appear, and only stay for 3 minutes then leave)


Stasis Tower Spawn

Destroying each Stasis Tower spawns (each):

2 x Battlecruisers (Imperial Templar Justicar) orbits at 10km
1 x Battleship (Imperial Templar Martyr) orbits at 49km


Neutralizer Tower Spawns

Each Neutralizer Tower that is shot will spawn (each):

3 x Cruisers (Divine Imperial Champion/Justicar) orbits of 14-32km
2 x Battlecruisers (Imperial Templar Justicar) orbits at 10km


Breeding Facilities

One Amarrian Breeding Facility triggers the “Objective Complete” and the following spawn

3 x Battleships (Imperial Templar Dominator/Martyr) orbits at 17-49km


Tips
  • Set drones to “Passive” or they will trigger the Neutralizer Tower spawns

Done this in two Maelstroms. With 80% resis on the tank you need about 200dps shield transfer capstable to negate peak damage. Concentrate artis on the missle batteries while the drones kill the frigates. Watch them not to scratch the Towers. After that it gets easy.

To blitz, head straight for the breeding facilities, they go down in 1-2 strikes. This could even work solo (with sufficient resists). Ships with cap needs wont prevail.
