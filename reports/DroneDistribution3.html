Did it in my hulk with 5x Hobgoblin to handle the drones (rats). After the 7 Splinter Alvi were dispatched (took longer to target them, than to kill them) I recalled the Hobgoblins and released the 5x Mining Drone I.

Sent the drones to one roid and strip miners on 3 other roids. Took 2 cycles (less than one 3 minute cycle per roid... under 6 minutes total) to pop all 7 asteroids in the space and give me exactly 40,000 Banidine to complete the quest.

1x Sansha's Minion (Small Belt Rat)
