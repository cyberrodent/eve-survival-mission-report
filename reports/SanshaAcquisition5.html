Sansha Acquisition, Level 5


Last edited by KreaTurs:
cruise missile spawns
Wed, 19 Nov 2014 18:15 UTC

Faction: Sansha’s Nation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace, no gate
Web/Scramble: All Frigates, Stasis Tower
Extras: Tracking Disrupter
Damage dealt: EM 64%, Therm 36%
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ships: (1) Maelstrom w/ Active Tank and (1) Maelstrom w/ Shield Transporters , Ishtar (Active tanked) with sentries for sniping, Paladin as used for Sansha level 4 missions (2 EM + 1 Thermal hardener) (warp in at 50 or more and snipe from smallest/fatest to largest/slowest)
Completed in: Tengu with passive tank, kill batteries, then statis tower, then smaller->larger ships

Direct warp into Deadspace. No gate.

Warp-In


4 Battlecruisers and 4 Battleships are present around the Battletower at 50k from warp-in. Immediate aggro.
More ships spawn around the Battletower, every 30 seconds for 5 minutes, until there is a total of:



12 x Frigates (Centii Loyal Manslayer/Minion/Ravener/Savage*/Slavehunter*) orbits of 4-10k, Web/Scramble, *30% Tracking Disrupter
9 x Destroyers (Centior Abomination/Devourer/Misshape/Monster) orbits of 8-16k
13 x Cruisers (Centum Fiend/Hellhound/Loyal Mutilator/Loyal Torturer) orbits of 10-32k
6 x Battlecruisers (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon/Specter) orbits at 9-22k
7 x Battleships (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant) orbits at 17-49k
6 x Sansha Cruise Missile Batteries
1 x Sansha Stasis Tower

Each cruise missile battery spawns a Centum Loyal Slaughterer/Execrator when destroyed.

Destroy Sansha’s Battletower (280,000 hp) Objective


Tips
  • No Energy Neutralizer Sentries means this mission is Active Tank friendly
  • Maximum damage dealing of all ships is 2204 hp/s (EM 64%, Therm 36%)
  • Mission is flagged complete when the Battletower is destroyed. There are no prisoners to return despite what the mission states.
