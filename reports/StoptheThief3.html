Bounty & loot

Faction: Mercenary
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal (no acceleration gate)
Damage dealt: All kinds, mostly Kin
Recommended damage dealing: Kin, Therm
Recommended ships: Huginn, Celestis, Retribution, Drake, Hurricane, Gila

Single Pocket


Single Group:

auto aggro
20-30km
1x Frigate (The Thief)
5x Cruisers (Mercenary Commander)

Blitz
Kill the thief and get the reports.

Isk
Bounty : 431,000 ISK
Loot : ~ 229,000 isk

Hint
Warp in 20km+ if you're a sniper.
