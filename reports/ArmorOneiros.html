Oneiros


Role
  • Armor logistic which can run cap stable and remain independent.
  • Tracking link bonus gives a significant boost to the fleet's applied damage.

Skills
  • Certificate : Core Capacitor Standard
  • Logistics 4
  • Remote Armor Repair Systems 5
  • Multitasking 3
  • Sensor Linking 4

Fitting Notes
  • Mandatory 4 large armor remote repair systems. DO NOT fit any medium armor remote repair systems.
  • Typical rigs : Capacitor Control Circuits / Remote Repair Augmentors / Ancillary Current Routers.
  • Standard 4 slot tank (3 resist slots and 1 plate slot) for basic fits.
  • Ship's EM-Thermal resistance is given more priority for better defence against more damaging laser hits.
  • NO explosive resist module installed (it's not strictly needed). The ship's natural signature radius will negate a major amount of Kin-Exp missile damage.
  • NO active armor hardeners installed to conserve capacitor.


Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • No afterburner (typical).
  • Maximum number of tracking links that can be fitted.
  • Full cap stability is not mandatory. Cap stable repair for minimum 3 large armor repair systems acceptable.

Assault/HQ
  • Afterburner fitted (improves survivability).
  • Full cap stability mandatory.
  • Sensor booster fitted for quick target locks.
