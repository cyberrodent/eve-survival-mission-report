Faction: Gurista Pirates
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kin, Therm


Single Pocket

All agroes on warp in.

Single Group:


3x Pithi Despoiler (frigate)
2x Pithi Saboteur (frigate)
1x Pithi Killer (cruiser)

Structures
Warehouse drops sealed cargo containers, 50x metal scraps, and 4 different trade goods.

Pressure Silo drop ammo.
The power source structure drops nothing.
