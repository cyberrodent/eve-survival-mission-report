Absolution


Role
  • Good close range dps with pulse lasers. Non-existent mid range dps.
  • No sniper role.

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Medium Pulse Laser Specialization 3
  • Medium Beam Laser Specialization 3
  • Controlled Bursts 5
  • Command Ship 4

Fitting Notes
  • Heavy Pulse fitted.
  • Ship's naturally high Kin-Exp resistance profile removes the need for any specific kinetic or explosive resist modules.
  • Ship's EM-Thermal resistance profile raised to better resist laser hits.
  • Typical rigs : Energy Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator / Anti-Thermic Pump.
  • Damage Control II + EANM combination avoids triple stacked resist penalty if an OGB is present.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • Stationary fleets do not require for afterburner to be fitted.
  • Mobile fleets require either an afterburner or MWD to be fitted.
  • Heavy Pulse Laser fitted (typical).

Assault/HQ
  • Afterburner fitted (typical).
  • Heavy Pulse Laser fitted (typical).
