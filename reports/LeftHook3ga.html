The Left Hook, Level 3


Last edited by Mjolnir69
Sat, 25 Jun 2016 18:39 UTC

Faction: Gallente Federation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space, one gate
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Cruisers deal Scourge Heavy Missile (Kin)
Frigates deal Inferno Light Missiles (Therm)
Extras: Sensor Dampening
Recommended damage dealing: kin/therm
Recommended ship classes: Cruiser, Battlecruiser, Stealth Bomber (e.g Manticore)

Single Pocket

Overview: Single pocket with 1 group and 2 spawns.

Group 1

4x Frigates (Elite Federation Arx) sensor dampening
2x Cruiser (Elite Federation Quadrieris)
Attacking the Gallente Tactical Support Center triggers spawn 1.

Spawn 1

4x Frigates (Federation Praktor Belos/Harpagos)
2x Battlecruiser (Federation Praktor Arx/Auxilia)
Damaging the armor of the Gallente Tactical Support Center triggers spawn 2

Spawn 2

4x Frigates (Federation Praktor Belos/Harpagos)
2x Battlecruiser (Federation Praktor Arx/Auxilia)

Mission is complete when Gallente Tactical Support Center is destroyed.

Blitz
Destroy the Gallente Tactical Support Center, warp out.

Tip
  • Attacking the Tactical Support Center triggers the second and third spawns, destroy each new spawn before re-targeting it.
  • This mission can also be run with a sniper fit Manticore. Then just orbit and shoot the Tactical Support Center at max range (e.g. 65km) and speed tank all NPCs.
