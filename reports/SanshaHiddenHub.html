Sansha Hidden Hub


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Sat, 05 Jul 2014 02:10 UTC

Faction: Sansha
Location: Nullsec
Damage Dealt: EM/Therm
Web/Scramble: None
Extras: Tracking Disruption Centii Loyal Savage/Slavehunter, Centum Beast/Execrator/Slaughterer, Centum Loyal Execrator/Slaughterer, Centum Beast Lord/Plague Lord
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM (best), Therm(secondary)

Single Pocket


On warp-in everything will agro. Trigger is final kill of specified rat type.

Initial Group

6x Frigate (Centii Butcher/Enslaver/Manslayer/Plague)
5x Destroyer (Centior Abomination/Horror/Monster
5-6x Cruiser (Centum Execrator/Fiend/Hellhound/Mutilator/Slaughterer/Torturer) Tracking Disruption Execrator/Slaughterer
6-8x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon/Phantasm/Specter)
3x Battleship (Centus Lord/Slave Lord) Trigger

Wave 1

3-4x Frigate (Centii Enslaver/Manslayer/Plague)
3-4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Enslaver/Plague)
2-3x Cruiser (Centum Fiend/Hellhound) Trigger

Wave 2

2x Frigate (Centii Butcher/Enslaver/Plague)
3x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Savage/Slavehunter) Tracking Disruption
2x Destroyer (Centior Horror/Monster)
2-3x Battleship (Centus Beast Lord/Plague Lord) Tracking Disruption Trigger

Wave 3

3-4x Frigate (Centii Enslaver/Manslayer/Plague)
3-4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Savage/Slavehunter) Tracking Disruption
1-2x Cruiser (Centum Fiend/Hellhound)
4x Battleship (Centus Slave Lord/Plague Lord) Tracking Disruption Plague Lord Trigger

Wave 4

3-4x Frigate (Centii Butcher/Enslaver/Manslayer/Plague)
3-4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Savage/Slavehunter) Tracking Disruption
1-2x Cruiser (Centum Fiend/Hellhound/Mutilator)
2x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Execrator/Slaughterer) Tracking Disruption

2x Frigate (Centii Enslaver/Plague)
2-3x Cruiser (Centum Beast/Juggernaut) Tracking Disruption
4x Battleship (Centus Beast Lord/Overlord/Savage Lord) Tracking Disruption Beast Lord Trigger

Wave 6

2-3x Frigate (Centii Butcher/Enslaver/Manslayer/Plague)
2x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Enslaver/Plague)
2x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Beast/Execrator/Sluaghterer) Tracking Disruption
