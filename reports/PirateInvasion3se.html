Pirate Invasion, Level 3 (Serpentis)


Last edited by ErikKalkoken:
Added details about war installation and gates
Sat, 27 Feb 2016 06:02 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Webifier & Scrambler: Coreli Guardians, Initiates & Scouts
Damage dealt: Kinetic, Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic, Thermal
Recommended ships: Drake, Hurricane, Myrmidon
Loyalty Points: 983 (depending on skills)
ISK: 403000 (depending on skills)
Bonus: 407000 (depending on skills
Difficulty: Very easy
Video: Cerberus

Pocket 1

Ships aggro when you attack them. You need to kill all ships in this pocket to unlock the acceleration gate.

Single Group (8-32km)

2x Frigates (Coreli Guardian Initiate/Scout) (Web/Scramb)
5x Frigates (Coreli Patroller/Watchman)

Pocket 2

Each group aggros when you attack them.
Acceleration gate sits at 65-70km. You need to kill all ships in this pocket to unlock it.
Fitting an afterburner is recommended.

Group 1 (40-50km)

6x Frigates (Coreli Guard/Safeguard)
2x Destoyers (Corelior Soldier/Trooper)
2x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender/Protector)

Group 2 (80-90km)

5-7x Frigates (Coreli Defender/Guard/Protector/Safeguard/Soldier)
1-3x Destroyers (Corelior Infantry/Soldier)
5x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender/Patroller/Protector/Watchman)
1x Battlecruiser (Corelatis Wing Leader)

Structures
1x War Installation: Ammo, 1-2 T1 modules
2x Static Caracal Navy Issue: No Loot
3x Stationary Badger II: No Loot
3x Partially Constructed Megathron: No Loot
6x Walkway Debris: No Loot
6x Ripped Superstructure: No Loot
8x Dark Shipyard: No Loot
12x Starbase Major Assembly Array: Minerals & Alloys (each worth ~10k)
15x Spaceshuttle Wreck: No Loot

The War Installation takes quiet a while to destroy because of the large amount of hit points
(One reported destruction of the War Installation required over 1000 Heavy missiles)

Pocket 3

Full pocket aggro upon attacking Group 3.

Group 1 (30-40km Auto Aggro)

2x Frigates (Coreli Guardian Initiate/Scout) (Web/Scramb)
4x Frigates (Coreli Guard/Safeguard)
3x Destoyers (Corelior Infantry/Soldier/Trooper)

Group 2 (25-35km)

9x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender/Guard/Protector/Safeguard/Scout/Spy)

Group 3 (50-60km)

5x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Guard/Safeguard)
3x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Platoon Leader/Squad Leader/Wing Leader)

Blitz
Kill all in pocket 1 & 2. Group 3 in the last pocket. That should flag the mission complete.
You do not have to kill the War Installation to finish the mission

Structures
Structures are invunerable.

Mining
Veldspar - 36 Asteroids
