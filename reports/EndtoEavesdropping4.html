Faction: Gallente Federation
Mission type: Encounter
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/Therm

Single Pocket

3x Frigate (Federation Hastile)
3x Frigate (Federation Clavis)
2x Frigate (Federation Kontos)
2x Frigate (Federation Hoplon)
1x Frigate (Federation Manica)
1x Frigate (Federation Libertus)

Tip

  • Use a large kinetic smartbomb to dispose very quickly.
  • Use drones to dispose the rest that are too far away for the smartbomb

Standings

2.4% Gallente standing loss (due to Ship Kill).
