Kronos


Role
  • Excellent close range range dps with neutron blasters. Non-existent mid range dps.
  • Excellent sniper.
  • Utility high slots enable emergency remote repairs and energy transfer cap chain.

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Certificate : High-Velocity Helmsman Improved
  • Large Blaster Specialization 3
  • Large Railgun Specialization 3
  • Controlled Bursts 5
  • Marauder 4
  • Energy Emission Systems 4
  • Remote Armor Repair Systems 4
  • Multitasking 3

Fitting Notes
  • Ability to swap between close range and sniper configurations without changing (or wasting) rigs is heavily emphasized.
  • Neutron Blaster / 425mm Railgun fitted.
  • Typical rigs : Hybrid Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator / Anti-Thermic Pump / Anti-Explosive Pump.
  • Typical utility high slots : Large Energy Transfer Array / Large Remote Armor Repair System.
  • Ship's Kin-Exp resistance is given more priority for better defence against more damaging missile hits.
  • Damage Control II + EANM combination avoids triple stacked resist penalty if an OGB bonus is applied.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • MWD fitted (typical).
  • Neutron Blaster fitted (typical).
  • Minimum 2 Large Remote Armor Repair Systems for quick emergency armor repairs.

Assault/HQ
  • No MWD (typical).
  • 425mm Railgun fitted (typical).
  • Minimum 2 Large Energy Transfer Arrays for ad-hoc cap chain. Increased resistance vs NPC cap neuting.
  • Use faction Iridium charge for targets at 120-140km. Better applied damage.
