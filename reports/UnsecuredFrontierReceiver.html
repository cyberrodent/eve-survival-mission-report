Unsecured Frontier Receiver

Last edited by MournfulC
Wed, 18 Feb 2015 08:09 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Radar
Space type: Normal
Damage dealt: (EM/The 23%, Kin/Exp 27%)
Recommended ship classes: Battlecruiser, Battleship, Marauder
Recommended generic setup: Heavy Omni-tank
Blue Loot Payout: 49 Million ISK

All sleepers will auto aggress; Sleepers will switch targets, drones and support ships are not immune.

Single Pocket


Initial Group

5x Frigates (Emergent Defender) [web]
3x Cruisers (Awakened Preserver) [rr] TRIGGER
Max Incoming DPS: 348 (EM/The 35%, Kin/Exp 15%)

Wave 1

3x Cruisers (Awakened Defender)
1x Cruiser (Awakened Preserver) [rr] TRIGGER
3x Cruisers (Awakened Upholder) [web, neut]
Max Incoming DPS: 370 (EM/The 31%, Kin/Exp 19%)

Wave 2

4x Frigates (Emergent Upholder) [rr, neut]
1x Battleship (Sleepless Defender) [web] TRIGGER (on first shot landing)
Max Incoming DPS: 490 (EM/The 28%, Kin/Exp 22%)

Wave 3

4x Frigates (Emergent Defender) [web]
2x Battleships (Sleepless Preserver) [rr]
Max Incoming DPS: 608 (EM/The 12%, Kin/Exp 38%)

Structures
7x Deformed Sleeper Databanks (spawn container, codebreaker)
Small chance of a Deserted Talocan Cruiser (spawn container, salvager)
