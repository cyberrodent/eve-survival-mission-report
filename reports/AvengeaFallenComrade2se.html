Avenge a Fallen Comrade, Level 2


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 05:25 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Extras: None
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/therm
Recommended Ships: Caracal

Warp into an Acceleration Gate

Single Pocket


Four Groups, group 1 aggros immediately. Remaining groups will not aggro until you attack them, get within approx 38 km, or attack the Habitat. Kill the other groups in order from left to right.

Group 1:
2x Frigates - 2km from warp in; instant agro

Group 2:
5x Frigates - 40km. May contain a destroyer.

Group 3:
1x Cruiser
4x Frigates - near habitat

Group 4:
7x Frigates

Blitz:
Kill Habitat and leave.

None of the structures drops anything.

Asteroids
None
