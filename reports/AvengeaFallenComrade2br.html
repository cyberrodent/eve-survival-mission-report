Avenge a Fallen Comrade, Level 2


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 05:25 UTC

Faction: Blood Raiders
Mission type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM/Therm
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ship: Heavy frigate, cruiser
Completed in: Pulse-Punisher MacLypse

Single Pocket

4 groups. All groups aggro. Groups appear after the last one is destryed at 30-40 km

Group 1: (10 km)

3x Frigates (Corpii Diviner/Raider)

Group 2:

3x Destroyers (Corpior Visionary)
1x Cruiser (Corpum Arch Templar) (appears at 50 km after the destroyers get close to you)

Group 3:

6x Frigates (Corpii Follower)

Group 4:

5x Frigates (Corpii Diviner/Engraver)

Blitz:
Just destroy the Habitat and warp back to your agent.
