Core Stronghold, Class 5

Last edited by Markj2
Sat, 06 Feb 2016 12:19 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Cosmic Anomaly
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Omni
Webbers: Emergent Sentinel, Emergent Warden, Awakened Sentinel, Sleepless Guardian
Scramblers: Emergent Sentinel, Emergent Warden, Sleepless Guardian
Recommended ship classes: Capital, Battleship, T3 (7+ Remote Repair Modules), Marauders (with Bastion)
Recommended generic setup: Multi-range, Heavy Omni-tank, Remote Repair (Spider Tanking)
Blue loot: 234.9 mill ISK / 819.9 mill ISK (Cap + Site)

All sleepers will auto aggress; Sleepers will switch targets, drones and support ships are not immune.

Single Pocket


Capital escalation waves spawn between 60 and 65km directly above the initial warp to 0, show sensor overlay to see the initial warp to 0 location.

Capital Escalation Wave 1

4164 DPS, Max Nos -216
6x Battleship (Sleepless Guardian) [scram, web, neut]

Capital Escalation Wave 2

5552 DPS, Max Nos -288
8x Battleship (Sleepless Guardian) [scram, web, neut]


Initial Group

680 DPS
6x Sentry Guns (Orthrus) (up to 115km from warp in)
4x Frigates (Emergent Sentinel) [scram, web] TRIGGER

Reinforcement Wave 1

1866 DPS, Max Neut -24, RR Strength 146 w/o resists
2x Battleships (Sleepless Keeper) [nos]
2x Battleships (Sleepless Warden) [rr] TRIGGER

Reinforcement Wave 2

747 DPS, Max Neut -18
6x Frigates (Emergent Warden) [scram, web, nos]
7x Cruisers (Awakened Sentinel) [web] TRIGGER

Reinforcement Wave 3

2651 DPS, Max Neut -24, RR Strength 451 w/o resists
4x Cruisers (Awakened Warden) [rr] (will orbit at 45km)
2x Battleships (Sleepless Keeper) [nos] (stable orbit at 65km)
3x Battleships (Sleepless Warden) [rr] (will orbit at 70km)
