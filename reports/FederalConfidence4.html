Federal Confidence, Level 4


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sun, 29 Jul 2012 16:27 UTC

Faction: Caldari
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with a gate
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Extras: Nos/neut from the Public Confidence Operative, some jamming
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Kin
Recommended ships: Ishtar, Vargur

Single Pocket


Three initial groups, aggro from groups 1 and 2. Group 3 will aggro when the Public Confidence Operative or the missile batteries are attacked. Destroying the last ship in Group 3 triggers Spawn 2. Last ship in Spawn 2 drops a cargo container with the objective item.

Initial Spawn


Group 1 (Aggro)

Distance: 30km
4x Cruisers (Taibu State Bajo/Samurai)
1x Battleship (Public Confidence Operative)

Group 2 (Aggro)

Distance: 40km
2x Caldari Cruise Missile Battery
2x Tower Sentry Caldari III

Group 3

Distance: 55km
6x Battleships (State Zen/Oshiro/Shukuro Tenno)

Spawn 2


Group 1 (Aggro)

Distance: Near Group 3's initial position
4x Battleships (State Shukuro Bishamon/Shukuro Shogun)
