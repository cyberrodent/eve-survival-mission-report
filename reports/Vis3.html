Vis3


This pilot is on a mission to see what can be completed solo in a Frigate, Originally using uses two fittings on a Rifter, one for normal speed tanking up close at 800m and the other to deal with webbing ships that need to be kept at range (20km), Vis3 has around 50 million skill points in training, newer characters may have trouble duplicating his results, as of Feb 2011 he exclusively uses a Dramiel to handle both short and long range situations.
