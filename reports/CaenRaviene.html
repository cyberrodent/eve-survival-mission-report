Part 2 of 2


Faction: Angels
Mission type: Encounter (Faction standings mission)
Space type: Deadspace with two rooms (mwd does not work)
Damage dealt: Kin/therm/em/exp
Extras: Nos/damp/jam/tracking disruption ?
Web: Arch Gisti Raider
Recommended damage dealing: kin/therm/em/exp ?
Recommended ship classes: Battleship
Recommended generic setup: close combat, heavy tank


Pocket 1


Upon gate into this room you draw agro from group 1 and 2, other groups agro upon proximity, warp gate to second room is 55km from gate in point.

Group 1: 4x Frigate (Gisti Trasher) 20km

Group 2: 3x Battleship (Gist Gen) 30km

Group 3: 4x Cruiser (Arch Gisti Raider) 40 km

Group 4: 3x Battlecruiser (Gistatus Legatus) 50km

Group 5: 2x Battleship (Gist Seraphim) 90km

Mineable Asteroid Info
41 Velspar, did not mine so value unknown, the belt is spread 34km to 115km from the entry point.

Tip
I accidentially pulled agro from groups 1 -4 with my drones, kited them with no problem in a Dominix.

Pocket 2

10 seconds after gate-in the entire room gives agro

Group 1:
4x Interceptor (Arch Gisti Impaler) 55-65km
4x Battleship (Gist Nephilim)

Group 2:
4x Interceptor (Gist Outlaw) 65-75km
4x Battlecruiser (Gist Saint)
3x Battleship (Gist Seraphim)
1x Battleship (Overlord Sgnard)

Group 3:
4x Destroyer (Gisti Hunter) 85-95km
4x Battlecruiser (Gisti Centurian)


Tip
I have no advice beyond this, do not attempt solo. This mission requires multiple ships in a coordinated effort, lost a Dominix and 5.76% faction standing after cancelling mission.
