Mission type: Encounter -Storyline

Faction that may give this mission: Angel Cartel， Thukker Tribe, Serpentis

Part 1 of 5 - Unwanted Settlers

Faction:Minmatar/Mercenary
Space type: Deadspace
Recommended damage dealing: therm
Recommended ship classes: Ishtar, Dominix

Single Pocket

Warp in message:
As you approach the Minmatar colony you notice a gathering of mercenary ships by the entrance beacon. Obviously hired by the colonists, but where did they get that kind of money? Without even a simple hailing, the mercenaries attack.

All groups auto-aggro.
Group 1: less than 10km
2x Battleships (Mercenary Overlord)
3x Cruisers (Mercenary Lieutenant)

Group 2: 40km
2x Frigates (Militia Protector)
3x Cruisers (Militia guardian)

Asteroid Colony is around 50 km from warp-in and needs to be destroyed to retrieve the mission item.
