Last edited by GaladTirin

Faction: Mercenaries / Rogue Drones
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace
Damage Received: All Types
Web/Scramble: Mercenary Wingman, Strain.. drones
Recommended Damage Dealing: Thermal (EM vs. Drones)
Recommended Ships: Huginn, Drake, Hurricane

No hostiles at the initial gate.

Pocket 1


Group 1

auto aggro
20-22km
1x Striker Alvatis

Group 2

60-65km
1-2x Frigate (Barracuda/Devilfish/Raider/Sunder Alvi)
0-1x Elite Frigate (Strain Infestor Alvi) web/scramble
2x Cruisers (Disintegrator Alvum/Viral Infector Alvum)
May substitute for a cruiser
1x Battlecruiser (Enforcer Alvatis)

Structures
Structures have no loot

Pocket 2


Group 1

auto aggro
15-20km
1x Elite Frigate (Mercenary Wingman) web/scramble
1x Frigate (Mercenary Elite Fighter)
2x Cruiser (Mercenary Commander)

Group 2:

60-70km
1x Elite Frigate (Mercenary Wingman) web/scramble
3x Cruiser (Mercenary Commander)

Pocket 3


Single Group:

2x Drone Sentry I (30-40km)
2x Drone Sentry II (30-50km)
1x Frigate (Terak Nomuri) (38km)

Killing Terak Nomuri completes the objective

Pleasure hub has decent chance of dropping science skillbook(s) and materials
