Completed several times with two self-rep Tengu (DG Large Shield Booster 702 DPS tank), one with HML and one with HAM. Sentry guns don't aggro on warp-in, so long as you don't stray too far from the warp-in point. We kill 5 of the frigs before approaching the sentries and killing them. Then we kill the trigger.

Webbing from BS in the second wave can be a problematic if you're relying on speed tanking.

Remote rep in final wave can be tedious. We start with Defenders, since they burn out towards us pretty quickly, out of RR range of the rest of the ships. Then we deal with the Awakened Preservers, since they die faster and it's easier to alpha through the RR from the BS. Finally, we kill the BS. If we try to kill the BS first, the combined rep of 2x Cruisers and 2x BS is impossible to overcome.
