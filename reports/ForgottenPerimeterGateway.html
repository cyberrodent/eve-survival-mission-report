Forgotten Perimeter Gateway

Last edited by XeCara
Mon, 29 Apr 2013 12:23 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Magnetometric
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Omni
Webbers:
Scramblers: None
Recommended ship classes: Soloable in well tanked Drake (~500 dps tanked)
Recommended generic setup: Omni tank

Unlike most sleeper magnetometric sites, there are only 3 waves, each which auto aggress.
Sleeper ships re-target every few minutes. Drones and support ships are not immune.

Single Pocket


Initial Group

4x Cruisers (Awakened Patroller) orbit 15 km
1x Cruiser (Awakened Watchman) [nos] TRIGGER orbit 30 km

Reinforcement Wave 1

1x Battleship (Sleepless Patroller) TRIGGER
2x Frigates (Emergent Watchman) [nos]

Reinforcement Wave 2

1x Battleship (Sleepless Escort)
1x Battleship (Sleepless Watchman)
4x Frigates (Emergent Patroller)

Tip
  • 8 containers to be analyzed using the Analyzer archaeology module
