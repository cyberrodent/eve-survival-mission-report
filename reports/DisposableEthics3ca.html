Disposable Ethics, Level 3


Last edited by JoannaDoe
Tue, 01 Dec 2015 05:57 UTC


Faction: Caldari
Faction that may give this mission: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space with gates
Damage dealt: Kin/therm/
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: kin/therm
Recommended ship classes: Destroyer, Cruiser

Single Pocket


Group 1

30-40km from warp-in
1x Battlecruiser (Caldari Buyer)
1x Cruiser (Angel Contact)

When you approach or attack the Caldari Buyer it will blow up the Angel Contact and summon Group 2. The Caldari Buyer drops the mission objective.

Group 2

40 km from Group 1
2x Cruisers (Caldari Ambusher)
Last ship triggers Group 3

Group 3

40 km from Group 1
3x Cruisers (Caldari Ambusher)
Last ship triggers Group 4

Group 4

40 km from Group 1
2x Cruisers (Caldari Ambusher)

Blitz
Kill Caldari Buyer, Loot the mission item and warp out.

Standing Loss
Around -0.018% to Caldari State
