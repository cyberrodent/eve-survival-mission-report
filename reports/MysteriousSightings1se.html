Mysterious Sightings, Level 1


Last edited by ShintuFelper:
Updated info
Wed, 04 Feb 2015 17:10 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter/courier
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: Kin / Therm
Extras: none
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Therm / Kin
Recommended ships classes: Any

Step 1 of 4


Pocket 1


Distance: 25km
2x Frigates (Federation Kopis)
1x Frigate (Unidentified Spaceship)

Both federation frigates are destroyed within about 5 sec of warp in and Unidentified space ship warps out.
Mission is complete.

Step 2 of 4


Courier


Deliver 1 unit reports.


Step 3 of 4


Complex is composed of 4 rooms in a square. The diagonal gates cross to the opposite corner while the other two cross along the side they are on. You start in the lower right corner of the square.

You will find the Unidentified Spaceship in one of the four rooms randomly sitting 35km off the Beacon you land on.

Target the Unidentified Spaceship and mission completes as he warps away. If you do not target him before he warps away he will be in one of the other 3 rooms.

Step 4 of 4


Pocket 1


8x Frigates (Coreli Agent/Initiate/Scout/Spy)
1x Industrial (Republic Industrial)
Industrial is destroyed upon engaging the frigates.
Mission completes when all enemies are killed.
