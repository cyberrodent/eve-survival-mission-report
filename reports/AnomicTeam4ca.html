Anomic Team: Hawk Burner, Level 4


Last edited by CirimCollective
Fri, 20 Feb 2015 17:37 UTC

Faction: Caldari
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM
Web/scramble: Burner Hawk
Extras: You can not enter mission site in anything larger than a Frigate hull.
Recommended damage dealing: EM
Recommended ship types: Kestrel, kiting long range frigate (25km+)
Video: Kestrel (mixed T1/T2)


Pocket 1


All ships aggro on warp-in.

Hostile Ships (40-45 km)

2x Frigates (Burner Bantam) (Remote Shield Repair)
1x Elite Frigate (Burner Hawk)


Kiting Tips
  • set the default orbit distance before you enter the mission site;
  • set the orbit to whatever the range of your guns/launchers can support, but do not set it inside Burner Hawk's webbing range;
  • Burners can and usually do have "faction" webs, so watch out when choosing your orbit range;
  • Activate your Microwarp Drive and start orbiting the Burner Hawk as soon as you land. Otherwise, you'll end up being webbed and scrambled because Burner Hawk is pretty fast;
  • Destroy Burner Bantams first as they remote repair each other and the Burner Hawk;
  • Burner Hawk uses Defender Missiles. If you notice that you are not hitting it with your own missiles, try turning off your launchers and turning them on again. This may place your firing cycle between the firing cycles of Burner Hawk's Defender missiles;


Loot
CCP said that there is a slight chance of getting faction stuff in the loot. If you don't get faction modules, the loot is mostly made of Meta 4 modules. Burner Hawk is known to drop meta Ballistic Control Systems (Muon Coil, Cross-linked), among other loot.

Bounty
5,000,000 ISK for the Burner Hawk
