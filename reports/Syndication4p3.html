Epic Arc Overview

Agent Name: Ascain Adeset
Agent Corporation: Transstellar Shipping
Agent Location: Mesybier System at Mesybier X - Transstellar Shipping Storage (Station)
Additional Requirements: None

Important Note:
  • Your standing is only checked by the starter agent. After starting the epic arc, subsequent agents will not check standings.
  • This means while running the epic arc, you can continue doing missions even if your standing drops below +5.00 yet remains above -2.00 Faction standing.

Chapter 3 : Inertia

Note
  • This is the final chapter of Syndication - Gallente Level 4 Epic Arc.

Go to Chapter 1: Impetus
Go to Chapter 2: Eagle Grip (High Road)
Go to Chapter 2: Scoping The Scene (Low Road)

Mission 13 - Places to Hide

Mission Type: Epic Arc Pathway Choice

Mission Objective:
  • Choose one of three different options to continue the Epic Arc:
  • Little Fingers (Mission 14A) - This pathway option occurs within the current high-sec location.
  • Oldest Profession (Mission 14B) - This pathway option requires travel through low-sec to a different high-sec location.
  • Octomet Plantation (Mission 14C) - This pathway option requires travel to low-sec mission location.

Note
  • Making a selection completes the mission.


Mission 14A - Little Fingers

Note
  • This pathway option occurs within the current high-sec location.
Faction: Independent (Gallente Ships)
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Damage Dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/Scram: Rattler Row Elite Frigate
Extras: N/A
Recommended Damage Dealing: Thermal, Kinetic

Mission Objective:
  • Explore the Rattler Row. Search for any signs of Ralie's presence. Defeat any guards you find.

Warp to Site

Note
  • Last ship destroyed will trigger the next wave group.

Initial Group (15km, auto aggro)
5x 'Rattler Row Battleships' (Hyperion, Dominix)

Wave 1 (20km, auto aggro)
3x 'Rattler Row Elite Battleships' (Megathron, Hyperion)
4x 'Rattler Row Elite Frigate' (Imicus, Incursus) Web/Scram

Wave 2 (20km, auto aggro)
6x 'Rattler Row Rookie Battleships' (Megathron, Hyperion, Dominix)

Wave 3 (20km, auto aggro)
4x 'Rattler Row Elite Cruiser' (Thorax, Vexor, Exequor) Neut/Sensor Damp

Tip
  • Destroy structures and search loot drops for 'Carry On Token' which can be used to blitz part of mission 15.
  • Most propably on the middle fuel depot. Token spawns in a container.
  • Destroying the fuel depot incurs -3.0% faction standing for The Syndicate

Mission 15 - Carry On

Faction: Independent (Gallente/Serpentis)
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace (Gated)
Damage Dealt: All Types
Web/Scram: Veteran 'Carry On Guard' Frigate
Extras: Neut/Sensor Damp (Veteran 'Carry On Guard' Cruiser)
Recommended Damage Dealing: Thermal, Kinetic

Mission Objective:
  • Fly to the Carry On. Find the Carry On Hub and approach it.


Warp to site

1x Acceleration Gate - The Hub (13km) = Locked
1x Acceleration Gate - The Alley (15km)

Note
  • Mission location actually consists of three pockets, however the first two pockets can be bypassed:
  • Gate to 'The Alley' leads to Pockets 1 and 2.
  • Gate to 'The Hub' (Locked) leads directly to Pocket 3.

Tip
  • 'The Hub' gate (Locked) has a 'Toll Booth' next to it.
  • 'The Hub' gate (Locked) requires a 'Carry On Token' passkey.

WARNING
  • The 'Carry On Token' passkey will be consumed on use. Create a bookmark in 'The Hub' (Pocket 3) after using the gate and if needing to warp out, can just warp back to bookmark instead of slugging it out through 'The Alley' (Pockets 1 and 2).

Blitz
  • Warp to site with 'Carry On Token' passkey in cargohold and activate 'The Hub' gate (has 'Toll Booth' next to it). After entering 'The Hub' location (pocket 3), ignore all ships and quickly approach the 'Carry On Hub' structure to complete the mission.

The Alley - Pocket 1

1 Group (50km, auto aggro)
8x Amateur/Veteran 'Carry On Guard' Battleship (Independent Hyperion, Dominix, Megathron)
3x Veteran 'Carry On Guard' Cruiser (Independent Thorax, Vexor, Celestis) Neut/Sensor Damp (25-30km range)
6x 'Syndicate' Cruise Missile Battery (Gallente 'Inferno' Cruise Missile Battery) Thermal DPS

1x Acceleration Gate to 'The Alley' - Pocket 2 (36km).

The Alley - Pocket 2

1 Group (20km, auto aggro)
6x 'Carry On Guard' Battleship (Independent Hyperion, Dominix, Megathron)
8x 'Carry On Guard' Cruiser (Independent Thorax, Vexor, Celestis, Exequror)
2x 'Syndicate' Cruise Missile Battery (Gallente 'Inferno' Cruise Missile Battery) Thermal DPS
2x 'Syndicate' Heavy Missile Battery (Gallente 'Scourge' Heavy Missile Battery) Kinetic DPS

1x Acceleration Gate to 'The Hub' - Pocket 3 (22km).

Note
  • Various destroyable structures scattered within this pocket might drop loot (Market Trade Items).

The Hub - Pocket 3

1 Group (50km)
8x Amateur/Veteran 'Carry On Guard' Battleship (Independent Hyperion, Dominix, Megathron)
2x Veteran 'Carry On Guard' Cruiser (Independent Thorax, Vexor, Celestis, Exequror) Neut/Sensor Damp (25-30km range)
4x Veteran 'Carry On Guard' Frigate (Independent Tristan, Maulus, Atron) Web/Warp Scram
3x Transport ('Carry On Freighter')
2x Industrial ('Carry On Courier', 'Carry On Industrial')

1x 'Carry On Hub' Structure (54km)

Note
  • No loot drops from various destroyable structures scattered within this pocket.

Tips
  • No need to destroy ships to complete the mission.
  • Approach to within 10km of 'Carry On Hub' structure flags the mission completed.
  • All ships except the 3x Freighter, 1x Courier and 1x Industrial ship will despawn.


Mission 16 - Studio 1

Faction: Independent (Gallente/Minmatar)
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace (Gated)
Damage Dealt: Kinetic, Thermal
Web/Scram: N/A
Extras: Neut/Sensor Damp ('Syndicate Elite Cruiser')
Recommended Damage Dealing: Thermal, Explosive

Mission Objective:
  • The kid is in 'Studio 1'. Time to get him back and destroy the studio.

Warning
  • Expect very high alpha and high DPS (2k+) in this mission. Be prepared to warp-out if needed.
  • Maintain range from Elite Cruisers to avoid EWAR (Neut = 25km range / Sensor Damp = 30km range).

Pocket 1

2 Groups - both auto aggro

Group 1 (67km)
2x 'Syndicate Elite Battleship' (Independent Veteran Hyperion, Dominix, Megathron)
3x 'Syndicate Elite Cruiser' (Independent Veteran Thorax, Vexor, Celestis) Neut/Sensor Damp (25-30km range)
2x 'Syndicate Elite Frigates' (Independent Veteran Tristan, Atron, Maulus, Imicus)

Group 2 (83km)
3x 'Syndicate Elite Battleship' (Independent Veteran Hyperion, Dominix, Megathron)
2x 'Syndicate Elite Cruiser' (Independent Veteran Thorax, Vexor, Celestis) Neut/Sensor Damp (25-30km range)
2x 'Syndicate Elite Frigates' (Independent Veteran Tristan, Atron, Maulus, Imicus)

1x Acceleration Gate (37km) to Pocket 2.

Note
  • All ships must be destroyed to unlock gate.
  • No loot drops from various destroyable structures scattered within 35km range.

Pocket 2

Note
  • Various groups of ships warp out leaving 1 group to defend 'Studio 1' structure.

Initial Group (68-94km, auto aggro)
10x 'Syndicate Battleship' (Independent Hyperion, Dominix, Megathron)
2x 'Syndicate Elite Battleship' (Independent Veteran Hyperion, Dominix, Megathron)
3x 'Syndicate Elite Cruiser' (Independent Veteran Thorax, Vexor, Celestis) Neut/Sensor Damp (25-30km range)

1x 'Studio 1' Structure (38km) Trigger Wave 1 at structure damage.

Wave 1 (60km, auto aggro)
6x 'Syndicate Elite Battleship' (Independent Veteran Hyperion, Dominix, Megathron)
6x 'Pator 6 Elite Frigate' (Independent Veteran Rifter, Slasher, Probe, Vigil)

Note
  • Destroying 'Studio 1' structure triggers mission completion.
  • 'Studio 1' structure may drop loot (Market Trade Items).

Blitz:
  • Kill all rats in pocket 1.
  • Kill the cruisers in pocket 2. Destroy Studio I (Tank everyone else).


Mission 17 - Showtime

Faction: Pator 6 (Independent Gallente & Minmatar)
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Damage Dealt: All Types
Web/Scram: Elite Frigate
Extras: Neut/Tracking Disruption (Elite Cruiser)
Recommended Damage Dealing: Explosive, kinetic.

Mission Objective:
  • Follow the Pator 6, retrieve 1x Ralie Ardanne (1.0 m3) and return to agent.

Warning
  • Expect high DPS with heavy Explosive Damage Type in this mission.
  • Recommend Explosive Tank for the initial spawn with long distance snipe.
  • Most incoming damage at snipe range will be Explosive DPS from Nova Cruise Missiles.
  • Be prepared to warp-out if needed.

Warp to Site

Tip
  • Destroy all Frigates asap but concentrate attack on Cruisers first before attacking Battleships.
  • Maintain range from Elite Cruisers to avoid EWAR (Neut = 25km range / Tracking Disruption = 35km range)

1 Group (60km, auto aggro)
5x 'Pator 6 Elite Battleship' (Independent Veteran Tempest, Maelstrom, Typhoon) Trigger Battleship Wave (last destroyed)
3x 'Pator 6 Elite Cruiser' (Independent Veteran Stabber, Bellicose, Scythe) Neut/Tracking Disruption Trigger Cruiser Wave (last destroyed)
5x 'Syndicate Elite Frigate' (Independent Veteran Imicus, Maulus, Tristan, Atron) Web/Scram

Cruiser Wave (60km, auto aggro)
5x 'Syndicate Cruiser' (Independent Thorax, Vexor, Celestis, Exequror)

Battleship Wave (60km, auto aggro)
1x Rosulf Fririk - Minmatar Battleship (Sleeper AI, will switch targets and attack drones)
6x 'Syndicate Battleship' (Independent Hyperion, Dominix, Megathron)

Note
  • Mission is flagged complete when Rosulf Fririk is destroyed.
  • 1x Ralie Ardanne (1.0 m3) spawns in container next to Rosulf Fririk wreck.
  • Collect mission objective (Ralie Ardanne) as soon as it spawns. Item is cruical for mission completion.


Mission 18 - Where's the Line?

Mission type: Epic Arc Pathway Choice

Mission Objective:
  • Choose one of two different pathways to complete the Gallente Level 4 Epic Arc:
  • Everybody Has a Price (Mission 19A) - 1x Syndicate Cloaking Device & 'The Syndicate' Faction Standing increase = +10% Base (+12.5% with Social 5)
  • Safe Return (Mission 19B) - 1x Black Eagle Drone Link Augmentor & Gallente Faction Standing increase = +10% Base (+12.5% with Social 5)
