Part 7 - The Secret Meeting


Faction: Gurista Pirates
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/therm

Pocket 1


1x Battleship (Komni Envoy)
Does not trigger anything. Might drop an implant. Heavy EM damage (Paradise Cruise Missile).

Pocket 2


5 Groups, all about 40-50k away:

Group 1

3x Frigate (Pithi Wrecker/Plunderer)
2x Cruiser (Pithum Inferno)

Group 2

4x Frigate (Pithi Destructor/Demolisher)
2x Cruiser (Pithum Eraser)

Group 3

2x Battlecruiser (Pithatis Assassin/Death Dealer)

Group 4

2x Battlecruiser (Pithatis Assaulter)

Group 5

4x Cruiser (Pithum Silencer/Ascriber)

You will get aggro from Groups 1 and 2 if u come within 20km otherwise the other groups dont aggro up to 10km (as far as i tested, i targeted and engaged at 10km)
Attacking one group will not aggro any others.

Part 10 - The Final Battle


Faction: Gurista Pirates
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Recommended damage: Kin/therm
Recommended ships: Raven, Cerberus

Pocket 1

50KM out Aggro
3x Pithior Renegades
2x Guristas Sentry tower III
3x Pithior Anarchists
1x Pithatis Assassin
1x Pithatis Death Dealer

Pocket 2


Group 1 (Aggro) 7km

1x Cruiser
2x Frigates (web)
1x Elite frigate (web)

Group 2

3x Pithum Infernos

Group 3

2x Cruisers
4x Frigs

Group 4

2x Cruisers
4x Destroyers

Group 5

1x Battlecruiser
5x Destroyers

The Groups will not aggro unless provoked.

Pocket 3


Group 1 (Aggro) 10km

3x Pithi Plunderers
2x Wreckers
1x Plithum Eraser
1x Plithum Abolisher

Remaining groups will not aggro until attacked, unless you attack the control tower -- then they ALL aggro immediately

Group 2

1x Pithatis Enforcer @
2x Pithatis Assaulters near the tower.

They do aggro at about 13km

Group 3 (Left side)

2x Guristas Kyoukens
2x Guristas Webifiers
5x Pithatis Enforcers
3x Pith Destroyers
1x Pithatis Assaulter
1x Pith Conquistador

Group 4 (Right Side)

4x Pith Usurpers
3x Pithior Nihilists
2x Pithior Anarchists
4x Pithatis Death Dealers
1x Pithatis Revoluter

Tip
After you warp out against group 3 or 4 in room three, the remaining ships will return to their start position and wait. Re-entering the room will not cause them to aggro again unless attacked.

Blitz
Destroy the Control Tower.
(Tag the can, finish the mission, and then warp back to the can to pick up an implant.)
