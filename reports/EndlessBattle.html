Endless Battle, Level 1 (Tutorial)


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 08:18 UTC


Detection and elimination of pirates in our solar system is a constant battle. Can you help us overcome this fast growing plague?

Objective:
Warp to the mission location found in your journal and eliminate the saboteurs. Once they are killed, return to your agent.

Faction: Mercenaries
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM/Therm
Reccomended damage dealing: EM/Exp

Single Pocket

Hostile drops 1x Stolen Documents which are needed to take back to your agent.
This mission leads to Part 2: Encryption Code Book (Courier)

1x Saboteur Mercenary (Bounty: 1500 ISK)
