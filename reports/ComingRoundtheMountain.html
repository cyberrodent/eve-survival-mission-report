Coming 'Round the mountain, Level 3


Last edited by GouvViki:
Spacetype, Objective
Thu, 03 Sep 2009 05:26 UTC

Mission type: Mining
Space type: Deadspace
Faction: Same as belt rats.
Recommended ships: Hulk

Mine 20.000 Units of Augumene (6.000 m³).
There are 10 Augumene Asteroids in the Pocket.
No need for scanner since the encounter point puts you in the deadspace containing the ore. Mining all the ore in the deadspace provides exactly what you need to complete the mission.

Comments [Hide comments/form]
Arrival: No Combat (Standard Belt Rat Spawn always possible)
During: 15km from farthest 'roid
End: Mission Complete

Also: Scanner still helpful for picking them out of space, as well as knowing how long to hold your cycle.
