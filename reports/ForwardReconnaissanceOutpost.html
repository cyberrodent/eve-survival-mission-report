Forward Reconnaissance Outpost

Last edited by XeCara
Sat, 18 Jun 2011 10:57 UTC

Faction: Incursion Sansha's Nation
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Omni
Recommended ship classes: (easily) solo'd in a T2 armour tanked cane
Recommended generic setup: either heavy tank, or pure speed
Forces Required: 1-5 pilots

Incursion Sansha will auto aggress and switch targets (including drones).

Military Intelligence
Your primary objective is to destroy any Sansha vessels defending the transmitter arrays. Once the area is secured, surveillance teams can move in to backhack the Sansha information network.

This is a target of opportunity, and has minimal impact on the larger anti-Sansha campaign.

Rewards
- 50,000 ISK x ratio
- 50 CONCORD LP x ratio

Background Intelligence
Sansha’s Nation forces have established forward outposts for the purpose of reconnaissance and minor communication relays between fleets. Although these outposts play a relatively minor role they are nonetheless contributing to the Nation’s larger influence over systems and must be considered equally troubling in this regard.

Synopsis from ISHAEKA-0091. DED Special Operations.
Authorized for Capsuleer dissemination.
