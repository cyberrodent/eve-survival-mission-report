Part 1 of 3


Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Gated MWD: Yes
Damage dealt: exp/kin
Extras: timed spawns
Web/scramble:
Recommended damage dealing: exp/kin
Recommended ships: battlecruiser class if fighting (Drake, etc.)

Pocket 1

The gates are not locked, so you can blitz this mission in a shuttle without a single kill. Distance to gate ~ 25km

4 waves of enemies will spawn(30 seconds to 1 minute apart each) composed of 1 destroyer and 2 cruisers/elite cruisers OR 3 cruisers/elite cruisers.

Wave Format

0-1x Destroyer (Gistior Haunter/Seizer)
0-3x Cruiser (Gistum Centurion/Phalanx)
0-3x Elite Cruiser (Arch Gistum Breaker/Centurion/Defeater/Liquidator/Marauder/Phalanx)

Pocket 2

Mission completes shortly after you arrive.

Group 1 (spawns after about 15 seconds)

3-4x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Ambusher/Hunter/Raider)
3-4x Elite Cruisers (Arch Gistum Centurion/Phalanx)

Group 2 (Spawns after about 1 minute)

2-3x Destroyers (Gistior Seizer/Shatterer/Trasher)
2x Elite Cruisers (Arch Gistum Centurion/Phalanx)

Pocket 2 contains a very nice belt with 6,447,561 units of Veldspar ready to mine. Belt radius ~40km around beacon point.

See Recon2 for parts 2 and 3.

Mobs in 2nd gate were another story. I was forced to warp out twice and 4 drones died. I was using Antimatter charges again but close quarters is lethal in the 2nd gate. With 4 hardeners (2 explosion and 2 kinetic) and 2 Armour Repairer II's going I was forced to flee. Went back with Lead Charges to enable me to get range. Killed frigates first using Warrior drone. Yes, I know u don't have to kill any of them but it was fun and a test of stamina.
