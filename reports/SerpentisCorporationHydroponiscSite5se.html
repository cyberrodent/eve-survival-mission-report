Serpentis Corporation Hydroponics Site DED 5 of 10

Last edited by AndyDying
Wed, 21 Sep 2011 10:57 UTC

Escalation from Serpentis Den.

Faction: Serpentis
Complex: DED 5 of 10
Space type: Gated Deadspace
Damage dealt: Therm, Kin
Recommended damage dealing: Therm/Kin
Recommended ships: Marauder

Stage 1


Aggro on warp-in

All groups 25-35km

3x Battleships (Core Flotilla Admiral/Rear Admiral) Sensor Damp
2x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Wing Leader)
7x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Infantry/Corelum Chief Sentinel)
21x Frigates (Coreli Defender/Coreli Protector)

Gate in 40 km, locked. Destroy all ships to unlock gate

No destroyable structures

Stage 2


Aggro on warp-in

All groups 30-40km

11x Battleships (Core Admiral/Rear Admiral/Flotilia Admiral/Vice Admiral) Sensor Damp
11x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Wing Leader/Squad Leader)
11x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Infantry/Chief Sentinel/Chief Protector/Chief Defender)
3x Destroyers (Corelior Trooper)
6x Frigates (Coreli Defender/Protector)

Gate in 40 km, locked. Destroy all ships to unlock gate

No destroyable structures

Stage 3


Aggro on warp-in

All groups 30-40km

7x Battleships (Core Flotilla Admiral/Rear Admiral/High Admiral) Sensor Damp
1x Battlecruiser (Corelior Trooper)
10x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Infantry/Chief Sentinel)
11x Frigates (Coreli Defender/Protector/Initiate)
3x Serpentis Stasis Tower Heavy Web!
1x Serpentis Cruise Missile Battery
1x Serpentis Heavy Missile Battery
2x Serpentis Light Missile Battery
9x Tower Sentry Serpentis II
1x Sarpati Family Enforcer (Overseer Battlecruiser (1.5kk Bounty))

Destroy Sarpati Family Enforcer and complex will be completed. A good idea to destroy Stasis Towers

Sarpati Family Enforcer drops 18th Tier Overseer's Personal Effects and may drop Corelum B-type loot
