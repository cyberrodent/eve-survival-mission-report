The Sansha Spies, level 2


Last edited by ShintuFelper:
Updated ranges and rat list
Thu, 05 Feb 2015 12:43 UTC

Faction: Sansha's Nation
Mission Type: Deadspace
Damage Dealt: EM, Thermal
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM
Recommended ships: Drake, Harbinger

There are 2 groups (one 30km away, and another 60km away) which pretty much aggro as soon as you arrive. They consist of:

7x Frigate (Centii Servant)
1-2x Destroyer (Sansha Spy[Centior Cannibal])
2x Sansha Sentry I

There's quite a bit of damage coming in because everything aggroes in warp in. The Servants will be out of range but they'll approach quickly.

Blitz:
Kill the Spy(s) and warp out, he dies pretty fast when shot with EM damage.
