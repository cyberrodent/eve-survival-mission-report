Chapter 2

Epic Arc Overview


Starter agent: Arment Caute
Agent corporation: Federal Intelligence Office
Agent location: Orvolle (Gallente Counter-intelligence Center)
Standings required: 3.00 (Either Gallente faction or Federal Intelligence Office)

OR

Starter agent: Atma Aulato
Agent corporation: Ytiri
Agent location: Obe (Counter Intelligence Center)
Standings required: 3.00 (Either Caldari faction or Ytiri)

OR

Starter agent: Yada Vinjivas
Agent corporation: Guristas
Agent location: Taisy (Guristas Recruitment Center)
Standings required: 3.00 (Either Guristas Pirate faction or Guristas)

Additional skills required: Hacking.

Important note :

Standing is only checked by the starter agent. After that all subsequent agents within the Epic Arc will not check standings.
Also only the following ships are allowed into the majority of sites: Assault Ship, Covert Ops, Destroyer, Electronic Attack Ship, Frigate, Interceptor, Interdictor, Rookie ship, Shuttle, and Stealth Bomber

Chapter 1 - Probationary


Mission 1A - Enemy of my Enemy


Given by Arment Caute.
Report to Kori Latamaki in H-PA29 (H-PA29 IV - Moon 2 - Guristas Assembly Plant).

Mission 1B - Recruitment Drive


Given by Atma Aulato.
Report to Kori Latamaki in H-PA29 (H-PA29 IV - Moon 2 - Guristas Assembly Plant).

Mission 1C - Turning Coat


Given by Yada Vinjivas.
Report to Kori Latamaki in H-PA29 (H-PA29 IV - Moon 2 - Guristas Assembly Plant).

Mission 2 - Intelligence Mining


Faction: Minedrill (Considered Caldari)
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Kin (best), Therm (secondary)
Gated complex, no guards on initial gate.

First Room

Reinforcement's spawn every 2 minutes.

Wave 1:
3x Frigate (Minedrill Expedition Frigate)

Wave 2:
2x Frigate (Minedrill Expedition Frigate)
1x Elite Frigate (Minedrill Expedition Elite Frigate)

Wave 3:
1x Cruiser (Minedrill Expedition Cruiser)
2x Frigate (Minedrill Expedition Frigate)

Wave 4:
2x Destroyer (Minedrill Expedition Destroyer)
2x Frigate (Minedrill Expedition Frigate)
1x Industrial (Minedrill E518 Industrial)Drops objective

Mission 3 - Planning the Operation


A dialogue only arc branch point.

Mission 4A - Sabotage 101


Faction: Caldari State
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/Scramble: Web/Scam
Extras: Jamming
Recommended damage dealing: Kin (best), Therm (secondary)

Gated complex, no guards on initial gate.

First Room

Contains 44 Proximity Charge structures that will detonate if you get within 7km of them. Should be destroyable with weapons.

Second Room

Initial Group:
3x Destroyer (State Yumi) Trigger
2x Frigate (State Shukoro Shuriken)
1x Frigate (Taibu State Shuriken) Jamming
1x Frigate (Taibu State Suriage) Scram & Web

Reinforcments:
1x Cruiser (State Dogo)
2x Destroyer (State Shukoro Choji)

Assembly Station DC105-A contains the mission objective.

Mission 4B - Brassy Faced Bastard


Faction: Caldari State
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/Scramble: Web/Scram
Extras: Jamming
Recommended damage dealing: Kin (best), Therm (secondary)

First Room

Initial Group:(does not engage until engaged or within 20km of gate)
2x Cruiser (Caldari Cruiser Vessel)
8x Frigate (Caldari Frigate Vessel)

Second Room

Initial Group:
3x Destroyer (State Yumi) Trigger
2x Frigate (State Shukoro Shuriken)
1x Frigate (Taibu State Shuriken) Jamming
1x Frigate (Taibu State Suriage) Scram & Web

Reinforcments:
1x Cruiser (State Dogo)
2x Destroyer (State Shukoro Choji)

Assembly Station DC105-A contains the mission objective.

Mission 5 - Upward Momentum


Report to Celis Leglise in 6NJ8-V (6NJ8-V VII - Moon 2 - Guristas Logistics Outpost).
