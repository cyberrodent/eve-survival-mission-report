Striking Down Two Flies With One Swing, Level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 08:08 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel / Mercenary
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal
Damage dealt: Exp/Kin
Recommended damage dealing: Exp/Kin
Extras: Painters
Web/scramble: Repair Station's Stasis Tower

Single Pocket

No Enemies on Warp-in.

Spawn #1 (Spawns when outpost is harmed)

4 Groups at 60km from warp-in.

6x Frigates - Gistior
5x Cruisers - Gistum
3x Heavy Missile Launcher Batteries
1x Stasis Tower

Spawn #2 (Spawns when outpost is destroyed)

4x Mercenary Cruiser
1x Mercenary Frigate

Blitz
Destroy the Repair Station and warp away

Note
-The Repair Outpost will help enemies heavily, repairing them.
-If you warp out of the mission, and the warp-in again, you'll land at 2km from the Repair Outpost.
