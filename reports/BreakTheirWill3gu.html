Break Their Will, Level 3


Last edited by AmnesiaC:
Edited Tip
Tue, 24 May 2016 23:36 UTC

Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space (mwd does work)
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Tracking/Sensor Jam: Nullifiers / light Jam
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: kin/therm
Recommended ships: Drake

Single Pocket


At warp-in there are only structures, including the Repair Station. When the Repair Station is shot at, first group of rats spawns. Shooting the Aux Generators won't trigger the spawn.

First Spawn: when you first hit the repair station

6-7x Destroyers - Pithior Renegade/Terrorist/Nihilist
5-6x Cruisers - Pithium Nullifier/Killer/Silencer/Annihilator/etc. Light Jamming from Annihilator
3x heavy missile batteries 40K bounty each
1x stasis tower

Tip: Destroy the repair station, or else they will be insta-repaired.

Destroying the aux power generators next to the station, will disable the station's repair capability. You may have to warp out and come back to get ccp's system to notice the power is out. This is reported as not working. Even after warping out, It did not work.

Merc reinforcements spawn: After you kill the repair station.
4x Cruisers - Mercenary Lietenant/Corporal/Commander
1x Frigate - Mercenary Fighter

Blitz:

Just destroy the repair station and warp out

Roids
21 Scordite roids about 1.2m units
40 approx Veld roids. about 2.5m units

Another classy Level 3 mission for me with medium difficulty. Be prepared to face heavy lags after you shoot the repair station, because of the mass aggro.

The Sansha Missile Towers do three times the usual heavy missile damage (=450hp). An example: having 55% EM resistance one tower missile still will hit you for 203 damage. A normal heavy missile fired from ships will never do more than 150 damage. So take out the towers first if you aren't tanked against EM. I always use Therm/EM/Invul as hardeners as a Caldari in this mission.

When I 'blitzed' this mission, I once got jammed (by a Nullifier) before the repair station was shot down and thus had to face the massaggro for a longer period than I intended to. The Pithum Killers can jam you as well.
