Evolution, Level 4 (Storyline)


Last edited by ArrekLemmont
Tue, 17 Feb 2015 02:20 UTC


Faction: Rogue Drones
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm/EM/Exp
Extras: Neut(Drone Energy Neutralizer Sentry I)
Web/scramble: Drone Stasis Sentry I(web) Strain Splinter Alvi (Web/Scram)
Recommended damage dealing: EM primary, Thermal secondary
Recommended ship classes: Battleship/tengu
Recommended generic setup: Passive Tank

Note: As with all Storyline missions this will give you negative derived faction bump.

Pocket 1 of 2


Warp In:


8x Frigate (Worker Drone)

Spawn 1: Initiated by attacking Worker Drone


4-6x Battleships (Alvus Queen/Matriarch)
12x Frigates (Strain Splinter Alvi/Silverfish Alvi/Strain Silverfish Alvi)
4x Sentry (Drone Cruise Missile Battery/Drone Stasis Sentry I/Drone Energy Neutralizer
Sentry I) at 74km+ from warp in

Spawn 2: Initiated by destroying second to last Battleship.


4x Battleships (Alvus Queen/Matriarch)
4x Battlecruisers (Enforcer Alvatis)

Spawn 3: Initiates by destroying second to last Battleship.


4x Battleships (Matriarch Alvus/Albus Queen)
4x Cruisers (Strain Disintegrator Alvum)

Spawn 4:


4x Battlecruisers (Crippler Alvatis)
4x Battleships (Alvus Queen/Matriarch)

Loot:


Over 3000+ m³ of drone alloys 20kk bounty, making this the heaviest level 4 rogue drone mission, loot-wise.

Pocket 2 of 2 (Bonus)


All Aggro on Warp in


15x Battleships (Alvus Queen/Matriarch Alvus)
15x Frigates (Strain Sunder Alvi/Strain Raider Alvi/Hunter Alvi/Barracuda Alvi)
10x Cruisers (Striker Alvatis/Defeater Alvatis/Crippler Alvatis)

Hint: The total ships comes in 3 waves and each wave is trigged by killing the Strain Sunder Alvi's off from each group)
The gallente carrier drops a Federation Navy Fleet Rear-Admiral tag

Actual advice: This second room broke the tank on my vargur. That hasn't happened in any mission before.
The carrier wreckage (not an actual ship) is based on whatever faction you run the mission for. I got a minmatar carrier wreck from a SoE mission, hence I suppose the tag would be of that faction as well.

Kill the tackle first. Do not warp out if you do not have to, because if you do - the entire mission spawns will be triggered when you come back. You'll be facing volleys of easily 4000 - 6000 DPS. Fit accordingly.

Blitz


Mission complete when destroying the Infested Station inthe first pocket , no drones have to be destroyed, except scrammers of course.

Structures


No structures drop loot.

Total Loot/Salvage Value: ~ 41.600.000 isk ~59,931,500 isk (see comment below for breakdown)

-- CathyDrall (2011-06-13 04:38:42)
Passive shield tank Myrmidion works well for this (all lows Shield Power Relay, Core defense purgers in the rigs, and 2-3 large shield extenders). Still, it is a good idea to put the stasis tower and NOS tower out as early as possible. With passive hardeners and good shield skills, though, this one is a breeze.

Make it go faster with omnilinks and kinetic drones (Hornet, Vespa, Wasp/Warden). Use small drones (on small and medium enemy drones first) until you have destroyed the two towers, then you can switch to heavy drones/ sentries. Drones may see aggro, but not much and it is easily manageable (usually when a spawn comes in).

Auto aggro in the first room includes scrambling and webbing drones, so take them out first (the webbers slow you down even more, and it's always a good idea to keep your "warp door" open). Try not to target/fire at the worker drones, doing so will invite the next spawn (if you have good skills, though, you can handle it).

DPS with a passive Myrm isn't that great, so you will probably want to loot and salvage the first room before moving on to the second.
