Guristas Strike - Incriminating Evidence (5 of 10)


Faction: Odamian
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ships: Drake

Single Pocket


Group 1

Instant aggro.
Distance: 10km.
5x Frigates (Odamian Privateers) (Tristan)
3x Cruisers (Odamian Veterans) (Vexor)
1x Cruiser (Odamian Master) (Thorax) (Might drop a +3 implant)

Structures
Sometimes the Storage Facility drops general cargo, the rest drops nothing.

Fast cruisers, able to outrun a 400m/s Brutix.

Guristas Strike - Final Battle (10 of 10)


Faction: Serpentis
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/therm
Recommended ships: Drake

No hostiles at initial gate.

Pocket 1


Single Group

Distance: 40km
Instant aggro.
4x Serpentis light missile batteries
3-5x Frigates (Coreli Safeguard/Guard)
1x Cruiser (Corelum Chief Patroller/Watchman)

Structures
No loot from structures.

Pocket 2


Group 1

Distance: 17km
Instant aggro.
5-6x Frigates (Coreli Defender/Protector)
1x Battlecruiser (Corelatis Platoon Leader/Squad Leader)

Group 2

Distance: 50km
3x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Watchman/Chief Patroller)

Group 3

Distance: 60km
4x Frigates (Coreli Protector/Defender)
2x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Infantry/Chief Sentinel)

Group 4

Distance: 80km
5x Frigates (Coreli Protector/Defender)
1x Battlecruiser (Corelatis Platoon Leader)

Structures
The Storage Facility and Habitation Modules can drop general goods.

Pocket 3


Group 1

Distance: 1-10km
Instant aggro.
4x Destroyers (Corelior Trooper/Soldier)
2x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender/Protector)

Group 2

Distance: 60km
4x Frigates (Guardian Veteran) Web/Possible Scramble
1x Battlecruiser (Corelatis Platoon Leader)

Group 3

Distance: 100km
4x Battleships (Core High Admiral/Grand Admiral)
5x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Platoon Leader)
4x Destroyers (Corelior Trooper/Infantry)

Group 4

Distance: 110km
3x Battleships (Core Lord Admiral)
4x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Captain Sentry/High Captain)
5x Destroyers (Corelior Infantry/Soldier)

Structures
The Central Control Hub may drop a +3 implant.
Other structures have no loot.

The final pocket in the last mission is missing. And that is of quite a bit of interest, since it is harder than some level 4 missions if you want to fight it.

4 groups:

1. 5-10 km, Autoaggro, 5 frigs + 1 cruiser

2. 60km, Center: 1 Cruiser/BC, 4 frigs

3. 110 km, to the left: 3x BS (Core Grand Admiral, 1,2M), 4 BC, some frigs

4. 100km, to the right: 4x BS (3x Core Lord Admiral, 990k, 1x Core High Admiral, xxx), 5 BC, frigs

Attacking any single group causes no further aggro, attacking the sub center aggroes the whole room.

Web: Guardian Veteran. Don't know whether they scramble, too.

Strategy I used:
Approach the group. Target the BS's first, then the BC's, and frigs last. Tanked the damage while the Ogre I's killed the BS's. Damage was easily tankable once the first BS popped. Then just sat back and let the drones have their way with the rest of the BS's, then BCs and finally the Frigs.
