Guristas Hideaway


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Tue, 06 Oct 2015 14:41 UTC

Faction: Guristas Pirates
Location: High Sec
Damage Dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended Damage Dealing: Kin(best), Therm(secondary)

This anomaly has two different variants, each Hideout will be one or the other. The 'Observation Post' variant is characterized by the presence of a Forward Observation Post(the big satellite dish). The 'Bunker' variant is defined by having a Guristas bunker near the warp-in.

Observation Post Variant


On warp-in everything will agro. Trigger is final kill of wave.

Initial Group

1-2x Frigate (Pithi Arrogator/Imputor)

Wave 1

1-2x Frigate (Pithi Infiltrator/Invader)

Wave 2

2-3x Frigate (Pithi Arrogator/Imputor)

Wave 3

2-3x Frigate (Pithi Infiltrator/Invader)


Bunker Variant


On warp-in everything will agro. Trigger is final kill of wave.

Initial Group

1-2x Frigate (Pithi Arrogator/Imputor)

Wave 1

1-2x Frigate (Pithi Infiltrator/Invader)

Wave 2

2-3x Frigate (Pithi Arrogator/Imputor)

Wave 3

2-3x Frigate (Pithi Wrecker/Plunderer)
