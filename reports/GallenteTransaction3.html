Faction: Gallente

Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/scramble: Federation Navy Delta II Support Frigate (web)

Recommended ship classes: Cruiser, Battlecruiser

Group 3

Distance: 50km to right
2x Cruiser (Federation Navy Vexor)
5x Frigate (Federation Navy Soldier)


Blitz
Destroy Group 1.
