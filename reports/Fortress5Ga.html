The Fortress, Level 5


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:59 UTC


Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Unknown
Damage dealt: Therm/Kin
Extras: Neut (Gallente Energy Neutralizer Sentry III)
Web/scramble: Elite Federation Manica/Libertus
Recommended damage dealing: kin/therm

Warp-In


  • Everything auto-aggros on warp-in.
  • Wave 1 Spawns when the Capital Ship Maintenance Array goes below 25% shield.
  • Wave 2 Spawns when the Array goes below 50% armor.
  • Wave 3 Spawns when the Array goes below 50% hull.
  • Waves 1 and 3 are fighters, and do more DPS than frigates.

    • Initial Spawn


    6x Battleships (Federation Praktor Phanix/Praeses)
    6x Battlecruisers (Federation Praktor Arx/Auxillia)
    8x Frigates (Elite Federation Manica/Libertus) Web/Scram
    4x Gallente Cruise Missile Batteries
    4x Tower Sentry Gallente III
    8x Gallente Energy Neutralizer Sentry III

    Wave 1


    10x Fighters (Capital Ship Maintenance Array Guards)

    Wave 2


    8x Battleships (Federation Praktor Hyperion)
    6x Battlecruisers (Federation Praktor Arx/Auxillia)
    3x Frigates (Elite Federation Manica/Libertus) Web/Scram
    4x Tower Sentry Gallente III

    Wave 3


    10x Fighters (Capital Ship Maintenance Array Guards)

    Blitz
    Ignore initial spawns and triggers. Destroy Starbase Capital Ship Maintenance Array.

    Structures

    Starbase Capital Ship Maintenance Array drops trade goods and metal scraps. The Other structures drop no loot.

    Tip:
    • You can warp in at 100KM by warping to 0, stopping your ship, and then warping to 100KM.
    • Expect every frigate to web, scramble, or both.
    • Be prepared for frequent sensor dampening to come from the battlecruisers.
