The convoy wreck that you must salvage is 40km away surrounded by 1 group of Minmatar ships

Tip
Lots of Minmatar Battleships and they fly fast, outrun their turret tracking with a speed tank and watch out for the painter spawns.

The initial spawn is right.

Killing the Republic Tribal Jarl triggered a 5-battleship spawn, as per the mission information. Salvaging the mission wreck (and gettin the mission objective item) afterwards did not trigger the mission completion.

Killing ALL the Republic Jotuns did not trigger any spawns.
Killing the Cruiser group (Republic Chief Rodul) triggered the spawn of 5 battleships, as per mission info.
