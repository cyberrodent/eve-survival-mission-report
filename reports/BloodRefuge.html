Blood Refuge


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Fri, 04 Jul 2014 14:46 UTC

Faction: Blood Raiders
Location: High-Sec
Damage Dealt: EM/Therm
Web/Scramble: None
Extras: Tracking Disruption Corpii Collector
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM(best), Therm(secondary)

Single Pocket

Auto-aggro from each wave. Last ship in each waves triggers next wave.

Initial Group

2-3x Frigate (Corpii Follower/Herald/Upholder/Worshipper)
2x Sentry (Tower Sentry Bloodraider I/Light Missile Battery)

Wave 1

2-3x Frigate (Corpii Collector/Herald/Seeker/Upholder) Tracking Disruption Collector

Wave 2

2-3x Frigate (Corpii Collector/Raider/Reaver/Seeker) Tracking Disruption Collector
0-2x Destroyer (Corpior Devoter/Templar)

Wave 3

0-3x Frigate (Corpii Diviner/Raider)
2-3x Destroyer (Corpior Visionary)

Wave 4

0-2x Frigate (Corpii Diviner/Engraver/Raider)
1-3x Destroyer (Corpior Devoter/Templar)
