Completed this with 1x Raven, 1x Drake, 2x Basilisks. Combined DPS of about 700-750 (both combat pilots are young). DPS was a little tight on the final wave, but using the high alpha on the cruisers and having the drake get at minimum range made it take about 4-5 minutes.

Cap was not an issue for the basis (logi 4 and logi 5) as long as I turned off a couple shield transporters on the one getting neuted. Ran buffer fit on everybody, so approximately 70%+ resists on the DPSers and 80%+ on the basis.
