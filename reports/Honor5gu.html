Honor, Level 5


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:15 UTC

Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with acceleration gates
Damage dealt: Kinetic/Thermal
Extras: Neut, Web, Scramble, Jamming
Web/scramble: Elite Frigates
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic
Recommended ships: Passive shield tanked ships(eg: Drake, Nighthawk, Ishtar, etc); Active Tank + Logistics (eg: Marauder + Dominix / Rattlesnake)

No scramblers or webifiers in Pocket 1, but there are 6 in Pocket 2. Neut towers in both Pockets, so I recommend you go with passive tank.
First one to enter both Pockets will draw full aggro automaticly. Smaller respawns in both pockets, addition: no respawns at all, if all (not necessarily the neut) towers are destroyed first, same as Serpentis version probably.
Remember that this is just an example of what can spawn, you may get different spawns in your mission.

Pocket 1:


Full Room aggro upon warp-in.

Single Group

2x Guristas Energy Neutralizer Sentry III
4x Guristas Cruise Missile Batteries
1x Tower Sentry Guristas III
2x Elite Cruisers (Dire Pithum Mortifier) (Jam)
2x Battlecruisers (Pithatis Enforcer)
11x Battleships (Pith Massacer, Pith Exterminator, Pith Usurper)

Pocket 2:


Single Group

5x Guristas Energy Neutralizer Sentry II
7x Guristas Cruise Missile Batteries
7x Guristas Heavy Missile Batteries
6x Elite Frigates (Dire Pithi Despoiler, Dire Pithi Demolisher) (Web/Scramble)
3x Elite Cruisers (Dire Pithum Nullifier) (Jam)
3x Battlecruisers (Pithatis Death Dealer)
10x Battleships (Pith Usurper, Pith Exterminator, Pith Massacrer)

Tactics/Notes/Spoilers:
- The BS's may trigger another spawn of 4 frigates and/or 3 destroyers
- Mission is completed once the recruitment station is destroyed
