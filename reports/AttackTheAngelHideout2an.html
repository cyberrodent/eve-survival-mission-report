Attack the Angel Hideout, Level 2


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Wed, 04 Feb 2015 19:02 UTC


Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Damage dealt: Exp/kin
Recommended damage dealing: Exp/kin
Recommended ship classes: Frigate, Destroyer, Cruiser
Mission: Take out everyone. Drones are optional.
Remote Complete: No

Warp-in


No aggro on warp-in.

Group 1

Distance: 30km
2x Frigates (Cleaner[Balphagor/Mammon Apis/Infester Alvi])

Group 2

Distance: 60km
3x Frigates (Angel Harpy[Gistii Outlaw/Thug])
2x Frigates (Angel Medusa[Gistii Ambusher/Raider])
1x Cruiser (Mother-in-Law[Gistum Predator])

Structures
Angel Bunker sometimes drops loot. (Most of the time you wont get anything)

Blitz
Group 1 is not needed for completion.

Asteroids
1- Veldspar

Salvage/Loot
about 50m3

Time
Blitz: 45 sec to 5 min
No Blitz: 1:30 to 7 min

Rewards and Bonus

Reward: 127,000 ISK 171 LP
Bonus: 121,000 ISK within 2 hours and 8 minutes.

Bounty
Cleaner: 7,875 ISK
Angel Harpy: 4,875 ISK
Angel Medusa: 7,875 ISK
Mother-in-Law: 37,500 ISK

Tips
T1 drones work fine in this mission. They didn't shoot at any of mine.
