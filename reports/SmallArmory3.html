Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Explosive, Kinetic
Web/Scramble: Angel Webifier, Spider Drones II, Angel Viper
Recommended damage dealing: Explosive, Kinetic

no hostiles at initial gate

Pocket 1

2x Angel Heavy Missile Battery
2x Tower Sentry Angel II (Automatic aggro)

Group 1 (Automatic aggro)

40-45km
2x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Primus)

25-35km
3x Frigates (Gistii Hunter/Impaler)
2x Destroyers (Gistior Shatterer)

75-80 km
2x Frigates (Gistii Hunter/Impaler)

Structures

60-70 km

110-120km
2x Frigates (Angel Viper/Webifier) Web/Scramble
2x Cruisers (Gistum Centerion)
