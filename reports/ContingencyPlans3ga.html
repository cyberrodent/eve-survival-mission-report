Contingency Plans, Level 3


Last edited by ThisIsMyName:
Changed Range of FC to 48k. Confirmed (12/24/14)
Wed, 24 Dec 2014 00:59 UTC

Faction: Gallente Federation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space
Damage dealt: Therm, Kinetic
Extras: Sensor Dampening
Recommended damage dealing: Therm, Kinetic
Recommended ship classes: Stealth Bomber(to blitz), Battlecruiser, Cruiser

Single Pocket

No ships at gate. All ships initially present in the pocket together with a large fleet of allies fighting the Gallente.
To complete the mission, destroy the Fleet Commander (48km).

Group 1 (15 - 40km, auto-aggro)
6 x Frigates (Federation Clavis)
5 x Cruisers (Federation Loras/Federation Hastarius)
1 x Battleship (Federation Triarius)

Group 2 (50 - 70km, auto-aggro)
4 x Elite Frigates (Elite Federation)
4 x Battle Cruisers (Federation Praktor Centurion/Praktor Legionarius/Calo)

Blitz
Destroy the Fleet Commander.
