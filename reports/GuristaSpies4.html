The Guristas Spies, level 4


Last edited by OdotakMotsu
Sun, 08 May 2016 10:22 UTC

Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm
Extras: Jamming
Recommended damage dealing: Kin, therm.
Recommended ships: Rattlesnake, CNR, Dominix, Drake, Raven, Sentry Ishtar
Video: Tengu CNR


Single Pocket

The groups will spawn in intervals after you arrive, but it's just a few seconds between each spawn.
The spawns may vary, this is just an example.
Sometimes you may get agro when you warp in, sometimes you don't.
Drones are not attacked by non attacked groups.

Group 1 (23 - 30km)

1x Destroyer (Pithior Supremacist/Terrorist)
3x Battleships (Pith Extinguisher/Destroyer/Exterminator)

Group 2 (30km)

3x Battlecruisers (Pithatis Assaulter/Enforcer)
4x Battleships (Pith Eradicator/Obliterater/Exterminator/Extinguisher)

Group 3 (57 - 80km)

2x Destroyers (Pithior Supremacist/Terrorist)
3x Battlecruisers (Guristas Spy)
1x Battleship (Pith Massacrer/Conquistador)

Blitz
(Read comments) *Mission is completed after killing the group with the Spies; attacking them will sometimes draw aggro, but not always.

*This is an easy mission to do afk in a drone boat if you want bounties instead of blitzing it.

Easiest to blitz with long range BS. Just microjump past the groups and fire at the group with the spies, leave when done.
