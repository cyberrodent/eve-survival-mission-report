Silence Deep as Death, level 3


Last edited by TtoraiEve:
Mission loot can drop changed.
Sat, 28 Jul 2012 04:04 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: (Exp?, Kin?)
Recommended damage: Exp, Kin
Recommended ships: Drake

Single Pocket

At warp-in there is 1 group. Upon this group's destruction a reinforcement wave spawns.
Last ship in 2nd wave drops a cargo container. The 17 miners (i.e. 34.0m^3 mission objective) are in that can.

First Wave

1-15km
2/3x Frigates (Gistii Outlaw/Raider/Shatterer/Republic Radur)
2/3x Destroyers (Gistior Defacer/Defiler)
3x Cruisers (Gistum Liquidator/Marauder)

Reinforcement Wave

5-15km (spawned when 1st wave is destroyed)
2/3x Frigates (Gistii Impaler/Raider)
0/1x Crusier (Republic Solon)
2/3x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Praefectus/Tribuni)

Asteroids
Veldspar - 16 asteroids
