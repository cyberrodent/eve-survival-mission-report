The Blockade, level 3


Last edited by TechReviewsAndHelp
Wed, 16 Oct 2013 02:19 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Damage dealt: Kinetic/Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic
Recommended ships: Dominix, Hurricane, Drake
Video: http://youtu.be/tSKMDKrrx70

Notes: Spawns and triggers may vary, this is just an example.
Tip: If ships aren't the same, usually there is one ship name that has only one ship of it in each spawn, that is usally the trigger.

Initial Group: (20km) (Auto-Aggro)

1x Starbase Stasis Tower
3x Cruisers (Corelum Guardian Chief Infantry)
3x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Squad Leader)
1x Destroyer (Corelior Infantry) (Trigger)

Spawn 1: (105km) (Auto-Aggro)

7x Destroyers (Corelior Soldier/Cannoneer/Trooper)
5x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Wing Leader/Squad Leader)
1x Battlecruiser (Corelatis Platoon Leader) (Trigger)

Spawn 2: (95-145km) (Auto-Aggro)

4x Destroyers (Corelior Cannoneer)
2x Cruisers (Corelum Guardian Chief Protector)
6x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Wing Leader)
1x Battlecruiser (Corelatis Captain Sentry) (Trigger)

Spawn 3: (85-175km) (Auto-Aggro)

7x Cruisers (Corelum Guardian Safeguard/Chief Defender)
7x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Squad Leader)

Spawn 4: (Auto-Aggro)

8x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Guardian Watchman/Captain Sentry) (Dampening)
4x Fighters (Coreli Guardian Defender)

Structures
None drop loot

Blitz
Hit triggers in Initial Group and Spawns 1-3. Mission marked as complete after trigger for Spawn 4 is killed.

Video
This video shows one of many ways to do this mission.
http://youtu.be/tSKMDKrrx70
