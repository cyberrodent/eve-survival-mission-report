Trimming The Fat, Level 2


Last edited by TechReviewsAndHelp
Wed, 05 Jun 2013 04:15 UTC

Faction: Caldari
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kinetic/Thermal
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Thermal
Recommended ship class: Cruiser
Recommended generic setup: Fast DPS (5km-15km)
Video: http://youtu.be/2Xb1EbTpy3k

Warp-In (Aggro)


Group 1 (20km) (Auto aggro)

3x Frigates (State Nagasa/State Shinai)
1x Destroyer (State Kai Gunto/State Yumi)
1x Cruiser (State Bushi) [Objective]
  • Spawns a little after warp in

Blitz:
Kill 'The Executive' (Cruiser), and the mission is complete.

Mineable Asteroids
None

Structures
Many structures, No loot from the structures

Note
No faction standing lost

Video
This video shows you how it's done. It also proves that there is no standing lost.
http://youtu.be/2Xb1EbTpy3k
