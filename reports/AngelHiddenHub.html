Faction: Angel Cartel
Location: Nullsec
Damage Dealt: Exp/Kin
Web/Scramble: Arch Gistii Outlaw
Extras: Target Painting Gist Nephilim/Saint
Recommended Damage Dealing: Exp(best), Kin(secondary)

Single Pocket

Warp into a small pocket with a ruined stargate and a stonehenge object. Final kill is trigger for next wave, unless otherwise specified.

Initial Group

2x Cruisers (Gistum Centurion/Phalanx)
4x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Legatus/Tribunus)
4x Battleships (Gist Cherubim/Seraphim)


Wave 1

2x Elite Cruisers (Arch Gistum Centurion) Trigger
7x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Praefectus/Primus/Tribunus)
4x Battleships (Gist Cherubim/Seraphim)

Wave 2

2x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Hunter)
3x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Legatus, Primus, Tribuni)
7x Battleships (Gist Cherubim/Nephilim/Saint/Seraphim/Warlord) Target Painting Nephilim/Saint Trigger

Wave 3

1x Elite Frigate (Arch Gistii Outlaw) Scram & Web
1x Elite Cruiser (Arch Gistum Centurion/Phalanx)
6x Battleships (Gist Seraphim)
