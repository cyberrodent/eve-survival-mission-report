Stay Frosty, level 3


Mission type: Mining
Space type: Deadspace, no gate. Bookmarks not effective (you will always warp to beacon).
Faction: None. Rats typical for system will show up (Angel Thugs, for example).

Mine 10 units of Azure Ice (10000.0 m3). These are in two asteroids of 5 units each. They are 15-25km from beacon.

A mackinaw w/t2 ice harvesters and t1 drones does fine.

No other mineable rocks in system, just weird ones like Hollow Asteroid, Cloven Grey Asteroid, etc.

Two NPC structures were present, (Low Tech Energy Harvester & Mining Asteroid) loot drop unknown.
