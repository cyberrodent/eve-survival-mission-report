The Seven's Prison Facility, level 3


Last edited by SinSmith
Mon, 21 Jul 2014 09:00 UTC

Faction: Mercenaries
Mission type: Encounter
Spacetype: Normal
Damage Received: All Types
Recommended Damage Dealing: Kinetic/Thermal
Recommended Ships: Cruiser or higher
Difficulty: Very Easy
Video: Cerberus

Single pocket

Aggro from everything at warp-in.
3x Sentry Gun (I and/or II)
3x Frigates (Seven Grunt/Death Dealer/Deathguard)
1x Cruiser (Seven Assassin/Thug)
1x Cruiser (Named)

Blitz
The member of the Seven (named cruiser) may appear as Olufami, Elena Gazky, or Schmidt along with a Seven Assassin or a Seven Thug. The mission will complete when both of them are killed; the rest are optional kills.

Structures
Guns may drop ammo.
The Prison will drop 5 units of mining crystals and 10 livestock (Elite Slaves / Slaves / Prisoners).
