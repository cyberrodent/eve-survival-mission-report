Anomic Agent: Blood Raider Burner, Level 4


Last edited by CrocEvil:
typo fixing
Tue, 24 Mar 2015 16:49 UTC

Faction: Blood Raiders
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM+Therm (50% each)
Web/Scramble/NOS: Burner Cruor
Extras: You can not enter mission site in anything larger than a Frigate hull.
Recommended damage dealing: Exp/Kin
Recommended ship types: Assault Frigate, Pirate Faction Frigate


Pocket 1


Ship aggro on warp-in.

Hostile Ships (10 km)

1x Pirate Frigate (Burner Cruor)

General Tips
  • Burner NOS will drain typical AF capacitor in just several seconds;
  • Burner have "faction" webs/scram;
  • This Burner has very large armor buffer;

Kiting Tips
  • Burner NOS range is slightly bellow 10km;
  • Burner has no AB nor MWD, max nominal speed is around 380m/s;

Loot
CCP said that there is a slight chance of getting faction stuff in the loot. If you don't get faction modules, the loot is mostly made of Meta modules.

Bounty
5,000,000 ISK for the Burner
