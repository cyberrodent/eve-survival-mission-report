Rogue Drone Harassment, level 3


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Thu, 01 May 2014 23:59 UTC

Faction: Rogue Drones
Mission type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: All kinds (mostly Exp/Kin, some EM/Therm)
Web/scramble: Strain Infester Alvi
Recommended damage dealing: EM, Therm
Recommended ships: Drake, Harbinger

No hostiles at initial gate.

Pocket 1

No aggro upon warp in.

Group 1 (18-20km)

4x Cruiser (Annihilator Alvum)

Group 2 (20-25km)

4x Cruiser (Devastator Alvum)orbit @ 20km

Group 3 (40-50km)

Near Abandoned Drill.

1-2x Frigate (Raider/Sunder Alvi)
0-1x Elite Frigate (Strain Infester Alvi) Web/Scram
2x Cruiser (Violator Alvum)

Group 4 (65km)

Near Solar Harvester.

3x Cruiser (Viral Infector Alvum)orbit @ 21km

Asteroids
Veldspar: 41 Asteroids (889.059 units)

Pocket 2

Delayed aggro.

Group 1 (11-30km)

8x Cruiser (Devastator/Violator/Viral Infector Alvum)

Group 2 (20-45km)

1x Battlecruiser (Defeater Alvatis/Enforcer Alvatis)
1x Cruiser (Violator/Viral Infector/Disintegrator Alvum)

Group 3 (20-45km)

1x Cruiser (Bomber Alvum)

Asteroids
Veldspar: 32 Asteroids (279.059 units)

Blitz
Kill all drones in pocket 1. Group 1 in pocket 2(sometimes Group 2 has to be killed too, appears to be random). Warp out.
