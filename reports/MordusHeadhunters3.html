The Mordus Headhunters, Level 3


Last edited by SinSmith
Thu, 03 Apr 2014 23:54 UTC

Faction: Mordus
Mission type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kinetic, Thermal.
Web/scramble: Mordus Bounty Hunter
Recommended damage dealing: EM
Recommended ships: Drake, Retribution (AF), Hurricane, Caracal (T2 LM)
Video: Cerberus, Dominix

No hostiles at initial gate

Pocket 1




Group 1 (23-35km)

7x Frigates (Mordus Katana)
2x Cruisers (Mordus Puma)

Group 2 (45-65km)

3x Frigates (Mordus Katana/Rapier)
3x Cruisers (Mordus Leopard/Lion/Puma)

Pocket 2




Single Group (30-50km)

10x Frigates (Mordus Katana/Gladius)
2x Interceptors (Mordus Bounty Hunter) (Web/Scramble)
7x Cruisers (Mordus Leopard/Lion/Puma)

Structures
The small armories may drop an assortment of small to large ammo and light to heavy missiles

Loot
14.8kk (a few Meta4 modules)

Standing Loss
-0.0267% - This penalty was incurred for destroying Mordu's Legion Command's Mordus Leopard
-0.03% - This penalty was incurred for destroying Mordu's Legion Command's Mordus Lion
-0.03% - This penalty was incurred for destroying Mordu's Legion Command's Mordus Lion
