Passing the ISK, level 1

Faction: Local Pirates
Mission type: encounter/mining
Space type: Deadspace (mwd does not work)
Recommended ship class: Venture
Recommended generic setup: roid mining, with a medium and small shield extender

The goal is to mine 3600 units (1080 m3) of Angumene. The roid is hard to find by sight, and is located 22 km away from you. The Overview(All) let you see the roid (third in line).

Try to stay away from the station before warping-out, or you will be stuck in it.

Rats : 3 frigates, race depends on the system, sometimes they dont appear.
