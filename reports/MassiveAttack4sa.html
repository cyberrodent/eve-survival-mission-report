Massive Attack, Level 4


Last edited by GaladTirin:
added loot and salvage values
Wed, 02 Dec 2015 05:38 UTC

Faction: Sansha Nation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with Gates (MWD works as of March 6, 2010)
Damage dealt: EM (56%) / Therm (44%)
Web/scramble: Elite Frigates
Extras: Tracking Disruptor (Centum Execrator/Slaughterer, Centii Savage, Centii Slavehunter)
Recommended damage dealing: EM (best), Therm (secondary)
Recommended ship classes: T2/T3 Cruiser, Battlecruiser; Battleship if you have low skills & can't use T2
Video: Nightmare, Machariel


Note: The majority hostile ships are frigate/destroyer/cruiser class with only a handful of battlecruisers and only two battleships.
NPCs will aggro drones.

The warp gate into the pocket is guarded by a small group of ships. They will aggro immediately after you have warped in.

Warp Gate Guards:

5x Frigates (Centii Butcher/Manslayer)
3x Destroyers (Centior Abomination/Monster)


Pocket 1


Group 2 has a proximity aggro of 25km.
Deploying drones to attack Group 1 will aggro Group 2.

Group 1: Acceleration Gate (45km)

3x Frigates (Centii Butcher)
2x Destroyers (Centior Abomination/Monster)
3x Battlecruisers (Centatis Daemon/Wraith/Devil/Specter)

Group 2: Control Tower (40-45km)

3x Frigates (Centii Slavehunter/Savage) Tracking Disruptor
1x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Servant) Web/Scramble
2x Destroyers (Centior Abomination/Monster)
2x Cruisers (Centum Execrator/Slaughterer) Tracking Disruptor

Note : No loot from structures
Group 1 does not always aggro. If it doesn't, engaging group 1 will aggro group 2, but group 2 can be safely engaged without aggroing group 1.

Blitz
  • All warp gates are locked. Eliminate all groups from pocket 1 & 2.
  • Eliminate all ships from Group 3 in pocket 3. Warp out.
