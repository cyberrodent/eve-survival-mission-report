Break Their Will, Level 1


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:19 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Unknown w/o gate
Damage dealt: exp(55%)/Kin(16%)/therm(27%)/em(2%)
Extras: None
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended damage: (Explosive)

Single Pocket

1x Repair Outpost (39 km)

Group 1 (Auto Aggro)

Distance: 56-63km from Warp-in.
This group is triggered by shooting at the Repair Outpost.

4x Frigate (2x Gistii Hijacker, 2x Gistii Rogue)
4x Angel Point Defense battery/Repair Station's Point

Group 2 (Auto Aggro)

This group is triggered by destroying of the Repair Outpost.

5x Frigates (2x Mercenary Fighter, 3x Mercenary Rookie)

Blitz:
Destroy the repair station and warp out.

Standing:
Killing the Repair Outpost incurs a -4% standing penalty.

P.S.: As i have done this mission once, and blitzed it, i don't know if the 2 groups trigger additional rats.
