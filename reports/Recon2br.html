Recon, Level 2


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 05:53 UTC


Part 1 of 3


Faction: Blood Raider
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Em/Thermal
Extras: NOS (Elder Corpum Sage/Elder Corpum Priest)
Recommended damage dealing: Em/Thermal

Pocket 1

Four spawns, timed base, every 20 to 30 sec. All spawns are instant aggro. Ship names may change but ship class are the same in each spawn.

Spawn 1

1x Destroyer (Corpior Converter)
1x Elite Cruiser (Elder Corpum Sage - NOS)
1x Battlecruiser (Corpior Phantom)

Spawn 2

1x Elite Frigate (Elder Corpii Engraver)
1x Destroyer (Corpior Templar)
1x Elite Cruiser (Elder Corpum Priest - NOS)

Spawn 3

1x Destoyer (Corpior Devoter)
1x Cruiser (Corpum Shadow Sage)
1x Elite Cruiser (Elder Corpum Dark Priest)

Spawn 4

1x Destroyer (Corpior Friar)
1x Cruiser (Corpum Dark Priest)
1x Battlecruiser (Corpior Phantom)

Pocket 2

You will get mission completed when entering this pocket, so you don't have to kill the spawns there. The spawns are a bit random, ship name may change and you can get one ship more or less in each class ship group.

Spawn 1

x3 Elite Frigate (Elder Corpii Reaver)
x3/4 Elite Cruiser (Elder Corpum Dark Priest/Elder Corpum Shadow Sage)

Spawn 2

x2/3 Destroyer (Corpior Visionary/Corpior Cleric)
x2/3 Elite Cruiser (Elder Corpum Dark Priest)

Blitz
Use a shuttle (or any other preferred ((fast)) ship), get to the gate fast, use it, then warp out when you get to pocket 2. Mission is completed

Structures
No loot

Mining
Pocket 2 contains many Veldspar
