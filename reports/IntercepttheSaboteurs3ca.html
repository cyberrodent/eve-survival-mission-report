Intercept the Sabteurs, Level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:46 UTC

Faction: Caldari
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kinetic/Thermal
Extras: Light Jamming (State Ashigaru/Fudai/Chugen)
Web/scramble: Taido State Shirasaya
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic
Recommended ships: Drake

Warp-in


6x Frigate (State Shuriken)

Pocket 1

No Aggro upon warp-in

Group 1

40-45km
5x Cruisers (State Ashigaru/Bushi/Dogo/Fudai)
Attacking Group 1 will aggro Group 2

Group 2

60-75km
2x Elite Frigate (Taibu State Shinai/Shirasaya) (Web/Scram)

Group 3

87-100km
8x Frigate (State Daito/Shuriken)
5x Cruiser (State Bushi/Dogo/Fudai)
Attacking Group 3 will aggro Group 4

Group 4

100-117km
3x Frigate (State Shuriken)
2x Elite Frigate (Taibu State Nagasa/Shirasaya) (Web/Scram)
2x Destroyer (State Tsuba)
2x Cruiser (State Buke/Chugan)
3x Transports (Caldari Transport Ship)
Attacking Group 4 will aggro all groups.

Group 5

100-117km
3x Frigate (State Shuriken)
2x Destroyer (State Kissaki/Yumi)
2x Cruiser (State Ashigaru/Fudai)
Attacking Group 5 will aggro Group 4

Mineable Asteroids
51x Veldspar, 60-110km from warp-in

Blitz
Kill initial group, warp in, kill group 4, loot objective.

Tips
Objective drops from last ship killed in Group 4

Standing hits
Killing one of the transporters incurs a 2.4% standing penalty
