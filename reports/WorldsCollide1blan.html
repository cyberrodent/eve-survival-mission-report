Worlds Collide, level 1


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:47 UTC


Faction: Angel Cartel and Blood Raiders
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: All kinds
Reccomended damage dealing: EM/Therm for Blood. Exp/Kin for Angel.

Warp-In Pocket (Warzone)

First two accelerator gates are not locked and all NPCs at first and second level are optional kills, that will respawn. Only small ships may enter the first gate. Bookmarked location has Gate to Angel Base and Gate to Blood Base about 40km away.

Group guarding Angel Base Gate: (130km)

6x Gistii Hijackers (Frigate class)
2x Gistii Rogue (Frigate class)
1x Gistior Defacer (Destroyer class)

Group guarding Blood Base Gate: (110km) (Automatic aggro)

4x Corpii Seekers (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Collector (Frigate class)

Angel Base Pocket

Nearest group will aggro upon warp-in:

2x Gistii Ambusher (Frigate class)
1x Gistii Hunger (Frigate class)

(50% chance of spawn
1x Angel Overseer (Frigate class))

There are 4 other groups further away consisting of:

5x Gistii Hijackers (Frigate class)
1x Gistii Rogues (Frigate class)
5x Gistii Ambushers (Frigate class)
6x Gistii Raiders (Frigate class)
1x Gistior Shatterer (Destroyer class)

The gateway to research outpost is 15km from this warp-in point.

Blood Base Pocket

Nearest group will aggro upon warp-in:

3x Corpii Heralds (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Upholder (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Raider (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Diviner (Frigate class)

There are two other groups, one group consisting of:
1x Corpior Friar (Destroyer class)
1x Blood Disciple (Frigate class)

and the other group consisting of:
2x Corpii Engravers (Frigate class)
3x Corpii Reavers (Frigate class)

The gateway to research outpost is also 15km from this warp-in point.

Research Outpost Pocket

The nearest group will aggro upon warp-in:
The mission will complete when this group is destroyed.

2x Corpii Heralds (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Upholder (Frigate class)

The remaining:

10x Corpii Followers (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Herald (Frigate class)
2x Corpii Upholders (Frigate class)

do not aggro.

The Damaged Heron will contain the required 20 Ship's Crew this time, with no respawn of any NPCs or additional Ship's Crew throughout. 20 Ship's Crew is required to be brought back to agent to complete the mission.
