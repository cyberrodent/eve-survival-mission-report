No Excuse, Level 3


Last edited by DragonOfOne:
Bounty & loot
Mon, 16 Dec 2013 19:38 UTC

Faction: Gallente Federation Navy (although mission states Caldari!)
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (no gates)
Damage dealt: Thermal, Kinetic
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic, Thermal
Recommended ship classes: Ishkur, Hurricane
Recommended generic setup: Close combat. Normal tank.
Video: http://youtu.be/z2lZ1RaTCHA

Mission briefing
There is a monument where a battle in the Gallente-Caldari war occurred within Gallente space. We once tolerated pilgrimaging Caldari, but with this war we cannot allow the Caldari any excuse to proceed this deep within our space. Such leniency would only invite terrorism. I need you to destroy the monument, and any witnesses to the event.

Objective
Destroy the monument and leave no witnesses alive

Single Pocket:


Upon warp in, the pocket is empty. There are 7 monuments in a circle. Ships will spawn after destroying the monument in the middle. Mission is deemed complete after killing the last ship.

Single Group: (Automatic aggro)


Distance:15-17 km
3x Frigates (Federation Manica)
1x Frigate (Federation Libertus)
2x Cruisers (Federation Loras)

Isk
Bounty :
Loot : ~ 262,000 isk
Salvage : ~ 312,000 isk
Note
No standing loss with Gallente

Video: http://youtu.be/z2lZ1RaTCHA
