Pirate Business, Level 4


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sun, 29 Jul 2012 17:14 UTC

Faction: Minmatar
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Explosive, Kinetic
Recommended damage dealing: Explosive

Pocket 1

No auto-aggro. Groups 1 & 2 aggro together and Groups 3 & 4 aggro together.

Group 1

2x Frigate (Republic Kvarm / Otur)
3x Cruiser (Republic Tribal Ormur / Rodul)
2x Battleship (Republic Jarl / Pytara)

Group 2

5x Frigate (Republic Kvarm / Otur)
1x Frigate (Republic Tribal Gleeda) Target Painter

Group 3

4x Cruiser (Republic Tribal Ormur / Rodul)
2x Battlecruiser (Republic Norn)

Group 4

2x Cruiser (Republic Jarpur)
1x Cruiser (Republic Tribal Solon)
3x Battleship (Republic Jotun / Pytara)


Pocket 2

No auto-aggro. Groups 1, 2 & 4 aggro individually. Shooting Group 3 will aggro entire pocket.

Group 1

9x Frigate (Republic Kvarm / Otur / Gleeda) Gleeda: Target Painter
1x Elite Frigate (Republic Tribal Gleeda) Target Painter

Group 2

4x Cruiser (Republic Rodul / Manadis) Target Painter
4x Cruiser (Republic Tribal Rodul)
2x Battleship (Republic Pytara)

Group 3

4x Cruiser (Republic Tribal Ormur / Rodul)
4x Battleship (Republic Jotun / Pytara / Sigur)
2x Industrial (Minmatar Personnel Transport)

Group 4

4x Frigate (Republic Otur)
4x Cruiser (Republic Jarpur)
