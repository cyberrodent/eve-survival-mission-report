Intaki and Mordus Legion, Level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:45 UTC

Faction: Mordu's Legion
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: kin/therm
Extras: target painter
Recommended damage dealing: EM, kin
Recommended ship classes: Battleship, Battlecruiser
Recommended generic setup: All Ranges, Active Tank

Single Pocket


Group 1 (Aggro)
Distance: 40km
4x Frigate
8x Cruiser

Group 2 (Aggro)
Distance: 60km
1x Battleship

Summary
All ships come at you as soon as you initiate combat. They will orbit you at varying distances, from 2500m to 20km, and the battleship will try to stay at 30km. They fire a variety of hybrid weapons and all sizes of tracking kinetic missiles. Overall damage taken is high.

Tips
  • Get close to the Battleship and orbit it so it can't hit you.
  • If your ship is fast enough to avoid the hybrid turrets, you only need kinetic resistance to survive the missiles.
