Caldari Beacons, Level 5


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:55 UTC

Faction: Caldari
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace - Gated
Web/Scramble: All Frigates
Extras: Energy Neutralizer Sentries, NOS, ECM Jammers
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/Therm
Recommended ships: Completed in (1) Maelstrom w/ Passive Tank, and (1) Maelstrom w/ Shield Transporters, but recommend more DPS if you want to complete this mission within the 1.3 hour time bonus.

Acceleration Gate into Deadspace.

Pocket 1

Ships at 50-90k away



2 x Frigates (Taibu State Tachi) orbits at 10k, Web/Scramble, 28% ECM Jammer
2 x Cruisers (Taibu State Shugo) orbits at 18k, 35% ECM Jammer
4 x Battlecruisers (State Shukuro Seki) orbits at 23k
7 x Battleships (State Shukuro Nagashin/Turushima*) orbits at 68k/23k, *NOS
2 x Caldari Energy Neutralizer Sentry III

Pocket 2

Ships at 30-75k away



3 x Frigates (Taibu State Wakizashi) orbits at 17k, Web/Scramble
5 x Cruisers (Taibu State Bajo/Samurai) orbits at 33k
8 x Battleships (State Shukuro Nagashin) orbits at 68k
4 x Caldari Cruise Missile Batteries
2 x Caldari Energy Neutralizer Sentry IIIs

Mission is flagged Objective Complete when all ships are destroyed, even without destroying the Subspace Beacons (and despite what the mission objective states).


Spawn 1 (optional)

If you destroy the Subspace Beacon on the right

8 x Battleships (State Shukuro Shogun) orbits at 33k

Spawn 2 (optional)

If you destroy the Subspace Beacon in the middle

6 x Battleships (State Shukuro Turusima) orbits at 23k

Spawn 3 (optional)

If you destroy the Subspace Beacon on the left

6 x Battleships (State Shukuro Nagashin) orbits at 68k

Tips
  • Nagashins deal a heavier than average amount of thermal damage for Caldari NPCs
  • Pocket 1 ships deal 2,280 hp/s (Kin: 53%, Therm: 47%)
  • Pocket 2 ships deal 2,480 hp/s (Kin: 48%, Therm: 52%)
  • Use a Passive Tank, or plan a capacitor that receives an extra 80 units/sec
  • Consider FoF missiles and setting drones to “Aggressive” for when you are unable to target because of Jammers
