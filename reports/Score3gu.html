The Score, level 3


Last edited by PawelloSolette:
bounty
Mon, 03 Mar 2014 09:25 UTC

Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm
Extras: Jamming
Web/scramble: Sometimes by random frigate
Reccomended damage dealing: Kin, Therm
Bounty: 800k

Pocket 1


Group 1: (45-50km)

1x Pithi Destructor (Frigate class)
1x Pithum Mortifier (Cruiser class)
1x Pithum Inferno (Cruiser class)

Group 2: (45-50km)

1x Pithi Destructor (Frigate class
1x Pithum Inferno (Cruiser class)
1x Pithum Mortifier (Cruiser class)

Group 3: (60km)

1x Pithi Demolisher (Frigate class)
1x Pithior Terrorist (Destroyer class)

Pocket 2


Group 1: (60km)

1x Pithi Plunderer (Frigate class)
2x Pithum Inferno (Cruiser class)

Group 2: (50-60km)

2x Pithi Destructor/Demolisher (Frigate class)
1x Pithum Eraser (Cruiser class)

Group 3: (55-70km)

2x Pithi Destructor/Demolisher (Frigate class)
1x Pithum Mortifier (Cruiser class)
1x Pithatis Enforcer/Assaulter (Battlecruiser class)

Blitz

Kill all Ships in Pocket 1. Then kill Group 3 in Pocket 2. (Groups are all mixed together once they spawn. Target Group 3 based on name and distance. But see note below.)

Note
There's some disagreement on whether this mission is blitzable. Following my own blitz tip that has worked before, I just tried to blitz this mission twice, and it did NOT complete. Possibly the completion trigger is randomized, and hard to identify, due to Pocket 2 spawning all mixed together.


Structures

Pocket 1: no loot
Pocket 2: Structures cannot be destroyed.

Asteroids

Veldspar - 45 asteroids - 1,522,846 units of Veldspar
Plagioclase - 30 asteroids - 813,864 units of Plagioclase
Pyroxeres - 16 asteroids - 479,979 units of Pyroxeres
