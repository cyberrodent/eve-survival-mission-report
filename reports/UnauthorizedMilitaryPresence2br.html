Unauthorized Military Presence, level 2


Last edited by TechReviewsAndHelp
Sat, 01 Jun 2013 00:26 UTC

Faction: Blood Raiders
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM, Therm
Extras: Sensor Dampen - Corpii Seekers
Recommended damage dealing: EM, Therm
Recommended ships: Cruiser
Recommended generic setup: Close DPS
Video: http://youtu.be/yLdWQiLc-ss

Single Pocket


Group 1 (20-30km) (Delayed aggro)

6x Frigate (Corpii Herald, Corpii Upholder, Corpii Seeker, Corpii Upholder) (Corpii Seeker sensor dampens)

Group 2: (40km)

6x Frigate (Corpii Herald, Corpii Upholder, Corpii Seeker, Corpii Upholder) (Corpii Seeker sensor dampens)

Group 3: (55km)

6x Frigate (Corpii Diviner, Corpii Seeker, Corpii Collector, Corpii Raider) (Corpii Seeker sensor dampens)
1x Blood Raider Personnel Transport Ship


Blitz
Complete when Group 3 is destroyed, grab militants from Transport wreck for turn in, don't need to touch Group 1 or 2.

Mineable Asteroids
12x Plagioclase, 7x Pyroxeres, 5x Veldspar

Structures
Many, nothing dropped loot (did not try couplings or junctions).

Hint
Killing the Transport Ship incurs a -4.0% standing loss with Blood Raiders.

Video
This video shows what you will be dealing with.
http://youtu.be/yLdWQiLc-ss
