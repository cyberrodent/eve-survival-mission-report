Intercept The Saboteurs, level 3


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Thu, 01 May 2014 13:01 UTC

Faction: Sansha Nation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace w/gate
Damage dealt: EM,Therm
Extras:
  • Web/scramble (Centii Loyal Minion)
  • Weapon Disruption (Centii Slave Hunter/Centii Savage/Centum Beast)
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ship classes: Battlecruiser, Recon, Faction Cruiser

Mission objective drops from the last group 4 destroyed ship.

Warp in


Single group

Aggro after a few sec
6x Frigate (Centii Slavehunter/Centii Savage)

Pocket 1

Attacking group 3 or 4 get aggro from 3 and 4 (except when pulling aggro at range; this way you can easily do it in a much smaller ship).
Drones don't cause any aggro but may aggro further ship themselves when out. Taking them back in dronebay doesn't lead the aggro on you, NPC ships just head back to their initial location even if very close from you, as long as you don't fire.

Group 1 (Cloven Grey Asteroid - 40km)

Aggro after 10 sec
3x Cruiser (Centum Ravager/Ravisher)

Last kill generate respawn
2x Cruiser (Centum Juggernaut/Beast)

Group 2 (Hollow asteroid - 60km)

No aggro from rest of room when attacking these. This group can take up to 5 minutes to spawn.
2x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Servant/Minion) Web/Scramble

Group 3 (Solar Harvester - 100km)

Aggro from group 4 when attacking these
5x Frigate (Centii Ravener/Centii Scavenger)
4x Cruiser (Centum Beast/Ravager/Ravisher/Juggernaut)

Spawn when one of the frigates is shot:
3x Frigate (Centii Savage/Slavehunter), don't aggro

Group 4 (Barren Asteroid - 105/110km)

Stage aggro when attacking these first
3x Tranport Ships
5x Frigate (Centii Savage/Slavehunter) Weapon disruption
2x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Servant/Minion)
2x Destroyer (Centior Abomination/Monster)
2x Cruiser (Centum Execrator/Slaughterer)

Killing elite frigates sometimes spawns
3x Frigate (Centii Savage/Slavehunter)

Group 5 (Elemental Base - 110/115km)

Can be left beside, doesn't aggro if not engaged (except if attacking group 4 first)
3x Frigate (Centii Savage/Slavehunter) Weapon Disruption
2x Destroyer (Centior Devourer/Cannibal)
2x Cruiser (Centum Juggernaut/Beast) Weapon Disruption

Random BS spawn

This spawn might spawn, but not always.
1x Battleship (Centus Lord/Slave Lord)

Blitz
Mission completed once last Group 4 ship is killed (drops the needed item), you do not have to kill other group.
See note above for aggressing Group 4 individually without triggering Group 3.

Standing
Killing one of the transporters incurs a 3.0% standing penalty

Mining
2409772 unit of veldspar, in about 40 rocks - 80 km
