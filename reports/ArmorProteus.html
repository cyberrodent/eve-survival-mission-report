Proteus


Role
  • Good close range dps with neutron blasters. Poor mid range dps if railgun fitted.
  • No sniper role (typical).
  • Mobile MWD gun platform.

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Certificate : High-Velocity Helmsman Improved
  • Medium Blaster Specialization 3
  • Medium Railgun Specialization 3
  • Advanced Weapons Upgrade 5
  • Gallente Engineering Systems 5
  • Gallente Offensive Systems 5
  • Gallente Electronic Systems 4
  • Gallente Defensive Systems 4
  • Gallente Propulsion Systems 5
  • Hybrid Weapon Rigging 4

Fitting Notes
  • Hybrid Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator rigs fitted to maximize short range damage.
  • Neutron blaster fitted.
  • MWD : Localized Injectors subsystem minimizes strain on capacitor.
  • Dissonic Encoding Platform subsystem maximizes gun tracking while moving or firing Null charges.
  • No armor rigs fitted to maximize microwarp speed.
  • Remaining rig calibration points used to fit a Targeting System Subcontroller to improve ship's targeting speed.
  • EM-Therm and Kin-Exp hull resistance profile are evenly balanced.
  • Typical rigs : Hybrid Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator / Hybrid Metastasis Adjuster or Ambit Extension / Anti-EM Pump / Anti-Explosive Pump / Targeting Sytem Subcontroller.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • MWD fitted (typical).
  • Neutron blaster fitted (typical).

Assault/HQ
  • MWD fitted (typical).
  • Neutron blaster fitted (typical).
  • Stasis Webifier can be exchanged with a Target Painter or Tracking Computer.
