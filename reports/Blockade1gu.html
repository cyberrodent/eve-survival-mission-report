Last edited by SinSmith
Thu, 20 Mar 2014 22:42 UTC

Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace w/o gate
Webbing: Starbase Stasis Tower
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kin
Completed in: Kestrel

Video: Corax

Single Pocket

Rats marked "TRIGGER" triggers the next spawn.
The spawns and triggers may vary, every spawn tend to have one "unique" rat like in this example, and it is most likely the trigger.

4x Frigates (Pithi Arrogator)

First Spawn

1x Frigate (Pithi Infiltrator) TRIGGER

Second Spawn

Distance: 100 km to 135 km
6x Frigates (Pithi Arrogator)
1x Frigate (Pithi Despoiler/Saboteur) TRIGGER

Third Spawn

8x Frigates (Pithi Imputor/Arrogator)
1x Frigate (Pithi Saboteur/Despoiler) Jamming TRIGGER

Fourth Spawn

4x Frigates (Pithi Saboteur/Despoiler) Jamming
1x Frigate (Pithi Plunderer) Completes the mission
