Delving into the Past, Level 2


Last edited by AleCium
Mon, 25 Apr 2011 21:44 UTC


(Part 1 of 3)


Faction: Amarr
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM/Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: EM, Thermal
Recommended ships classes: Cruiser (for fight), Shuttle (for quick-in/out)

Warp In

Warp into deadspace and recover "Old Star Map" 0.1 m³.

Single Group

Hostiles may spawn upon reaching "Cartographer's Quarters" to retrieve "Old Star Map".
2-3x Cruiser (Imperial Templar Methura/Agatho)

Asteroids:
  • Scordite ~300.000
  • Plagioclase ~300.000
  • Pyroxeres ~300.000
  • Veldspar ~1.800.000


Tips:
  • You do not need to destroy the hostiles in order to retrieve the map.

(Part 3 of 3)


Faction: Amarr
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gate
Damage dealt: EM/Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: EM, Thermal
Recommended ships classes: Cruiser (Gate allows T1 Cruiser or less)
Recommended generic setup: Long Range

Single Pocket

Destroy all hostiles.

Single Group

No Aggro
35-40 km
5x Frigate (Imperial Bahir/Sixtus)
4x Cruiser (Imperial Templar Methura/Agatho)

Asteroids:
  • Veldspar

Tips:
  • Amarr vessels are short range - try to stay out 25+ km from them.
