The Space Telescope, Level 3


Last edited by JoshThomas:
remove spam
Sat, 16 Aug 2014 11:22 UTC

Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kin, Therm
Recommended ship classes: Battlecruiser, Battleship

Single pocket

  • Four groups (ahead, up, left and right).
  • Groups spawn 5 to 8 seconds after warp in
  • Usually, all groups aggro a few moments after spawn
  • If all groups aggro, be prepared to warp out if you do not have substantial tank.

Group 1 (50-60 km, Delayed Aggro)

2x Frigate (Demolisher)
2x Cruiser (Eraser/Abolisher)

Group 2 (90-120 km, Delayed Aggro)

3x Cruiser (Ascriber/Silencer)
2x Sentry Guns

Group 3 (90-100 km, Delayed Aggro)

1x Destroyer (Terrorist)
1x Cruiser (Eraser)
2x Sentry Guns

Group 4 (80-90 km, Delayed Aggro)

2x Cruiser (Inferno/Mortifier)
2x Sentry Guns



Blitz
Destroy telescope and warp out.

Structures
Space Telescope: OBJECTIVE Several hundred Electronic Parts, Toxic Waste
Guristas Bunker: Random Ammo

Hint
The damage is pretty massive when you get the common, unlucky 'all aggro'.

-- DebirAchen (2011-01-28 20:53:15)
This mission is a bit more difficult than some level 3's because of the aggro from 3 sides and above on warp-in.

To do this mission I dropped the group by the telescope first. I killed the 2x frigates and then moved towards the group that is to the left from warp-in location, taking down the 2x cruisers from the telescope group while on the move left. I then killed the left group, then top, and by the time I finished those all, the group from the right was within range. Dropped those while moving to the telescope, staying to the left side of it to minimize damage from the right sentry group (they still hit but for less than half what the others did).

This was in a full T1 fit Drake:

7x Limos Heavy Launchers with Scourge

2x Large Shield Extenders
2x Thermal Shields (1 Active, 1 Passive)
2x Ballistic Shields (1 Active, 1 Passive)

2x Muon Coil Bolt Array
2x Shield Power Relays

3x Medium Core Defence Field Purger (not defense, Eve has c in place of s)

*I did have scout drones with me but never pulled them out for this mission.

Fairly inexpensive fit and quick to train the skills required. Shields never dropped below 60%, just remember to have those active resists running on warp-in. No where near being a difficult mission for a decently fit T1 passive tank Drake.

I could see a Blitz attempt being possible with my setup but the telescope does soak up some damage. The ships were all easy kills with decent bounty for the time spent, plus the loot and salvage that I came back for after hand-in.
