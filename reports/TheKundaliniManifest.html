The Kundalini Manifest

Last edited by FayceOff
Wed, 16 Feb 2011 17:49 UTC

Faction: Incursion Sansha's Nation
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Omni
Recommended ship classes: Battleships, Strategic Cruisers, Logistics Cruisers
Recommended generic setup: Battleships tanked with high resistances and 100k+ EHP. Cruisers with high resists and AB for speed/sig tanking.
Forces Required: 30 - 50 pilots

Incursion Sansha will auto aggress and switch targets

Military Intelligence:
Your primary Objective is to locate the Sansha's Nation flagship and destroy it. The destruction of this vessel will bring the local incursion to a halt.

Rewards:
- 90,000,000 ISK x ratio
- 20,000 CONCORD LP x ratio

Background Intelligence:
Synopsis from ISHAEKA-0107, DED Special Operations.
Authorized from Capsuleer dissemination.

Single Pocket


Wave 1

Mothership
1 x The Kundalini Manifest (ecm burst, neut, rr)
Battleships
1 x Deltole Tegmentum [neut, paint, scram]
3 x Intaki Colliculus [rr]
1 x Ostingele Tectum
1 x Outuni Mesen [neut, scram, web]
2 x Yulai Crus Cerebi [sniper]
Cruisers
2 x Arnon Epithalamus (ecm)
1 x Auga Hypophysis (scram, web)
5 x Mara Paleo [rr]
2 x Romi Thalamus
1 x Uitra Telen
1 x Vylade Dien [command ship]

containers:
1 x Logistics control array
1 x Sansha control relay

All of the support ships will respawn along with waves of frigates and 10 x Lirsautton Parichaya fighter bombers.

Strategy:
Ignore the support except for jamming ships and fighter-bombers. Fighter-bomber damage is extremely high, and it's best to warp out if you notice them yellow-boxing you.
