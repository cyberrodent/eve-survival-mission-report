Suntendi Recall, Level 1

Five parts.

Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:43 UTC

Part 1

Mission type: Courier

Part 2

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace without gates
Damage dealt: exp(20%)/Kin(40%)/therm(20%)/em(20%)
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: exp

Mine the Plagioclase asteroid for your agent

Single Pocket

Aggro when you target within 10k or start mining the one plagioclase roid in this area.
Mission journal flags as completed when the plagioclase roid is depleted, but you have to bring the ore back to your agent to complete the mission.
* Note* if you are dally, a second spawn of 2 frigates arrives after approx 10 min.

Distance: 42-47km from Warp-in.
2x Frigates (Gistii Hijacker, Gistii Rogue)

Part 3

Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace without gates

Pick up the scientist from the research station and return with him to your agent.
Station hits for 300 damage when you are within about 2000m.

Single Pocket

No aggro on Warp-In.

Caught the scientist and completed the mission objective without any opponents appearing.

Part 4

Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gate

Approach the Research Station to pick up the faulty Suntendi Virtu-Real Implants.

Single Pocket

No aggro on Warp-In.

Caught the implants and completed the mission objective without any opponents appearing.
Container with implants pops when you are approx 1500 m from station.

Part 5

Faction: Caldari State
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace without gates
Damage dealt: kin(64%)/therm(36%)
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: kin

Destroy the Virtu-Real Scrambling Beacon.

Single Pocket

All aggro on Warp-In.

Distance: 10-39km from Warp-in.
10x Frigates (2x State Showato, 6x State Bo-Hi, 1x State Nagasa, 1x State Shinai)
