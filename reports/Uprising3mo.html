The Uprising - The Disappearance (1/4)

Faction: Mordu
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace, single pocket
Damage dealt: Kinetic, Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic, Thermal (or EM on shield and Explosive on armor)
Recommended ships: Hurricane

Single pocket

3 groups, aggro from Group 1 at warp-in.

Group 1: (10-25km)

2x Mordu's Missile Battery
9x Frigates (Mordus Gladius/Katana/Sabre)

Group 2: (45km)

3x Cruisers (Mordus Puma)

Group 3: (65km)

3x Frigates (Mordus Gladius)
2x Cruisers (Mordus Bobcat/Lion)

The Uprising - Strike Them Where It Hurts (2/4)

Faction: Mordu
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace, single pocket
Damage dealt: Kinetic, Thermal
Web/scramble: Mordus Bounty Hunter
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic, Thermal (or EM on shield and Explosive on armor)
Recommended ships: Hurricane

Single Pocket

This mission has a total of 5 waves of respawns at close distance to warp-in. First wave might already have spawned when you arrive. All waves automatically aggro on you. Waves are triggered by time (ca 3 min), not kills. Take care of your drones!

Spawn 1:

2x Frigates (Mordus Cheetah/Katana)

Spawn 2:

10x Frigates (Mordus Gladius/Sabre)

Spawn 3:

6x Frigates (Mordus Katana/Sabre)

Spawn 4:

8x Frigates (Mordus Rapier)

Spawn 5:

1x Frigate (Mordus Katana)
1x Elite Frigate (Mordus Bounty Hunter) (Web/Scramble)

The Uprising - The Assault (3/4)

Faction: Mordu (+Militia)
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace, 3 pockets
Damage dealt: All kinds. Mordu = Kinetic/Thermal. Militia = EM/Explosive/Kinetic
Web/scramble: Mordus Bounty Hunter
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic, Thermal (or EM on shield and Explosive on armor)
Recommended ships: Hurricane

Pocket 1:

3 Groups, aggro from one of them.

Group 1: (6-12km)

5x Cruisers (Mordus Bobcat)

Group 2: (20km)

9x Frigates (Mordus Rapier/Gladius/Katana)
5x Cruisers (Mordus Leopard/Bobcat)

Group 3: (28km)

2x Elite Frigates (Mordus Bounty Hunter) (Web/Scramble)
2x Frigates (Mordus Gladuis)

Pocket 2:


Single Group: (20km) (Auto-Aggro)

1x Mordu's Light Missile Battery
1x Mordu's Heavy Missile Battery
4x Elite Frigate (Militia Guardian/Protector) (Web/Scramble)
2x Cruisers (Mordus Lion)

Pocket 3:


Single Group: (2-10km)

9x Elite Frigates (Militia Guardian/Leader) (Web/Scramble)

The Uprising - Last Stand (4/4)

Faction: Mordu (+Militia)
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal
Damage dealt: All. Mordu = Kinetic, Thermal. Militia = EM, Explosive, Kinetic
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic, Thermal (or EM on shield and Explosive on armor)
Recommended ships: Hurricane

Single Pocket

The Single Spawn is triggered by a kill, though time delayed respawn.

Single Group: (7-16km)

3x Mordu Sentry Gun
13x Elite Frigates (Militia Guardian/Leader/Protector) (Web/Scramble)

Single Spawn

6x Frigates (Mordus Katana)
2x Cruisers (Mordus Leopard)
