Faction: Sleepers

Webbers: Awakened Keeper, Emergent Sentinel, Emergent Keeper, Emergent Warden, Awakened Sentinel, Sleepless Sentinel

Recommended ship classes: Capital, Battleship, T3 (8+ Remote Repair Modules)
Recommended generic setup: Multi-range, Heavy Omni-tank, Remote Repair (Spider Tanking)

All sleepers will auto aggress; Sleepers will switch targets, drones and support ships are not immune.

-Marauder Insturctions

Initial Group

5x Frigates (Emergent Sentinel) [scram, web]
5x Cruisers (Awakened Keeper) [scram, web, nos] TRIGGER

Bookmark one of the debris and Warp-in so you position yourself between debris and the structures
1st group is easy. If you miss the spot, just hold the last trigger until you position yourself.



Reinforcements Wave 1

3x Frigates (Emergent Keeper) [rr, web]
3x Frigates (Emergent Warden) [scram, web, nos]
4x Cruisers (Awakened Keeper) [scram, web, nos] TRIGGER
2x Cruisers (Awakened Sentinel) [web]
4x Cruisers (Awakened Warden) [rr]

1st wave spawns nearby (15km). Keep holed up and shooting.



Reinforcements Wave 2

5x Frigates (Emergent Warden) [scram, web, nos]
2x Battleships (Sleepless Keeper) [nos] TRIGGER
1x Battleships (Sleepless Sentinel) [scram, web, nos]

Same with 2nd wave. Keep still, keep shooting.
Note though that Sleepless Keepers are going to pull range and they orbit around 45km-55km

If using a marauder/battleship with NOS, DO NOT kill the sleepless sentinel. Just keep it in low structure.
You are going to carry it as capacitor source as battleships on the next wave will pull range out of your NOS.




Reinforcements Wave 3

2x Cruisers (Awakened Keeper) [scram, nos]
3x Cruisers (Awakened Warden) [rr]
2x Battleships (Sleepless Keeper) [nos]
2x Battleships (Sleepless Warden) [rr]

Battleships will pull range and orbit you at 55-65km.
Snipe them as soon as they get on the field.




Structures
15x Broken Sleeper Data Bank (spawn container, data analyzer)
1x Abandoned Talocan Battleship (relic analyzer)
