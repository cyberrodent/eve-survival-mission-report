Rogue Drones
This large, hollow, asteroid would make an ideal location for a Rogue Drone Hive and that is something that this group of Rogue Drones seems all to aware of.
 

Faction: Rogue Drones
Location: High-Sec
Damage Dealt: All Types
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM(best), Therm(secondary)

Single Pocket

Auto-aggro from each wave. Last ship in each waves triggers next wave.

Initial Group

1-2x Frigate (Decimator/Infester/Raider/Render Alvi)

Wave 1

1-3x Frigate (Decimator/Render Alvi)

Wave 2

0-3x Frigate (Infester Alvi)
0-2x Destroyer (Shatter/Ripper Alvior)

Wave 3

1-2x Frigate (Decimator/Hunter/Raider/Silverfish Alvi)

Wave 4

2x Frigate (Raider/Silverfish/Sunder Alvi)
