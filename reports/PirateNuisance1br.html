Pirate Nuisance, level 1


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:38 UTC

Faction: Blood Raiders
Mission type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM, Therm
Reccomended damage dealing: EM, Therm

Single Pocket

It would be useful to take a webifier if you can't hit a fast drone near yourself without it. When you jump in Splinter Alvi is only about 5 km away from you and Aggro.
If you will destroy the Cargo Rig, you will find L and M charges for Hybrids and Projectiles and Light Missiles.

Single Group:

1x Splinter Alvi
1x Corpii Seeker (Frigate class)
1x Corpii Follower (Frigate class)
