Last edited by TechReviewsAndHelp

Faction: Rogue Drones
Mission type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM, Thermal
Web/Scramble: Strain Infester Alvi
Recommended damage dealing: EM
Recommended ships: Cruiser (Normal Ship Restrictions)
Recommended generic setup: Short Range DPS
Video: http://youtu.be/pGPfpBvgzE0


Pocket 1


Group 1 (15km) (Automatic aggro)

6x Alvi (Raider Alvi, Splinter Alvi, Infester Alvi)
  • May spawn the elite 'Strain Infester Alvi' which Scrams and Webs.

Group 2 (60km)

5x Alvi (Splinter Alvi, Render Alvi)

Pocket 2


Single Group (10km) (Automatic Aggro)

1x Wrecker Alvum

Blitz:
None, complete on all NPCs dead.

Mineable Asteroids
Pocket 1: 15x Veldspar
Pocket 2: 32x Veldspar

Structures
Pocket 1: Asteroid Mining Post, Dropped nothing
