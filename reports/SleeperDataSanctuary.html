Sleeper Data Sanctuary

Last edited by Jemjem787
Sun, 27 Apr 2014 20:38 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Cosmic Anomaly
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Omni
Webbers: Emergent Escort
Scramblers: None
Recommended ship classes: Soloable with Passive Drake and Dualrep Harbinger
Recommended generic setup: Omni Tank

As with all Sleeper combat anomalies there are 3 waves each which auto aggress.
Sleeper ships may target switch. Drones and support ships are not immune.
Any Times on Initial Group the 3 Frigates doesnt spawn

Single Pocket


Initial Group

3 x Sentry guns (Sirius)
2 x Cruisers (Awakened Escorts) TRIGGER (orbits at 45km)
3 x Frigates (Emergent Patroller) (Dont Always Spawn)

Reinforcement Wave 1

1 x Battleship (Sleepless Escort) TRIGGER
4 x Frigates (Emergent Escort) [web]

Reinforcement Wave 2

1 x Battleship (Sleepless Escort) TRIGGER

Reinforcement Wave 3

1 x Battleship (Sleepless Outguard) [nos]

Tips :
  • Waves 2 and 3 do not always spawn
  • The Sleepless Outguard does heavy damage, but it is possible to out-track it by orbiting close.
