A Case of Kidnapping, Level 4


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sun, 29 Jul 2012 16:10 UTC

Faction: EoM/Mercenaries
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space
Damage dealt: EoM - Kin/Therm | Mercenaries - All types (heavy Kin/Therm, EM, Exp)
Web/scramble: Frigates - EoM Saboteur, Mercenary Wingman
Extras: NA
Recommended damage dealing: EoM - Kin | Mercenaries - Therm
Recommended ships: CNR, Golem, Dominix

Note: Although it may appear that the use of acceleration gates lead to deadspace pockets, all pockets for this mission are actually in normal space.
MWD, fleet warp to member and individual bookmark for each location works without any restriction.

All acceleration gates for this mission are locked until you have eliminated all hostile ships.

Pocket Entrance (EoM)


Auto aggro at warp-in.

Single Group: (7-10km)

3x Battleships (EoM Ogre/Hydra)


Pocket 1 (EoM)


No aggro at warp-in.

Each group can be attacked independently without drawing pocket aggro.
Drones can be deployed normally.

Group 1: (16-18km)

4x Battleships (EoM Hydra)

Group 2: (22-26km)

4x Cruisers (EoM Black Priest)
2x Battleships (EoM Ogre)

Group 3: (28-32km)

4x Frigates (EoM Saboteur) Web/Scramble
2x Battleships (EoM Ogre)

Group 4: (38-42km)

6x Frigates (EoM Succubus)
5x Cruisers (EoM Crusader)


Pocket 2 (EoM)


No aggro at warp-in.

Each group can be attacked independently without drawing pocket aggro.
Drones can be deployed normally.

Reinforcement spawn is triggered after you have killed the last battleship in Group 2.

Group 1: (27-32km)

3x Battleships (EoM Ogre/Hydra/Death Lord)

Group 2: (34-44km)

6x Cruisers (EoM Prophet)
2x Battleships (EoM Hydra) Trigger (last battleship killed)

Reinforcement Spawn: (26-28km)

3x Battleships EoM Ogres


Pocket 3 (Mercenaries)


No aggro at warp-in.

Attacking Group 1 will aggro the entire pocket, attacking Group 3 you will get Aggro from Group 3 and Group 1.
Fleet members that warped in and attack will draw aggo from Group 2 if not previously engaged.

Attacking Group 2 will not aggro Group 3. Mercenary wingman warp scramble.

Group 1 (Auto-Aggro): (20-24km)

4x Battleships (Mercenary Overlord)

Group 2: (20-24km)

4x Cruisers (Mercenary Commander)
2x Battleships (Mercenary Overlord)

Group 3: (20-24km)

6x Cruisers (Mercenary Commander)
4x Frigates (Mercenary Wingman) Web/Scramble


Pocket 4 (EoM)


Auto aggro from Group 1 & 2 at warp-in.

Group 1: (54km)

1x Battleship (EoM Behemoth)

Group 2: (10-24km)

4x Tower Sentry Drone II (EoM Sentry Gun)
2x Tower Sentry Drone III (EoM Sentry Gun)

Tip:
  • Spacial interference prevents the use of stasis webifiers or "other items of its type" on the EoM Behemoth.
  • Misson is flagged completed after you have killed the EoM Behemoth battleship. Don't forget to retrieve the dropped VIPs passengers.

Blitz:
  • None.
