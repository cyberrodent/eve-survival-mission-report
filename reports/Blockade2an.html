The Blockade, level 2


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 05:38 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Unknown
Damage Dealt: Exp/Kin
Extras: Unknown
Web/Scramble: Possible from Arch ships
Recommended damage dealing: Exp

Single Pocket


  • 5 spawns of enemies.
  • The next spawn is triggered by killing a specific ship in the current spawn. Usually it's the unique cruiser.
  • Aggro from every spawn.
  • Spawns and triggers may be different from mission to mission.

Initialspawn: (10km from BM)

1x Starbase Stasis Tower
4x Frigates (Gistii Ambushers/Impalers)
5x Destroyers (Gistior Defacers/Shatterers) (trigger)

Following are examples of the 3 next spawns:

Example 1: (100km from BM)

3x Cruiser (Gistum Predators)
1x Cruiser (Gistum Depredator) - trigger for next wave
6x Destoryers (Gistior Shatterers/Defacers)
6x Frigates (Gistii Ambushers/Nomads)

Example 2: (100km from BM)

1x Cruiser (Gistum Depredator) - trigger for next wave
6x Destroyers (Gistior Defacers/Haunters)
4x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Ruffian) - may web/scram

Example 3: (120km from BM)

1x Cruiser (Gistum Depredator) - trigger for final wave
2x Destroyers (Gistior Shatterers)
4x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Ambushers) - may web/warp scramble
3x Frigates (Gistii Impalers)

Example 4: (150km from BM)

3 Groups.

2x Frigates (Gistii Ruffian)
2x Destroyers (Gistior Defacer)
2x Cruisers (Gistum Predator)

3x Frigates (Gistii Impaler)
3x Destroyers (Gistior Shatterer)
1x Cruisers (Gistum Predator)
Last ship remaining is trigger

Example 5: (150km from BM)

2 Groups.

3x Destroyers (Gistior Defiler)
3x Interceptors (Arch Gistii Nomad) (Web/scramble)

4x Destroyers (Gistior Shatterer)
1x Cruiser (Gistum Depredator) (Trigger)

Example 6:

2 Groups

2x Destroyers (Gistior Defacer)

3x Interceptor (Arch Gistii Raider) (Web/scramble)
4x Frigates (Gistii Impaler)
1x Cruiser (Gistum Predator) (trigger)

Fifth and Final Spawn:

Example 1: (35km from BM)

1x Cruiser (Gistum Phalanx) - End boss
3x Destroyers (Gistior Seizers)
4x Frigates (Gistii Raiders)

Example 2:


3x Frigates (Gistii Impaler)
4x Destroyers (Gistior Trasher)
1x Cruiser (Gistum Centurion)
