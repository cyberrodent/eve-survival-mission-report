Honor, Level 5


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:15 UTC

Faction: Blood Raiders
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with acceleration gates
Damage dealt: EM/Therm
Extras: Energy Neut Towers, Stasis Towers, NOS from battleships and T2 cruisers.
Web/scramble: T2 frigates(Elder Corpi prefix) have a chance to web/scramble.
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ships: Passive shield tanked ships(eg: Drake, Nighthawk, etc)

Pocket 1


Everything agros on warp in.

2x Blood Energy Neutralizer Sentry III
4x Blood Cruise Missile Battery
1x Tower Sentry Bloodraider III
1x Blood Stasis Tower
11x Battleships (Corpus Archbishop/Patriarch/Pope)
2x Heavy Assault Ships (Elder Corpum Arch Sage)
3x Battlecruisers (Corpior Exorcist)

Pocket 2


Note: This pocket contains the recruitment station that must be destroyed to complete the mission.

Group 1


Everything agros on warp in.

8x Blood Cruise Missile Battery
4x Blood Heavy Missile Battery
5x Blood Energy Neutralizer Sentry II
10x Battleships (Corpus Archbishop/Patriarch/Pope/Harbinger)
2x Heavy Assault Ships (Elder Corpum Sage)
4x Battlecruisers (Corpior Exorcist)
6x Assault Ships (Elder Corpii Collector/Reaver)

Group 2 - Spawns after killing the last Corpus Harbinger


4x Battleships (Corpus Cardinal/Pope)

Group 3 - Spawns after killing the last Corpus Patriarch


4x Battleships (Corpus Patriarch)
3x Cruisers (Corpum Arch Priest)

Group 4 - Spawns after killing the last Corpus Pope


4x Battleships (Corpus Patriarch)
4x Assault Ships (Elder Corpii Reaver)
