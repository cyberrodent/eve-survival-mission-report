Hidden Stash, level 1


Last edited by ShintuFelper:
Added info
Wed, 04 Feb 2015 15:56 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace with gate
Damage dealt: Exp, Kin
Recommended damage dealing: Exp, Kin

Single pocket


Two groups of enemies mashed together, Group 1 auto aggros. Group 2 will attack drones and aggros based upon range from warehouse.

Group 1

8x Frigate (Gistii Hijacker)

Group 2

4x Frigate (Gistii Hijacker)

Warehouse and Habitation Module drop trade goods. Pressure Silo(high hp) and Cargo Rig drop ammo.

About 20 veldspar rocks, unknown how much they contain.
