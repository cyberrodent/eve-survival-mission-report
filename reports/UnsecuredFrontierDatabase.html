Unsecured Frontier Database

Last edited by ArkiNats
Sat, 07 Sep 2013 12:07 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Data
Space type: Normal
Damage dealt: (EM/The 37%, Kin/Exp 13%)
Recommended ship classes: RR Battleships, Duel X-Large Ancill Battleship, T3 Cruisers, Kitchen Sink + Logi (4+ Remote Repair Modules)
Recommended generic setup: Heavy Em/The Tank

All sleepers will auto aggress; Sleepers will switch targets, drones and support ships are not immune.

Single Pocket


Initial Group

3 x Cruisers (Awakened Defender)
3 x Frigates (Emergent Preserver) [scram, web, neut] TRIGGER
Max Incoming DPS: 264 (EM/The 24%, Kin/Exp 26%)

Reinforcement Wave 1

4 x Cruisers (Awakened Upholder) [web, neut]
4 x Cruisers (Awakened Defender) TRIGGER
Max Incoming DPS: 392 (EM/The 29%, Kin/Exp 21%)

Reinforcement Wave 2

1 x Battleship (Sleepless Upholder) [neut] TRIGGER
3 x Cruisers (Awakened Upholder) [web, neut]
3 x Frigates (Emergent Defender) [web]
Max Incoming DPS: 628 (EM/The 40%, Kin/Exp 10%)

Reinforcement Wave 3

2 x Battleships (Sleepless Upholder) [neut]
3 x Cruisers (Awakened Upholder) [web, neut]
2 x Frigates (Emergent Preserver) [scram, web]
Max Incoming DPS: 1050 (EM/The 42%, Kin/Exp 8%)

Structures
10x Deformed Sleeper Databanks (spawn container, codebreaker)
