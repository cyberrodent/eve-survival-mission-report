Faction: Angel Cartel
Location: Low Sec/Nullsec
Damage Dealt: Exp/Kin
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended Damage Dealing: Exp(best), Kin(secondary)

Single Pocket

Auto-aggro from each wave. Last ship of specified type in each waves triggers next wave.

Initial Group

3x Elite Frigate (Arch Gistii Hunter/Impaler)
4x Battlecruiser (Gistatis Legionnaire/Primus) Trigger
2x Angel Heavy Missile Battery
2x Tower Sentry Angel


Wave 1

3x Elite Frigate (Arch Gistii Ambusher/Impaler)
2x Battlecruiser (Gistatis Legatus) Trigger

Wave 2

4x Destroyer (Gistior Defiler/Hunter/Seizer/Thrasher)
7x Battlecruiser (Gistatis Legatus/Legionnaire/Praefectus/Primus)
4x Battleship (Gist Malakim/Throne) Trigger

Wave 3

3x Battlecruiser (Gistatis Primus)
3x Battleship (Gist War General/Warlord)
