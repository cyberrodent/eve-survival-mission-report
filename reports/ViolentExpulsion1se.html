Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace w/o gate
Damage dealt: Kinetic/Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic

Single Pocket

The rats will spawn one and one at a time, with 1-2 seconds in between. The Coreli Agent spawns 5-7 seconds after the others.

First Spawn: (25km) (Automatic aggro)

6x Frigates (Coreli Initiate/Servant/Agent)

Killing the 4x Coreli Initiate will spawn:

Second Spawn: (25km)

3x Frigates (Coreli Scout/Agent/Initiate)

Blitz:
Killing the first Coreli Agent completed the mission.
