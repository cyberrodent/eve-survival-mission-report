Solar Cell

Last edited by SylvaniumOrlenard:
Added Capacitor Neutralization Data
Mon, 08 Sep 2014 18:46 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Cosmic Anomaly
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: (EM/The 22%, Kin/Exp 28%)
Recommended ship classes: Battlecruiser, Battleship
Recommended generic setup: Heavy Omni-tank (Kin/Exp Favored)
Blue Loot Payout: 29.5 Million ISK (37 Million isk with additional spawn)

All sleepers will auto aggress; Sleepers will switch targets, drones and support ships are not immune.

Single Pocket


Initial Group

1 x Cruiser (Awakened Upholder) [web, neut]
1 x Frigate (Emergent Preserver) [scram, web, neut] TRIGGER
[1 x Battleship (Sleepless Defender) [web] (possible additional spawn)]
Max Incoming DPS: 74 (EM/The 28%, Kin/Exp 22%
With Additional Spawn: 396 (EM/The 25%, Kin/Exp 25%)
Max Capacitor Neutralized : 9 GJ/s

Reinforcement Wave 1

1 x Cruiser (Awakened Upholder) [web, neut]
3 x Cruisers (Awakened Defender)
2 x Frigates (Emergent Defender) [web]
2 x Frigates (Emergent Upholder) [rr, neut] TRIGGER
Max Incoming DPS: 294 (EM/The 25%, Kin/Exp 25%)
Max Capacitor Neutralized : 12 GJ/s

Reinforcement Wave 2

1 x Battleship (Sleepless Preserver) [rr]
1 x Battleship (Sleepless Defender) [web]
2 x Cruisers (Awakened Defender)
Max Incoming DPS: 690 (EM/The 19%, Kin/Exp 31%)
