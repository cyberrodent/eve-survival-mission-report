Mordus Headhunters, level 2


Last edited by EveKat
Thu, 06 Aug 2015 16:46 UTC

Faction: Mordus
Mission type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kinetic, Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic
Video: http://youtu.be/b1OQlidtHp8

Entry

Ships have short range and No Web/Scramble.

Group: (25-30km)

5x Mordus Rookie (Frigate)

Pocket 1


Group 1: (15-20km) Auto Aggro

4x Mordus Sabre (Frigate)
2x Mordus Gladius (Frigate)

Group 2: (30-35km)

6x Mordus Sabre (Frigate)
1x Mordus Cheetah (Cruiser)
1x Mordus Gladius (Frigate)

Group 3: (60-70km)

4x Mordus Sabre (Frigate)
2x Mordus Katana (Frigate)


Blitz
Take out Group 2

Video
http://youtu.be/b1OQlidtHp8
I blitz it in the video by taking out the cruiser.

Asteroids

16 Veldspar Rocks. Total of 119,424 m3

Entry:
Ships are at 25-30km range

Pocket 1:
Group 1 is at 30-40KM
Group 2 is at 20-25km
Group 3 is at 60-70km

Note that Group 2 does not appear at the same time as the other groups. You can hold fire 1 until Group 2 appears as none of the groups agro until you shoot at them.

Rating: Very easy if you use long range weapons. All ships have short range weapons and no web/scramble.
