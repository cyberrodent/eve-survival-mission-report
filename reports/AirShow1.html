Air Show!, Level 1, Gallente


Part 1 of 5

Faction: None
Mission type: Courier
Space type: Normal space


Part 2 of 5

Faction:
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: None
Extras: Must use a shuttle

Might need to use a bit of manual flying to get through the hollow asteroid (beacons 1, 4 and 7)
8 beacons in total
Tip: Make sure that beacons are visible in your overview.

Part 3 of 5

Faction:
Mission type: Encounter / Courier
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: None
Extras: Must use Shuttle

Warp-in, move to the damaged pod, pick-up the stranded pilot, and go.


Part 4 of 5

Faction: Rogue Drones
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: em(46%)/therm(54%)
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ship classes:

No aggro on Warp-In.

Group 1 to 4

Distance: 30-37km from Warp-in.

You have to shoot at one foe to get aggro of the group it is in.
Every group consists of 3x frigates (Mammon Apis/Belphegor Apis/Infester Alvi)

Group 5

This group appeared when i have killed 2 groups.
Instant aggro.
Mission objective is completed on killing group 5.

Distance: ~ 4km.

2x Frigates (Splinter Alvi)

Part 5 of 5

Faction:
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space (mwd does work)
Damage dealt: None
