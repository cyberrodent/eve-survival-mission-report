The Sansha Spies, Level 4


Last edited by IcareAboutOre:
Added Bounty info
Thu, 26 Dec 2013 13:06 UTC

Faction: Sansha Nation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM, Therm
Web/scramble: NONE
Extras: NA
Recommended damage dealing: EM (best), Therm (secondary)
Recommended ships: CNR, Raven, Abaddon, Apocalypse

(Warp Gate)

Single Pocket


Auto aggro from Group 1 or 2 at warp-in. All other groups remain neutral.

Attacking Group 1 OR 2 does not aggro the whole pocket.
Drones can be deployed as per normal.


Group 1 (Auto-aggro): (36-42km)

1x Tower Sentry Sansha I
4x Tower Sentry Sansha II

Group 2: Sansha Lookout (70-75km)

4x Destroyers (Centior Devourer/Abomination)
4x Cruisers (Centum Hellhound/Fiend)
2x Battleships (Centus Savage Lord/Mutant Lord)

Group 3: Sansha Headquarters (90-95km)

1x Battleship (Centus Dark Lord/Overlord)
3x Battlecruisers (Sansha's Spy - Centatis Devil/Wraith)

Tip
  • Mission is flagged completed once you have eliminated all ships from Group 3.

Blitz
  • Eliminate Group 3 and warp out.

Bounties
2,929,000.00


Standing
Killing the ships incurs a -3.0% standing hit with Sansha's Nation
Killed Group 3 and received no standings penalty, not even for ship kills (Dec 2011, Crucible).
