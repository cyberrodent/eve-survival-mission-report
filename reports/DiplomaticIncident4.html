-- ForteEXE (2010-01-08 19:09:52)
To be clear, I did not have the option of warping to the beacon at range. I forgot to test if warping to a bookmark would work.

The mission is serious about destroying the entire Amarr fleet in the mission. I destroyed the frigates first, then battleships, then cruisers. Only once the last cruiser was destroyed did I get credit for completing the mission.

The prisoners that you need to turn in for the mission drop from the last Amarr ship destroyed, whichever that happens to be.

Amarr standing losses for destroying their ships in this mission aren't too bad. I incurred a total of about -0.098% standing loss with the Amarr Empire for "Combat - Ship Kill". This is in addition to (but very small, compared to) the "Derived Modification" loss you get for doing a Storyline Mission for the Minmatar. Faction-balancers who have the ships needed to do the mission can accept this mission without worrying. For comparison, I gained +8.4% with the Minmatar Republic for the mission and lost -3.7212% with the Amarr Empire for "Derived Modification". (Be aware that when the TRANSACTION LOG for faction standings says "Derived Modification" that the percentage change is an absolute percentage of 10.0, not the typical percentage change of (extreme you are moving towards - your current standing.)

DIPLOMATIC INCIDENT 01/Oct/2012
Warp to x is not available.
However, form your own fleet, then use 'Warp squad to location within (50km)' this works nicely.
My Rattler always has plenty of tank, but for this mission I was never below 94% until after all Bs were dead and I turned around.
I used 1xInvuln,
1xEm amp(passive)
1x Therm hardener(active).
EM 78% THERM 84% (worth noting passive recharge of 58hp/s also)
Missiles and drones are +1 since tracking disruption is heavy for this mish.
For drones I used some (disposable) Ogre I's as i wasn't sure how drone aggro would work out.
There seems to be none whatsoever, I'll use sentries next time or some ogre II's.
Maintain range directly away from the fleet until Martyr and all BS are gone per previous notes.
Once you have aggro, bring in the Noctis...those tags are the only reason I can think of to run this mission at all so beat the Ninja's!!!

Tags and armour plates:15M at least.
