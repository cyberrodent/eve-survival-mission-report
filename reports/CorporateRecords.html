Corporate Records, Level 1


Last edited by BuilderAlphaOne:

Fri, 21 Sep 2012 13:08 UTC


Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kinetic/Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic
Recommended ship class: Frigate
Recommended generic setup: DPS w/ Afterburner
Special Ship Restrictions: T1 Cruiser, T1 Destroyer, T1/T2 Frigate, Rookie Ship, Shuttle

Warp In

Warp In is 38km from first gate
  • No spawns, asteroids, or structures

Pocket 1

Gate leaves you 28km from Cargo Container with objective

Group 1 (20km) (Auto Aggro)

7x Frigate (Pithi Invader, Pithi Infiltrator, Pithi Arrogator)


Blitz [Pocket 2]
Once through gate, fly to and loot cargo container and leave.

There are 5x frigates and 2x destroyers, but you need NOT engage them if you are fast enough [hence the AB].


Mineable Asteroids
9x Veldspar
9x Scordite
9x Pyroxeres

Structures
None

Hint
An afterburner really helps on this mission
