Drone Assembly
You observe the remnants of a small outpost, probably used by smugglers or perhaps it was a small mining colony. All that remains now are dust and echoes.
 

Faction: Rogue Drones
Location: High-Sec
Damage Dealt: All Types
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM(best), Therm(secondary)

Single Pocket

Auto-aggro from each wave. Last ship in each waves triggers next wave.

Initial Group

1x Frigate (Splinter Alvi)

Wave 1

2x Frigate (Devilfish Alvi)
1x Destroyer (Ripper/Shatter Alvior)

Wave 2

1-2x Frigate (Devilfish/Hunter Alvi)
1x Destroyer (Predator/Shredder Alvior)

Wave 3

3x Frigate (Barracuda Alvi)
2-3x Destroyer (Shatter Alvior)

Wave 4

2-3x Frigate (Devilfish Alvi)
2-3x Destroyer (Shredder Alvior)

Bonus Wave

0-1x Commander Destroyer (Sentient Shredder Alvior)--Drops rogue drone parts
