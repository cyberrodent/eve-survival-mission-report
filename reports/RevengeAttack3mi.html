Revenge Attack, Level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 08:03 UTC


Part 2 of 2


This mission is the second part of a storyline. Part 1 is Trespassers.

Faction: Minmatar
Mission type: Encounter
Damage dealt: Exp/Kin/Therm/EM
Web/scram: Republic Fleet C-2 Support Frigate
Recommended damage dealing: Therm/Exp
Recommended ships: Completed in a Cyclone

Pocket 1


4x Minmatar Heavy Missile Battery
3x frigates (Republic Fleet C-2 Support Frigate)
1x frigate (Republic Fleet Elite Soldier)
3x frigates (Republic Fleet Soldier)
3x cruisers (Republic Fleet Rupture)
4x cruisers (Republic Fleet Stabber)
1x cruiser (Republic Fleet Officer)

The enemies in Pocket 1 are divided into three groups, each about 60km away from the warp-in point. The gate to the second pocket is 67km away.

The Republic Fleet Officer appears as a frigate but has cruiser stats.

Pocket 2


3x Tower Sentry Minmatar II
2x frigates (Republic Fleet C-2 Support Frigate)
1x cruiser (Republic Fleet Raid Leader)
2x cruiser (Republic Fleet Rupture)
1x cruiser (Republic Fleet Stabber)

Enemies are about 30km away from the warp-in point.
