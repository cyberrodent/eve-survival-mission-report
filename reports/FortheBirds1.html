For the Birds, Level 1


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:27 UTC


Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM/Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: EM
Recommended ship classes: Frigate
Special Ship Restrictions: Shuttle, Rookie Ship, T1/T2 Frigate, or Destroyer - Only

Warp in

You see a Gallentean Terrorist but he disappears before you can aggro.

Pocket 1


Group 1 (20km) (Auto Aggro)
3x Gallentean Guard (Types: Dragonfly Apis/Incubus Apis)

Group 2 (30km)
3x Gallentean Guard (Types: Dragonfly Apis/Scorpionfly Apis/Belphegor Apis)

Mineable Asteroids
None
Structures
3x Cathedral Ruins, lots of hp, drop nothing


Pocket 2


Group 1 (<15km) (Auto Aggro)
5x Gallentean Guard (Types: Dragonfly Apis/Scorpionfly Apis/Belphegor Apis)

Mineable Asteroids
None
Structures
Ruined Bunker, drops random ammo
Diplomat (Type: Meat Popsicle), drops nothing
2x Deserted Battery, drops nothing


Pocket 3

Damage dealt: Kinetic/Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic

Group 1
1x Gallentean Terrorist (Cruiser)
  • Drops Cargo Container with Achuran White Song Birds (0.0m3) Objective

Mineable Asteroids
None
Structures
Blasted Neon Sign, drops nothing


Tips
  • Gallentean Guards all have very low hp, but do a considerable amount of damage
  • Gallentean Guards are all various Apis drones, and do EM/Thermal. No drone loot
  • Gallentean Guards do not incur any standing loss
  • The Cruiser in the final pocket results in a -2.4% standing loss with Gallente
