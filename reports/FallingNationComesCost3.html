Falling the nation comes at a cost, level 3


Faction: Sansha
Mission Type: encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage Dealt: em/therm
Recommended damage dealing: em/them
Recommended ships: Harbinger, HAC, Drake

Single pocket


x3 Renegade Sansha's Slaver (30-40km)

Just warp in, kill the 3 ships and the mission is flagged completed.

Side note: I believe this mission changes the NPC with the faction you are doing the mission. I did this mission with True power corp (sansha) and I got sanshas. so if you do this mission with amarr you will get Renegade Amarr's Slaver, and so on.
