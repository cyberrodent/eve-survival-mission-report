Epic Arc Overview

Agent Name: Nilf Abruskur
Agent Corporation: Republic Security Services
Agent Location: Aldrat System at RSS Liaison Headquarters (Celestial Beacon)
Additional Requirements: Hacking III and Archaeology III (skill), Data Analyzer and Relic Analyzer (module)

Important note:
* Your standing is only checked by the starter agent. After starting the epic arc, subsequent agents will not check standings.
* This means while running the epic arc, you can continue doing missions even if your standing drops below +5.00 yet remains above -2.00 Faction standing.

Chapter 2 - Songs of the Past

Go to Chapter 1: The Passage
Go to Chapter 3: Revelation

Mission 1 - Dead End Intercept

Faction: Minmatar Republic
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Recommended Damage Dealing: Explosive

Mission Objective:
  • Retrieve 1x Singed Datapad (0.1 m3) and return to Agent.

Initial Spawn (65km)

1x Lomar Vujik - Minmatar Battleship (Republic Tribal Kinai)

Tip
  • 1x Singed Datapad (0.1 m3) spawns after Lomar Vujik is destroyed.


Mission 2 - Surfacing

Faction: Mercenaries
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Damage Dealt: All
Web/Scram: Mercenary Wingman, Spider Drone I

Mission Objective:
  • Retrieve 1x Encrypted Transmission (0.1 m3) from Tili's Brothel and return to Agent.

Initial Spawn (5 - 20km)

2x Frigate (Mercenary Wingman) Web/Scram
4x Cruiser (Mercenary Commander)
5x Battleship (Mercenary Overlord) Trigger

Note
  • Trigger is last Mercenary Overlord destroyed.

Wave 2 (5-20km)

2x Frigate (Mercenary Wingman) Web/Scram
5x Cruiser (Mercenary Commander)
4x Battleship (Mercenary Overlord) Trigger

Note
  • Trigger is last Mercenary Overlord destroyed.

Wave 3 (5-20km)

5x Frigate (Mercenary Elite Fighter)
2x Elite Frigate (Mercenary Wingman) Web/Scram
3x Spider Drone I (50km Range) Web
4x Battleship (Mercenary Overlord)

Blitz
  • Mission completes after last Battleship is killed. Blitzing will require you burn through all the BS's. Kill frigates if necessary to warp out.

Tips
  • Mercenary ships will deal a mix of all damage types.
  • Mercenary ships spawn at 20km range after 1x Encrypted Transmission (0.1 m3) is removed from Tili's Brothel.
  • Recommend short range heavy weapons for Battleships, long range light weapons or Drones for Cruisers and Frigates.


Mission 3 - Who Art in Heaven

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Damage Dealt: Explosive
Web/Scram: Elite Frigate

Mission Objective:
  • Meet Historians at Archaeological Site.

Initial Spawn

2 groups - Instant full room aggro.

Group 1 (20-40K)
7x Frigate (Arch Gistii Hunter, Arch Gistii Impaler)
4x Cruiser (Arch Gistum Breaker, Arch Gistum Defeater)
7x Battleship (Gist Cherubim, Gist Seraphim, Gist Throne)

Group 2 (70-90K)
8x Frigate (Gistii Hunter, Gistii Impaler)
3x Elite Frigate (Arch Gistii Hunter) Web/Scram
3x Battlecruiser (Gistatis Praefectus)

Tips
  • Outpost is destroyed when ship is within 20km.
  • Group 2 will despawn after Outpost is destroyed.
  • Mission is completed when Agent sends message to abort.
  • To collect all available NPC bounty, warp in at 100km and maintain distance from Outpost.

Blitz
  • Ignore NPC's and quickly approach within 20km of the Outpost to trigger its destruction. Warp out when Agent sends message to abort mission.


Mission 4 - Playing All Their Cards

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Recommended Damage Dealing : Explosive / Kinetic
Requires: Hacking III (minimum) and Data Analyzer module.

Mission Objective:
  • Retrieve 1x Drive Cluster EDF-285 (0.1 m3) and return to Agent.

Note
  • Destroying Angel ships is not required to complete mission.

Blitz
  • Fit maximum tank and your data analyzer. Rush to the container and hack it, loot it. GTFO. Not an easy hack, but doable.
  • If using a T3, fit the Emergent Locus Analyzer subsystem (bonus for hacking). Helps wonders.

Initial Spawn (100km)
11x Frigate (Arch Gistii Hijacker, Arch Gistii Hunter, Arch Gistii Impaler, Arch Gistii Rogue, Arch Gistii Thug) Web/Scram Hijacker/Rogue/Thug
6x Destroyer (Gistor Seizor, Gistor Trasher)
4x Cruiser (Arch Gistum Phalanx, Arch Gistum Defeater, Arch Gistum Centurion)
10x Battleship (Gist Cherubim, Gist Seraphim, Gist Throne)

Tips
  • Data Analyzer and Hacking level III is required to open the container.
  • Spawn occurs when ship is within 5km of container.
  • Only the EDF-285 Drive Cluster Item is required to complete the mission.
  • The Angel Battlestation has large amount of Damage Points and drops no loot.


Mission 5 - History In The Making

Mission Type: Travel

Mission Objective:
  • Report to new Agent - Hiva Shesha - located in Tanoo System at Krusual Mobile Library (Celestial Beacon).

Note
  • Starting conversation with Hiva Shesha completes the mission objective.
