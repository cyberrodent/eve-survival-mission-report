Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Extras: Jamming (Pithi Despoiler/Pithi Plunderer)
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/Therm
Recommended ships: Harbinger

Blitz: Kill Group 2 (far group).

Single Pocket

Drones may get aggro from group 2.

Group 1: (40-50km) Instant aggro

4x Frigates (Pithi Wrecker /Pithi Plunderer)
2x Cruisers (Pithum Mortifier /Pithum Inferno)

Group 2: (100-110km)

3x Frigates (Pithi Saboteur /Pithi Despoiler) Jamming
1x Battlecruiser (Pithatis Assaulter)
