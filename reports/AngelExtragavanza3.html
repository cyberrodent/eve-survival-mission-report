Angel Extravaganza, Level 3


Last edited by AmnesiaC:
Added Bounty, Loot, and Salvage info
Sat, 21 May 2016 02:38 UTC


Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Exp, Kin, (Exp, Therm in bonus pocket)
Extras: Drone aggro from respawns
Web/scramble: Angel Viper, Angel Webifier, Spider Drone
Recommended damage dealing: Exp, Kin
Recommended ships: Dominix, Drake, Myrmidon, Hurricane
Video: Cerberus, http://youtu.be/TfVUUoEUHdc

Groups are now triggered spawns, usually the highest value singleton. Drones will get aggro from new spawns. Waves auto aggro you and/or drones.

No hostiles at initial gate

Blitz
Destroy all structures before destroying the first wave unlocks the gates without any further waves. This works for Pockets 1 to 4.
Bring an afterburner as some structures are 80+ km from warpin.
Mission is completed after the named BC and the two Spider Drones in Pocket 5 are destroyed.


Pocket 1


Wave 1

75-80km
4x Cruisers (Gistum Breaker/Defeater/Liquidator/Marauder) Trigger for Wave 2 when killed is highest bounty

Wave 2

15-20km
4x Destroyers (Gistior Defiler/Seizer)
1x Cruiser (Gistum Depredator/Predator) Trigger for Wave 3 when killed

45-65km
1x Elite Frigate (Angel Viper/Webifier) web/scram
2x Cruisers (Gistum Centurion/Phalanx)

Blitz
Destroy the Angel Cartel Structure and Small Refinery before killing the trigger and there will be no wave 2 and 3 spawned

Structures
No loot

Wave 5

125-140km
2x Spider Drones web/scram

Structures
No loot
Total Bounty around 2.8 mil

Bonus Pocket (optional)

Needs Angel Palladium tag to enter, not consumed.

You warp in roughly 75-90km from the rats, which all aggro immediately except for the sentry, presumably because it's out of range. Angel Vipers/Webifier close in pretty fast, other frigs are a little slower. There are no triggers or respawns reported.

1x Tower Sentry Angel III
4x Elite Frigates (Angel Viper/Webifier) web/scram
2x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Ambusher/Raider) web/scram
4x Battleships (Gist Nephilim/Saint/Warlord) target painting


Structures
Battlestation drops loot. Be prepared to shoot a long time. (The loot can be generic and unimpressive, and generally not worth it. At 200dps it will take about ten minutes. The explosion, on the other hand, is gigantic.)

Terzam's Stash drops random generic loot as well

Loot
Loot: ~5,300,000 isk
Bounties: ~5,300,000 isk
Salvage: ~2,500,000 isk


Tip
Watch out for heavy webbing/scrambling in the bonus stage.
Saint uses Bane torpedoes [explo.] AND some shorter range [45 km?] weapon with thermal damage.
Nephilim does a fair amount of explosive damage using Bane torpedoes
Warlord does a fair amount of explosive damage using Bane torpedoes
