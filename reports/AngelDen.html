Faction: Angel Cartel
Location: High-Sec
Damage Dealt: Exp/Kin
Web/Scramble: None
Extras: Target Painting Gistii Ruffian
Recommended Damage Dealing: Exp(best), Kin(secondary)

Single Pocket

Auto-aggro from each wave. Two sets of spawns, one triggered by ship destuction and the second triggered by Sentry Tower destruction.

Initial Group

6x Frigate (Gistii Hunter/Ruffian) Target Painting Ruffian
4x Destroyer (Gistior Trasher)
2x Sentry (Angel Light Missile Battery)

Ship Wave 1

3x Frigate (Gistii Hijacker)

Sentry Wave 1

2x Sentry (Angel Light Missile Battery)

Sentry Wave 2

2x Frigate (Gistii Ambusher)
2x Destroyer (Gistior Trasher)

Sentry Wave 3

4x Frigate (Gistii Nomad)
1x Faction Cruiser (Domination Predator) Optional Spawn
