Intercept The Saboteurs, level 4


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Sun, 04 May 2014 09:01 UTC

Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace w/gate
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm
Web/scramble: Dire Pithi
Extras: Jamming from Annihilator/Nullifiers
Recommended damage dealing: Kin
Recommended ship classes: Battleship, Command Ship, HAC, Strategic Cruiser.
Video: Paladin


Spawns may vary, this is just an example.


Warp-In

(Acceleration Gate)
3x Frigates (Pithi Destructor/Demolisher)
2x Destroyers (Pithior Guerilla/Terrorist)


Single Pocket

Attacking group 2a aggros entire pocket.
Attacking groups 1 or 3 will usually aggro that group and group 2a.
Drones might be aggroed by neutral groups due to proximity.
Attacking an elite frigate in group 2b or group 4 will only aggro that group, which can be sniped at leisure.

Confiscated Viral Agent is dropped by the last defeated ship in group 2a (mission completion trigger).


Picture to make it clear where which group is located



Group 1 (51 - 82km)

2x Destroyers (Pithior Guerilla/Terrorist)
3x Cruisers (Pithum Annihilator) (Jamming)
3x Battlecruisers (Pithatis Assaulter/Assassin)
1x Battleship (Pith Eradicator)

Group 2a (55 - 75km)

3x Guristas Transport Ship
5x Frigates (Pithi Demolisher/Destructor/Guerilla) (Web/Scramble)
3x Cruisers (Pithum Mortifier/Inferno)
2x Battlecruisers (Pithatis Assassin/Assaulter/Enforcer)
1x Battleship (Pith Eliminator/Eradicator/Extinguisher) (Eliminator Jamming)

Group 2b (55 - 75km)

3x Elite Frigate(Dire Pithi Infiltrator/Invader) (Web/Scramble)
3x Frigates (Pithi Demolisher/Destructor)

Group 3 (45 - 65km)

2x Destroyers (Pithior Terrorist/Guerilla)
3x Cruisers (Pithum Mortifier/Inferno)
2x Battlecruisers (Pithatis Assaulter/Enforcer)
1x Battleship (Pith Obliterator/Eradicator)

Group 4 (55 - 75km)

3x Elite Frigates (Dire Pithi Imputor/Arrogator/Invader/Infiltrator) (Web/Scramble)
3x Frigate (Demolisher/Destructor)

Armory may drop T1 Ammo
Habitation Modules may drop Trade Goods

Blitz

Kill ships at entry room, use gate.
Kill all ships in group 2a, collect viral agent, warp out. (Note: This method will trigger full room aggro)

Mining
Nothing here

Bounties
3,245,442 ISK

Standing hits
Killing one of the transports incurs a 2.4% standing penalty.
