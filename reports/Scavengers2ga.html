Scavengers, Level 2


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 05:59 UTC

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Scavenge
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Thermal/Kinetic
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Explosion
Recommended ship classes: Elite Frigate, Cruiser, Battlecruiser
Recommended generic setup: Heavy Tank, Salvager
Required Equipment: Mission requires a Salvager module

Hint
  • There are about 50 wrecks at warp in, with wreck icons in overview.
  • The objective appears with a can icon in overview.
  • You must successfully salvage the can to open it.

Warp In


Group 1 (10-20km) (Auto Aggro)

4x Frigates (Mercenary Rookie)
1x Cruiser (Mercenary Commander)

Group 2

Spawned after 30 seconds
6x Frigate
2x Cruiser


Blitz
Find the Reconnaissance Ship Wreck, Salvage it to access it, Take High-Tech Data Chip, warp out

Mineable Asteroids
None

Structures
None

Hint
Sends player to low security to salvage, ignoring the '5 jump' rule. Beware pirates.

Completed in an overtanked Buzzard. Note - no gate; MWD works.
Blitz: Cloak fitted to get past losec PC's (only saw a couple, but a lot of NPC's at gates); MWD or cloak up to target, salvage it, open it, grab datacore and run. Shield barely troubled, even after group 2 spawned.

Probably doable in a standard Kestrel or Condor, properly tanked (although have to watch for PC's if not blitzing). Note that, 16 jumps from home, bringing in your salvager is probably not an option.

Mission itself complete in 5 mins, but 16 jumps (3 regions, 5 constellations) to & from target, plus proper losec caution - this isn't a fast mission.
