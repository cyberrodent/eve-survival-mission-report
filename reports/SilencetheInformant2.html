Silence the Informant, level 2


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Thu, 22 Jan 2015 23:57 UTC

Faction: Mercenaries/Rogue Drones.
Mission type: Encounter.
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Thermal
Recommended ships: Frigate, Cruiser (Normal Ship Restrictions)
Recommended generic setup: Mixed Range DPS
Mission: Take out former secret agent. (Pocket 3)
Remote Complete: No
Video: http://youtu.be/jZjCnwlgQas

Pocket 1

Faction: Rogue Drones

Group 1 (15Km) (Automatic Aggro)

4x Frigates (Decimator/Render/Sunder Alvi)

Group 2 (15km)

4x Frigates (Decimator/Render/Sunder Alvi)
  • Spawns within 10-20 seconds.

Pocket 2:

Faction: Mercenaries.

Group 1 (20km) (Automatic Aggro)

2x Frigates (Mercenary Elite Fighter)
  • On entering pocket, receive transmission, and group 2 and 3 spawn.

Group 2 (55km)

2x Frigates (Mercenary Elite Fighter)

Group 3 (100km)

3x Frigates (Mercenary Elite Fighter)

Group 4 (55km)

2x Frigates (Mercenary Elite Fighters)

Pocket 3:

Faction: Mercenaries.

Single Group (20km)

Tower will aggro upon entry. Mission frigate will have delayed aggro.
1x Tower Sentry Drone I
1x Frigate (Named Informant)

Blitz
Destroy everything in pocket 1 to advance, then pocket 2, then only need objective in pocket 3

Mineable Asteroids
Pocket 1 and 3: None
Pocket 2: 4x Scordite, 6x Veldspar

Structures
Pocket 1: Battery, Bunker: Bunker may drop random refineables
Pocket 2: None
Pocket 3: Scanner Post, Mobile Residence: Residence can drop implants

Video
http://youtu.be/jZjCnwlgQas


Tips:
  • AB is very useful in pocket 2 for slow, short range ships
  • Mobile Residence is listed as Deadspace Overseer's Structure in Overview
  • T1 Drones work great in this mission. Just make sure to set them on agressive.

Rewards and Bonus
Reward: 175,000 ISK 253 LP
Bonus: 136,000 if you complete it in 3 hours and 10 minutes.

Bounty
Render Alvi: 4,500 ISK
Sunder Alvi: 6,000 ISK
Decimator Alvi: 4,875 ISK
Mercenary Elite Fighters: 10,000 ISK
Tower Sentry Drone I: 30,000 ISK
Mission Generic: 5,000 ISK (tends to drop an implant)

Time
Blitz:(Same time as no blitz)
No Blitz: About 3:12 minutes
