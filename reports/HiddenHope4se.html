Hidden Hope, Level 4 (Storyline)


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sun, 29 Jul 2012 16:50 UTC


Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates (MWD does not work)
Damage dealt: Therm/Kin
Extras: No EW
Web/Scramble: N/A
Recommended Damage Dealing: Kin/Therm
Recommended Ship Classes: Battlecruiser/Battleship/HAC
Recommended Generic Setup: Normal LVL 4 Tank
Completed in: Maelstrom

Pocket #1


There are a number of SoE transports upon warp-in. Serpenti attackers show up shortly after and aggro.
Respawns appear to arrive when all ships are destroyed.
*Respawn aggro-ed drones

Initial Spawn:

4x Corelatis Platoon Leader - Cruiser
4x Core Port Admiral - Battleship

Spawn #1

1x Core port admiral - Battleship
2x Core Defender - Frigate

10km to gate.

Pocket #2


Serpenti appear shortly after warp-in and aggro.
Respawns appear to arrive when all ships are destroyed.
*Respawns aggro-ed drones

Initial Spawn

3x Core Chief Infantry - Cruiser
3x Core Port Admiral - Battleship

Spawn #1

3x Coreli Safeguard - Elite Frigate
2x Core Rear Admiral - Battleship

Spawn #2

2x Corelum Chief Defender - Cruiser
3x Core Rear Admiral - Battleship


Tips:

*There are many Gallente structures and SoE ships. I did not attack them because I am protective of my standings.
