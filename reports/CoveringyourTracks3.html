Covering your Tracks, level 3


Last edited by ElemWiz
Mon, 05 May 2014 13:31 UTC

Faction: Mercenaries
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal (bookmark works)
Damage dealt: All kinds.
Web/scramble: Mercenary Wingman, Zerak Cheryn
Recommended damage dealing: Thermal
Recommended ships: Onyx

This is a hybrid mission, meaning you will warp to a beacon, but there's no deadspace affecting your mwd, nor will there be a gate.

Single Pocket


Single Group


Aggro from entire group from warp-in point.

2x Frigate (Mercenary Wingman)
2x Frigate (Mercenary Elite Fighter)
1x Cruiser (Mercenary Lieutenant)
1x Cruiser (Ytari Niaga, Zerak Cheryn or Kuran "Scarface" Lonan)

The last rat killed will drop 1 unit of Special Delivery that is required to be brought back to agent to complete the mission. None of the NPCs in the entire mission respawn.

The beacon is 15km from the group of rats, meaning you can try to warp in at 100km getting some distance. But the rats are very fast, and will swarm you in a few seconds.

Note: Cruiser (Ytari Niaga, Zerak Cheryn or Kuran "Scarface" Lonan) have a small chance to drop fraction module ( Dread Guristas Co-Processor from Ytari Niaga) or implant

Structures

The Casino will drop 20 units each of two random types of commodity or perhaps tourists.

ISK

Bounty : ~270,000 ISK
Loot : ~327,000 ISK (EVE estimated, salvage included)

Standing

2.4% standing penalty with opposing faction to agent.

Mining

None.
