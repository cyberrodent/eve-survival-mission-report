Escaped Terrorist, Level 1


Last edited by DanteVonKarma
Tue, 09 Oct 2012 06:26 UTC

Faction: Caldari
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Extras: none
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/therm
Recommended ships: Incursus


Killing the Caldari ships in the first pocket (which is required to unlock the gate) will net you
a -2.4% with Caldari State which makes this mission completely unworthy to get and complete.

Pocket 1


No aggro on warp in. Several Caldari frigates. Short ranged, two with rockets or light missiles.

All Structures in Pocket 1 have ammunition, trade goods, metal scraps, except the power generator.


Pocket 2


Few more Caldari frigates with Telos Varshan, the terrorist in a gallente frigate, Atron class.

Silo in the pocket drops Metal Scraps, Trade Goods.

Items with goods: Starbase Silo,
