Intercept the Saboteurs, level 4


Last edited by EveSurvival
Tue, 29 Oct 2013 16:16 UTC

Mission type: Deadspace
Faction: Minmatar
Damage dealt: Exp, Therm, Kin
Recommended damage: Exp
Contains webbers and possibly scramblers.
Completed in standard Raven (2x T2 exp and 2x T2 therm hardeners, X-Large booster) DR: Easy/Medium
Video: Paladin


Pocket 1


Single Group

5x Frigates

Pocket 2


Group

Distance: 40-70km
3x Battleships
16x Battlecruisers
21x Frigates (6x Chief Republic Frigates may web & scramble)
3x Transport Ships

Rats at roughly 40-70km away. No aggro on warp-in. Attacking anything but a frigate aggros more or less everything. Attacking a frigate only aggros all the frigs. The Chief Republic frigs web you and may scramble according to EVE-info although this did not happen to me. With webbers/scramblers killed, the rest should be easy as the BCs and BSs dont do too much damage unless very close

Standing hits
Killing one of the transporters incurs a 2.4% standing penalty
