Faia Arashi and FaiaTaka were both electronically incarnated in City of Heroes, the difference being that in CoH FaiaTaka is the primary. Taka goes even further back to the late 80s under Champions rules when we played with pencil/paper/dice.

My eventual goal is to fly all of the ships of EVE, as of Apr 24 2010 I have 32 of them. Sadly the wallet has not kept pace with skills.

For missions Arashi flies a Drake while Taka uses a Hurricane for "cleanup". For mining Arashi does the grinding in a Hulk and Taka hauls it away in an Orca.
