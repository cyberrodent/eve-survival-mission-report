Reclamation, Level 5


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:19 UTC

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Thermal/Kinetic
Extras: Neut, Web, Scramble, Damps, NOS
Web/scramble: Elite Frigates
Recommended damage dealing: Thermal
Recommended ships: Passive shield tanked ships(eg: Drake, Nighthawk, etc)

Single Pocket

Everything agroes automaticly, but some agroes faster than others. Also, 10 ships able to Web/Scramble, so be aware. Also heavy cap drain.
The ships listed under Spawn will spawn over time, or when you kill rats. And they can agro mates/drones.

Single Group

Tower Sentry Gallente III
2x Gallente Energy Neutralizer Sentry III
12x Elite Frigates (Elite Federation Libertus, Elite Federation Manica) (Web/Scramble)
10x Destroyers (Federation Praktor Phalarica, Federation Praktor Machina)
4x Elite Cruisers (Elite Federation Quadrieris)
6x Battlecruisers (Federation Praktor Arx) (Damps)
7x Battleships (Federation Praktor Hyperion)

3x Federation Praktor Auxilia (Damps)

Spawn:

3x Tower Sentry Gallente III
Gallente Energy Neutralizer Sentry III
6x Elite Cruisers (Elite Federation Arx, Elite Federation Auxilia) (Damps)
6x Battleships (Federation Praktor Hexeris, Federation Praktor Hyperion, Federation Praktor Dionia, Federation Praktor Polemo) (NOS from Dionia)
