Serpentis Forlorn Hideaway


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Fri, 04 Jul 2014 14:45 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Location: High-Sec
Damage Dealt: Therm/Kin
Web/Scramble:None
Extras: Sensor Damping Coreli Watchman
Recommended Damage Dealing: Kin(best), Therm(secondary)

Single Pocket

Auto-aggro from each wave. Last ship in each waves triggers next wave. Waves spawn at Abandoned Asteroid Station, 40km from warp-in.

Initial Group

5x Frigate (Coreli Patroller/Watchman) Damp Watchman

Wave 1

4x Frigate (Coreli Patroller/Watchman) Damp Watchman
1x Crusier (Corelum Chief Scout)

Wave 2

2x Destroyer (Corelior Soldier / Trooper)
3x Crusier (Corelum Chief Spy)

Wave 3

5x Crusier (Corelum Chief Scout / Spy)
4x Destroyer (Corelior Artillery)
