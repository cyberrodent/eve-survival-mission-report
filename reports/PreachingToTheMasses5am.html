Preaching to the Masses, Level 5

Last edited by KreaTurs:
# neut respawns was unclear; tested and added once
Tue, 01 Jul 2014 11:37 UTC

Faction: Amarr
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with Gate
Web/Scramble: Stasis Towers, All Frigates
Extras: Amarr Energy Neutralizer Sentry IIIs, Tracking Disrupter
Damage dealt: EM 58% / Therm 42%
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ships: (1) Maelstrom w/ Passive Tank and (1) Maelstrom w/ Shield Transporters

Acceleration Gate into Deadspace

Single Pocket

Ships 35-85km away. Immediate aggro

Initial Group



10 x Frigates (Divine Imperial Nabih/Paladin/Valok/Bahir*) orbits of 4-16km, Web/Scramble, *25% Tracking Disrupter, Trigger
12 x Battleships (Imperial Templar Dominator/Martyr/Ultara) orbits of 17-49km
4 x Tower Sentry Amarr III
5 x Amarr Cruise Missile Batteries
2 x Amarr Stasis Towers
2 x Amarr Energy Neutralizer Sentry IIIs (Both respawn once!)


Spawns

Destroying Frigates spawns:

2 x Frigates (Divine Imperial Imran) orbits at 4km
2 x Frigates (Divine Imperial Napat) orbits at 10km, 30% Tracking Disrupter
3 x Cruisers (Divine Imperial Wrath/Tamir) orbits of 7-19km, NOS, 40% Tracking Disrupter

Destroy Broadcast Station (150,000hp)

Tips
  • Use a Passive Tank and save time by not having to destroy Energy Neutralizer Sentries which re-spawn themselves if destroyed
  • Or plan a capacitor that receives an additional 80 units/s
  • Maximum damage dealing of initial group is 1981 hp/s (EM 58%, Therm 42%)
  • Mission flagged complete when the Broadcast Station is destroyed
