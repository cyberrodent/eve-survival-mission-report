Retribution, level 3


Last edited by CirimCollective:
structure intel
Sun, 06 Mar 2016 12:33 UTC

Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Extras: Jamming (Pithum Murderer)
Web/scramble: Webifiers/Kyoukan
Reccomended damage dealing: Kinetic
Difficulty: Easy

Single Pocket

No aggro upon warp-in.

Group 1 (25-30km - Closest Rock Formation)

4x cruisers (Pithum Killer/Pithum Murderer) Jamming

Group 2 (35-40km - Slave Worker Facility)

5x cruisers (Pithum Ascriber/Pithum Silencer)

Group 3 (35-40km - Spaceshuttle Wreck)

3x cruisers (Pithum Inferno/Pithum Mortifier)

Group 4 (65-75km - Furthest Rock Formation)

3x Elite Frigate (Webifier/Kyoukan) Web/scram
1x Battlecruiser (Pithatis Enforcer)

Structures
Large Collidable Structures are very tricky to select without being on overview.
The small armory drops ammo.

Blitz
Destroy outpost (70k from warpin) and ride into the sunset.
