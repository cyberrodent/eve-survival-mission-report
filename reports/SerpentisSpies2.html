Serpentis Spies, level 2


Last edited by TechReviewsAndHelp:
Didn't know the rewards and bonus change
Tue, 15 Jan 2013 23:56 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Therm, Kinetic
Recommended damage dealing: Thermal
Recommended ships: Frigate/Cruiser
Recommended generic setup: Fast, Short Range DPS
Mission: Kill Serpentis Spies, and report back to the agent. (You can not remote complete)
Remote Complete: No

Single Pocket


Group 1 (20KM) (Automatic Aggro)

Many Serpentis Initiate
1x Coreli Agent (Frigate)
3x Coreli Initiate (Frigate)
1x Coreli Safeguard (Frigate)

Group 2 (70KM)

3x Serpentis Spy (Frigate)

Blitz
Fly past structure, should see spies spawn ahead, kill them.
(Update 1/15/13)
Many people has reported that it's not impossible to blitz this mission by going to the structure.

Mineable Asteroids
4x Scordite

Structures
Serpentis Lookout: No loot
Radio Telescope: No loot
Serpentis Bunker: Sometimes the Serpentis Bunker drops a container.

Tips
Just send out T1 drones with light gun fire, and align with the stargate you need to use when you're done with this mission.

LP
111 LP


Bounty
Coreli Agent: 4,875 ISK
Coreli Initiate: 3,000 ISK
Coreli Safeguard: 7,875 ISK
Serpentis Spy: 8,250 ISK

Time
2-5minutes
