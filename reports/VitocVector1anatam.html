Vitoc Vector - The Pickup (1 of 4)

Mission type: Courier

Vitoc Vector - Earning Their Trust (2 of 4)

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: unknown w/o gates
Damage dealt: exp(43%), kin(39%), therm(17%)
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: exp

Bring the hacked data chip to your agent.
Make sure you fit out the Civilian Codebreaker that your agent gave you before you leave the station. You may have to run it for a few cycles before you find anything on the wrecked ship.

Single Pocket

Approach the "Wrecked Science Vessel" which is 33km from Warp In. It's a "Spawn Container" and You have to hack it to get the mission item "Data Chip".
There were 16 Veldspar roids.

Group 1

This group is appearing when You are 3km away from the "Wrecked Science Vessel".
Immediate Aggro.
Distance: 19km
2x Frigates (1x Gistior Defacer, 1x Gistior Shatterer)

Vitoc Vector - Mounting a Defense (3 of 4)

Faction: Ammatar Mandate
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: unknown with gates
Damage dealt: EM(38%), kin(31%), therm(31%)
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: EM or therm

Destroy all the Ammatar attackers.

Pocket 1

8 Veldspar roids present.
Group appearance is triggered by time.
Acceleration Gate seems not to be locked.

Group 1

Aggro on Warp-in.
Distance: 53-54km from Warp-in.
4x Frigates (Ammatar Navy Executioner)

Group 2

Aggro on Warp-in.
Distance: 53-54km from Warp-in.
3x Frigates (Ammatar Navy Scout)

Group 3

Aggro on Warp-in.
Distance: 53km from Warp-in.
3x Frigates (Ammatar Navy Inquisitor)

Pocket 2

No aggro on Warp In.

Group 1

Distance: 40-41km from Warp-in.
2x Frigates (Ammatar Navy Inquisitor)

Group 2

Distance: 56-61km from Warp-in.
5x frigates (Ammatar Navy Executioner)

Group 3

Distance: 56-61km from Warp-in.
5x frigates (Ammatar Navy Executioner)

Group 4

This Group is appearing if Group 1 is killed.
1x frigates (Ammatar Navy Inquisitor)

Group 5

This Group is appearing if Group 2 is killed.
3x frigates (Ammatar Navy Executioner)

Group 6

This Group is appearing if Group 5 is killed.
6x frigates (Ammatar Navy Executioner)
If this Group is killed, You get aggro from all remaining groups.

Vitoc Vector - A Terrible Thing (4 of 4)

Faction: Ammar Empire
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: unknown with gates
Damage dealt: EM(52%), therm(48%)
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: EM

Destroy the Amarr Breeding Facility and return the enslaved Matari to your agent.

Pocket 1

6 Veldspar roids here.
All Groups aggro immediately on appearance.

Group 1

Distance: 13-17km from Warp-in.
8x Frigates (3x Imperial Disciple, 5x Imperial Haran)

Group 2

Appears when Group 1 is killed
2x Cruiser (1x Imperial Mathura, 1x Imperial Muzakir)

Pocket 2

12 Veldspar roids here.

Group 1

Aggro on Warp in.
Distance: 23-25km from Warp-in.
5x Frigates (2x Imperial Forian, 3x Imperial Matendi)

Group 2

Gives aggro when You approach the acceleration gate at around 11km.
1x Cruiser (Imperial Mathura)

Group 3

Is appearing when Group 1 is killed. Aggro.
Distance: 22-24km from Warp-in.
8x Frigates (3x Imperial Disciple, 5x Imperial Haran)

Group 4

Is appearing when Group 2 is killed.
Distance: 22-24km from Warp-in.
2x Frigates (Imperial Sixtus)

Pocket 3

To accomplish this mission destroy one or more of the 4 Ammarian Breeding Facilities until one of them drops Breeder Slaves. Bring them to the agent.

Group 1

Aggro on Warp in.
Distance: 23-25km from Warp-in.
4x Frigates named "Guard Drones" (2x Scorpionfly Apis, 1x Dragonfly Apis, 1x Incubus Apis)

Group 2

5x Frigate (4x Imperial Matendi, 1x Imperial Forian)

Group 3

Distance: 22-24km from Warp-in.
1x Cruiser (Imperial Muzakir)

Tip:
* Warning: Limit your enthusiasm. Trying to rescue more than the requested number of slaves may result in a HUGE fine.
