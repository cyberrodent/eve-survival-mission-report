Core Citadel, Class 6

Last edited by Markj2
Wed, 20 Jan 2016 18:25 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Cosmic Anomaly
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Omni (Missiles 50% Kin, 50% Exp, Lasers 3:2 Em/Th)
Extras: Neut, Stasis Webify, Warp Scramble
Webbers: Emergent Sentinel, Emergent Warden, Awakened Keeper, Sleepless Sentinel
Scramblers: Emergent Sentinel, Emergent Warden, Awakened Keeper, Sleepless Sentinel
Recommended ship classes: Capital, Battleship, T3 Webber (7+ Remote Repair Modules or 3 Capital RR Modules)
Recommended generic setup: Multi-range, Heavy Omni-tank
Blue loot: 450.2 Mill ISK / 1035.4 Mill ISK (Cap + Site)

All sleepers will auto aggress; Sleepers will switch targets, drones and support ships are not immune.

Capital escalation waves spawn between 60 and 65km directly above the initial warp to 0, show sensor overlay to see the initial warp to 0 location.
