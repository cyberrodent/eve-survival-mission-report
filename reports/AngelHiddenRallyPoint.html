Angel Hidden Rally Point


Last edited by ShintuFelper:
Fixed typo, cleaned, formatted
Fri, 04 Jul 2014 15:09 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel
Location: Nullsec
Damage Dealt: Exp/Kin
Web/Scramble: Arch Gisti Hijacker
Extras: Target Painting Arch Gisti Nomad & Gist Nephilim/Saint
Recommended Damage Dealing: Exp(best), Kin(secondary)

Single Pocket

2 Large rock formations in a small cloud, all waves spawn around or behind this area. Warp in is around 50k away below and in front.
Aggro on warp in.

NOTE: All waves aggress upon spawning, unless greater than 120km away. Aggro can be managed this way if you accidentally blow a trigger.

Initial Group

5x Destroyers (Gistior Defiler/Seizer)
6x Cruisers (Gistum Liquidator/Maurader) Trigger
3x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Legatus/Primus/Tribuni)
3x Battleships (Gist Cherubim/Seraphim)

Wave 1

3x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Hijacker) Scram & Web
4x Cruisers (Gistum Liquidator/Marauder)
7x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Praefectus/Tribunus)
4x Battleships (Gist Commander/General)

Next wave spawns when last battlecruiser or cruiser (whichever is last) is destroyed

Wave 2

6x Cruisers (Gistum Centurion/Phalanx)
7x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Legatus/Tribunis) Trigger

Wave 3

3x Elite Frigates (Arch Gistii Nomad) Target Painting
4x Elite Cruisers (Arch Gistum Liquidator/Marauder) Trigger
5x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Legatus/Tribunis)
2x Battleships (Gist Nephilim/Saint) Target Painting

Wave 4

2x Elite Cruisers (Arch Gistum Centurion/Phalanx)
8x Battleships (Gist Cherubim/Throne)
