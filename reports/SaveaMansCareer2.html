Save a Mans Career, Level 2


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Thu, 22 Jan 2015 23:36 UTC

Faction: Rogue Drones, Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (mwd does not work)
Damage dealt: Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Thermal
Recommended ship classes: Frigate
Recommended generic setup: Close Combat
Mission: Kill Sangrel Minn, and take the reports (0.1M). The reports will appear next to his wreck in a cargo container. Take back the reports to the agent.

Warp-In


Mission Info Popup:
This is Sangrel Minn's hideout, which is located in the midst of an area
of space infested by rogue drones. Few consider it a coincidence that
whenever Sangrel is chased into this area, the pursuers are immediately
set up on by a multitude of drone ships.

Group 1: (14km) (Automatic Aggro)

4x Frigate (Arachula/Asmodeus/Beezebub/Mammon/Malphas Apis) Trigger when all killed

Group 2: (20km)

2x Frigate (Alvi Splinter/Render)

Group 3: (20km)

1x Frigate (Sangrel Minn)

Spawn 1 (Automatic Aggro)

1-2x Destroyer (Alvior Shatter)
1-2x Frigate (Alvi Barracuda/Hunter)

Blitz
Kill Sangrel Minn, loot the Reports from cargo container dropped next to his wreck, warp out.

Mineable Asteroids
None

Structures
Cargo Rig - Random Ammo

Hint
Drone aggro here, make sure to keep your drones out of the way.
This can be done with T1 Drones. (I didn't lose any of mine)

Rewards and Bonus
Reward: 55,000 ISK and 95 LP
Bonus: 72,000 ISK for getting it done within 1 hour and 12 minutes

Bounty
Total: 67,563 ISK

Time
Blitz: about 2 min
No Blitz: about 10
