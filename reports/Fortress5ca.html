The Fortress, Level 5

Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:59 UTC

Faction: Caldari
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal Space. No Gate
Web/Scramble: All Frigates
Extras: Caldari Energy Neutralizer Sentry IIIs, ECM Jammer
Damage dealt: Kin 85% / Therm 15%
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/Therm
Recommended ships: (1) Maelstrom w/ Passive Tank and (1) Maelstrom w/ Shield Transporters

Direct warp into Normal Space. No Gate

Warp-In

Ships 45-50km from warp-in unless you select to warp to a different distance. All ships aggro at warp-in



8 x Frigates (Taibu State Nagasa/Shinai) orbits at 3k, Web/Scramble
3 x Battlecruisers (State Shukuro Bajo/Samurai) orbits at 23k
9 x Battleships (State Shukuro Bishamon/Daijo/Shogun) orbits at 33 – 35km
4 x Tower Sentry Caldari III
5 x Caldari Cruise Missile Batteries
7 x Caldari Energy Neutralizer Sentry IIIs


Capital Ship Maintenance Array Spawns:


When the Maintenance Array is reduced to 40% Shields there spawns
10 x Fighters (State Fighter) Capital Ship Maintenance Guards

When the Maintenance Array is reduced to 0% Shields, there spawns
4 x Frigates (Taibu State Nagasa) orbits at 3k, Web/Scramble
6 x Cruisers (Taibu State Daimyo/Shugo) orbits at 17km, 35% ECM Jammers
8 x Battleships (State Shukuro Shogun) orbits at 33km
4 x Tower Sentry Caldari III

When the Maintenance Array is reduced to 50% Hull there spawns
10 x Fighters (State Fighter) Capital Ship Maintenance Guards


Tips
  • Use a Passive Tank and save time by not having to destroy Energy Neutralizer Sentries
  • Maximum damage dealing of initial groups are 1,695hp/s (Kin 85%, Therm 15%)
  • Mission flagged complete Maintenance Array is destroyed, whether all ships are destroyed or not
