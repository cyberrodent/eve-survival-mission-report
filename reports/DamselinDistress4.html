The Damsel In Distress, Level 4


Last edited by AceEightySix
Sat, 16 Apr 2016 18:42 UTC

Faction: Mercenaries
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space (aka MWD works)
Damage dealt: All types. Therm(~63%), Kin(~24%), Exp(~6%), EM(~7%)
Web/scramble: Spider Drones web
Extras: NA
Recommended damage dealing: Therm (best all round), Kin (secondary)
Recommended ship class: Battleship, Battlecruiser
Video: Ishtar, Tengu, Golem, Machariel, Bastion KronosCNR


Single Pocket


The Damsel is in Kruul's Pleasure Garden located the farthest away from the warp in Beacon.

WARNING: When you destroy Kruul's Pleasure Garden it will emit a blast with a radius of 30-40KM that inflicts 3000 Explosive Damage to ship and drones.

Initial Group (Auto-aggro) (25-35km)

2x Frigates (Kruul's Henchmen)
4x Cruisers (Pleasure Hub Guardian - Mercenary Commander/Corporal)
3x Battleships (Pleasure Hub Sentinel - Mercenary Overlord)
1x Cruiser (Kruul) Trigger

Spawn 1 (Auto-aggro)

(Triggered When Kruul is Attacked)
6x Battleships (Pleasure Hub Sentinel - Mercenary Overlord)

Spawn 2 (Auto-aggro)

(Triggered when Kruul's Pleasure Garden is damaged to ~50% armor remaining)
2x Spider Drone I Web
4x Cruisers (Pleasure Hub Guardian - Mercenary Corporal)
4x Battleships (Pleasure Hub Sentinel - Mercenary Overlord)

Spawn 3 (Auto-aggro)

(Triggered when Kruul's Pleasure Garden is damaged to ~40% hull remaining)
5x Frigates (Seven Death Dealer)
3x Cruisers (Seven Assassin)
1x Battleship (Zor) (always drops a junk implant, but there is a chance for a second better implant to drop as well)

Structures
  • Kruul's Casino drops NPC trade goods/passengers.
  • No other structures drop loot.

Blitz
  • Destroy Kruul's Pleasure Garden furthest from warp-in, loot Damsel, warp out.
  • Beware significant damage from spawns.

Isk
Bounty : 4,956,000 isk
