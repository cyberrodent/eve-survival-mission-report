Forgotten Perimeter Power Array

Last edited by Markj2
Wed, 20 Jan 2016 17:30 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Magnetometric
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Omni
Webbers: None
Scramblers: None
Recommended ship classes: BC (Drake)
Recommended generic setup: NA
Blue Loot: 9.2 Mill

All sleepers will auto aggress; Sleepers will switch targets, drones and support ships are not immune.

Single Pocket


Initial Group

2 x Cruisers (Awakened Patroller) TRIGGER
Max Incoming DPS: 80 (EM/The 25%, Kin/Exp 25%)

Reinforcement Wave 1

2 x Cruisers (Awakened Escort) TRIGGER
3 x Frigates (Emergent Watchman) [nos]
Max Incoming DPS: 145 (EM/The 33%, Kin/Exp 17%) Max Nos: -9

Reinforcement Wave 2

2 x Cruisers (Awakened Escort)
2 x Cruisers (Awakened Watchman) [nos]
Max Incoming DPS: 168 (EM/The 38%, Kin/Exp 12%) Max Nos: -12

Tips:
  • 4 containers and abandoned talocan, relic analyzer module required to open.
  • You may ignore and tank the trigger ship in Initial Group and then analyze/salvage all containers.
  • Keep in mind however, that doing each container could also (randomly) spawn additional ships.
