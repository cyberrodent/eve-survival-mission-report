Epic Arc Overview

Starter Agent: Arsten Takalo
Agent Corporation: Brutor Tribe
Agent Location: Frarn System at Brutor Tribe Community Area (Celestial Beacon)
Standings Required: +5.00 Minmatar Republic (Faction) or +5.00 Brutor Tribe (Corporation) AND >-2.0 Minmatar Republic (Faction)
Additional Requirements: Hacking III and Archaeology III (skill), Data Analyzer and Relic Analyzer (module)
Recommended Ships: Shield Battleship: T1 Faction (Machariel, Raven Navy, Tempest Fleet, Scorpion Navy, etc) or Shield Marauder (Vargur or Golem are better for this), all with MicroJumpDrive, mobile depot and propulsion module in inventory.


Blitz:
  • A Tengu with perma-tank (Pithum-A booster, deadspace resists) is the ship of choice because of the many missions where you have to hack/relic containers. Its innate PVE ability and the Emergent Locus Analyzer subsystem which buffs Hacking/Relic skills, along its very good tank makes it possible you to hack/relic objectives without having to wipe out the field, making mission times much shorter.
  • Standard fit should be DPS+tank. Refit for the Hacking/Relic bonus subsystem for the selected missions.

Important Note:
* Your standing is only checked by the starter agent. After starting the epic arc, subsequent agents will not check standings.
* This means while running the epic arc, you can continue doing missions even if your standing drops below +5.00 yet remains above -2.00 Faction standing.

Final Rewards:
* Choice of path - Retraction = 10x RSS Core Scanner Probe (Base Sensor Strength 45 points) and Minmatar Republic Faction Standing increase = +5% Base (+6.25% with Social 5)
* Choice of path - Revelation = 25 Million ISK and Minmatar Republic Faction Standing increase = +10% Base (+12.5% with Social 5)

Mission 1 - A Demonstration

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Damage Dealt: Explosive, Kinetic
Web/Scram: Elite Frigate
Recommended Damage Dealing: Explosive, Kinetic
Recommended Ship: Battleship (long range fit with Afterburner)
Required: Archaeology III and Relic Analyzer module

Mission Objective:
  • Find and return 1x Olfei Medallion (0.1 m3) to Agent.

Note
  • The Medallion is located within a Cosmic Anomaly site called A Demonstration located somewhere within the Sveipar Constellation.

Initial Group:

5x Elite Frigate (Angel) Web/Scram
11x Elite Cruiser (Angel) Target Painting

Tips
  • No need to fit a Probe Launcher or do any scanning.
  • Cosmic Anomaly sites are listed at 100% in the Probe Scanner window with 'Show Anomalies' selected to allow warp ability.
  • Archaeology III skill and Relic Analyzer module is required to access the 3 containers within the site.
  • Each container holds 1x Olfei Medallion (0.1 m3). Only one Olfei Medallion is needed to complete mission.

Blitz
  • Purchase 1x Olfei Medallion (0.1 m3) from the Market and return to Agent.

Mission 2 - The Cost of Preservation

Faction: Mercenaries
Mission Type: Encounter
Space type: Normal Space
Web/Scram: Elite Frigate (Mercenary Wingman)
Recommended Damage Dealing: Thermal, Kinetic
Recommended Damage Tanking: Thermal, Kinetic
Recommended Ship: Battleship (long range fit + Afterburner)

Mission Objective:
  • Access the Archives, retrieve 1x Hauteker Memoirs (0.1 m3) and return to Agent.

Warning
  • A large amount of NPC Remote Repair happens in this mission. If you can't put enough DPS onto an Elite Frigate to instapop it, it will instantly repair all damage so focus on the easier-to-kill ships first, like the Cruisers. After the other NPC's are destroyed, nothing can remote repair the Elite Frigates which can then be easily destroyed with Combat Drones

Initial Group

1x Ailon Boufin (Gallente Elite Cruiser) Multiple triggers when attacked

Note
  • Spawn 1 triggered when Ailon Boufin is first attacked.
  • Spawn 2 triggered when Ailon Boufin receives Armor damage.

Spawn 1

5x Elite Frigate (Mercenary Wingman) Web/Scram
6x Cruiser (Mercenary Corporal, Mercenary Lieutenant)
5x Battleship (Mercenary Overlord)

Spawn 2

6x Battleship (Mercenary Overlord)
6x Cruiser (Mercenary Commander)

Tips
  • Stop attack on Ailion Boufin and concentrate attack on each NPC group when it spawns.
  • Ailion Boufin drops the Passkey required to access the Archives.
  • Passkey must be placed in the Archives to collect 1x Hauteker Memoirs (0.1 m3).

Mission 3 - Written By The Victors

Faction: Angel Cartel & Amarr Empire
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Damage Dealt: Explosive (Angel), EM (Amarr)
Web/Scram: Elite Frigate, Elite Cruiser
Recommended Damage Dealing: Explosive / Kinetic
Recommended Ship: Battleship (long range fit + Afterburner)

Mission Objective:
  • Retrieve 1x Wildfire Khumaak (0.3 m3) from the Central Burial Tomb and return to Agent.

Blitz
  • Ignore the incoming damage and go straight to the container and loot it. Warp out.

Initial Group (20-40 km) (Angel Cartel)

1x Elite Frigate (Arch Gistii Outlaw, Arch Gistii Thug) Web/Scram
7x Elite Cruiser (Arch Gistum Centurion, Arch Gistum Phalanx)
3x Elite Cruiser (Arch Gistum Breaker, Arch Gistum Defeater) Web/Scram
3x Battlecruiser (Gistatis Legatus, Gistatis Tribunus)Last Destroyed Is Trigger

Note
  • No standing lost for destroying these Amarr ships.

Spawn 1 (Amarr Empire)

2x Elite Battlecruiser (Imperial Templar Phalanx)
3x Battleship (Imperial Templar Martyr)
1x Elite Battleship (Imperial Templar Judgement)

Tips
  • Jump to 50-100km range, depending on your fit.
  • Destroy the EWAR Frigates first to help avoid high DPS!
  • Jump out and switch resistance modules upon new spawn.
  • Battlestation has large amount of Damage Points and drops no loot.

Mission 5 - From Way Above

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Damage Dealt: Explosive, Kinetic
Web/Scram: Elite Frigate, Elite Cruiser
Recommended Damage Dealing: Explosive, Kinetic
Recommended Ship: Battleship (long range fit with Afterburner)

Mission Objective:
  • Destroy all Angel ships and defend the Defiants base.

Warning
  • Spawns are time based = 1 to 2 minutes

Inital Group

3x Elite Frigate (Arch Gistii Ambusher, Arch Gistii Raider) Web/Scram
3x Elite Cruiser (Arch Gistum Phalanx, Arch Gistum Centurion)
2x Elite Battlecruiser (Gistatis Praefectus, Gistatis Tribunus)

Spawn 1

3x Elite Frigate (Arch Gistii Ruffian) Web/Scram
3x Elite Cruiser (Arch Gistum Marauder, Arch Gistum Liquidator)
1x Battleship (Gist Warlord)

Spawn 2

1x Elite Frigate (Arch Gistii Ruffian) Web/Scram
4x Elite Cruiser (Arch Gistum Breaker, Arch Gistum Defeater) Web/Scram
3x Battleship (Gist Throne / Cherubim)

Spawn 3

4x Destroyer (Gistior Seizer, Gistior Thrasher)
3x Elite Frigate (Arch Gistii Impaler, Arch Gistii Hunter) Web/Scram
3x Elite Battlecruiser (Gistatis Legatus, Gistatis Tribunus)

Spawn 4

5x Elite Cruiser (Arch Gistum Breaker, Arch Gistum Defeater) Web/Scram
3x Battleship (Gist Seraphim)
2x Elite Battleship (Gist Domination Saint, Gist Domination Nephilim) Target Painting (100km range)

Tips
  • Making a Bookmark and warping back in at range works, but you cannot warp to a different bookmark within the mission area.
  • 2x Gist Domination Saint / Nephilim EWAR (Target Painting) is effective at +100km range.
  • Destroying 2x Gist Domination Saint / Nephilim asap will help reduce incoming DPS.
  • Battleships can't hit at close range.

Blitz
  • Wait for the final spawn to arrive and destroy the 2x Elite Battleship (Gist Domination Saint / Nephilim).

After reading all the comments, I decided to use the 'warp to 100km' range option whenever available. This helped to spread out the rat packs. Most of the missions were easily and quickly completed except for Mission 5 - From Way Above.

Mission 5 - From Way Above:

NPC Spawns are indeed on a 1 to 2 minute timer, no triggers.
NPC spawn point is 50km from mission beacon.
NPC's will aggro up to 150km range.
NPC's will travel back to spawn point after reaching the 200km range.
NPC spawns will all group together and form 1 large group.

Due to my Sleipnir being fitted for close combat, I had to do a dance of 'Tug o War' with the group, working the different ranges listed above making the NPC's go back and forth from the spawn point out to 200km and back again. Doing this I was able to pick off the web / scram Frigates and Cruisers, then worked on the rest of the smaller NPC ships. After destroying all the Frigs, Destroyers, Cruisers and Battlecruisers at falloff range with Barrage M ammo and T2 Drones, I had no problem getting in close to the Battleships.

The 2x Domination Elite Battleships do Target Painting which is effective at +100km. I jumped on them first which greatly reduced the incomming DPS from all the other Battleships, plus they don't hit when doing up close combat.

Doing this took 5 of the 6 hr bonus time to complete it. If going in with close combat fit, probably a good idea to have a couple of fleet members on this mission. Wouldn't have to dance with the group then.
