Lost Records, Level 1


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:34 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage Dealt: Kin/therm
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Reccomended damage dealing: Kin/therm
Reccomended ships: Incursus (difficulty level: easy)

First Pocket


At warp-in the acceleration gate is 30 km away. When approaching the gate the enemies show up at 40+ km (7 frigates: 5 Coreli Scout and 2 Coreli Initiate) and start aggro immediately.
The gate can only be used by T1 frigates/destroyers/cruisers.

Second Pocket


In the second room the enemies are waiting at around 20 km and start aggro (2 frigates: 2 Coreli Agents and 2 destroyers: Corelior Soldier, Corelior Trooper).
The mission target is in the container guarded by the enemies.

There are no structures in the mission.
The second room carries like 350k units each of Pyroxeres, Scordite and Veldspar.
