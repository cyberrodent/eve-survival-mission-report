Friendly Spies, Level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 10:39 UTC

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Therm, Kin
Recommended damage dealing: Kin, Therm
Recommended ships: Drake

Single Pocket

Upon warp-in there are 5 ships that aggro. There are 2 spawns. Upon destruction of the last ship in the group, spawns will show up and auto aggro.

Initial Group

20-30km
3x Destroyers (Federation Arcus/Matara)
2x Cruisers (Federation Nauclarius/Praktor Hippeus)

First Spawn

20-30km
2x Elite Frigates (Elite Federation Insidiator)
2x Crusiers (Federation Praktor Centurion/Legionarius)

Second Spawn

20-30km
2x Elite Frigates (Elite Federation Machina/Phalarica)
1x Battlecruiser (Federation Calo)

Objective
Destroy the Radio Telescope

Blitz
The target [Radio telescope] is 29km away when you warp in. Just target and kill it. The Initial Group of Gallente ships mostly can't hit beyond 8,000 meters and only did 3,000 hp total damage before I took out the target. No sign of either webbing or warp jamming in Initial Group. Very easy mission; you could do it in a level 2 cruiser [Omen, Caracal, Vexor, Stabber].

Standings
No standing lose on destroying the Radio Telescope.
