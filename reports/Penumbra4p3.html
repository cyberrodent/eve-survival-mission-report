Mission 14 - The Knowledge to Act (Story Arc Branch) - (Hyasyoda path)

The story arc branches according to your choice :

If you did do 'An Honorable Betrayal' from the first branch, your choices will be :

- Home In Peace (leads to 25m ISK reward and Caldari State Faction standing increase.)
- Slipping Away (leads to Hyasyoda Mobile Laboratory reward and Caldari State Faction standing increase.)

Mission 14 - A Difference of Opinion (Story Arc Branch) - (Nugoehuvi path)

The story arc branches according to your choice :

If you didn't do 'An Honorable Betrayal' from the first branch, your choices will be :

- Home In Peace (leads to 25m ISK reward and Caldari State Faction standing increase.)
- Learning by Doing (leads to 20x Nugoehuvi Synth Blue Pill Boosters reward and Caldari State Faction standing increase.)

Mission 15A - Home In Peace - (Caldari State path)


Mission Type: Low Sec Courier
Recommended Ship: Hauler/Blockade Runner

Head to Teskanen and pick up ten thousand Caldari POW's (5,000m3) and transport them to Malkanen.

Note :
Mission completion ends the Caldari Epic Arc.

Mission 15B - Slipping Away - (Hyasyoda path)


Report to Arikio Kuretsu at Wuos V - Moon 1 - Hyasyoda Corporation Mining Outpost.

Mission 15C - Learning by Doing - (Nugoehuvi path)


Report to Katsen Isha at Poinen IV - Moon 13 - Nugoeihuvi Corporation Development Studio.

Mission 16A - Across the Line - (Hyasyoda path)


Faction: Nugoeihuvi (Caldari Ships)
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space (can warp to structures)
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/Scramble: Nugoeihuvi Elite Frigates warp scram
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic (best), Thermal (secondary)
Recommended ship: Any Battleship w/ primary drone damage or FoF missiles

Spawn on Entry: 1x Battleship (Nugoeihuvi Caretaker, see addendum below original entry)

The following rats spawn as soon as you get within about 100km or engage the caretaker:

13x Nugoeihuvi Elite Frigate (Talbu State Wakizashi/Suriage/Shirasay/Shinai) Web, Scramble
4x Nugoeihuvi Elite Cruiser (Talbu State Daimyo/Shugo) Jamming, Defenders
6x Nugoeihuvi Battleship (State Shukuro Shogun/Nagashin) (Kin/therm guns, Thermal missiles, 60-70km orbit)
1x Nugoeihuvi Caretaker (Battleship)(Does not agro until you attack it with your own weapons; drones will draw its agro if they engage anything)

Blitz: Kill Caretaker and scram frigs, grab family and warp. (use alt to agro or very dangerous)

Note :
Mission completion ends the Caldari Epic Arc.

Mission 16B - The Price of Silence - (Nugoehuvi path)


Faction: Caldari (ishukone)
Mission Type: High Sec Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic (best), Thermal (secondary)
Web/Scramble: The Ishukone Watch Hawks might web and warp scramble your ship Rooks and Buzzard heavy Dampening
Recommended Ship: Raven
Objective: Recover the original hard copies of the mission documents from somewhere inside the Ishukone Watch facility

Deadspace entrance guards:
1x Ishukone Watch Commander (Battleship)
6x Ishukone Watch Raven (Battleship)
4x Ishukone Watch Moa (Cruiser)
4x Ishukone Watch Rook (Cruiser) Damping
2x Ishukone Watch Buzzard (Frigate) Damping
2x Ishukone Watch Hawk (Frigate) Warp Scram
4x Ishukone Watch Kitsune (Frigate)
8x Ishukone Watch Cruise Missile Battery
2x Ishukone Watch Stasis Tower Web

After you kill all the ships you must approach the central building to receive a message and spawn a container with 1x Ishukone Operational Reports (0.1 m3). Return to agent and complete mission.

Blitz:
  • Kill the webbing towers and whichever other rats who web/scram you.
  • burn towards the tower, can will spawn some 50km from where the dialog pops up; Loot item and warp out.
  • Blitzing assume you can tank the whole room (e.g.:Tengu with specific resists). Control enemy DPS accordingly.

Note :
Mission completion ends the Caldari Epic Arc.
Ships have no bounty.
