Faction: Angel Cartel
Location: High-Sec
Damage Dealt: Exp/Kin
Web/Scramble: None
Extras: Target Painting Gistii Ruffian
Recommended Damage Dealing: Exp(best), Kin(secondary)

Single Pocket

Auto-aggro from each wave. Last ship in each waves triggers next wave.

Initial Group

2-3x Frigate (Gistii Hijacker/Outlaw/Rogue)
2x Sentry (Angel Basic Defense Battery/Light Missile Battery/Tower Sentry Angel I)

Wave 1

2-3x Frigate (Gistii Nomad/Thug)

Wave 2

1-3x Frigate (Gistii Ambusher/Nomad)
0-2x EW Frigate (Gistii Ruffian) Target Painting
0-1x Destroyer (Gistior Haunter/Hunter)

Wave 3

0-3x Frigate (Gistii Hunter/Raider)
1-3x Destroyer (Gistior Defacer/Shatterer)

Wave 4

0-2x Frigate (Gistii Hunter/Imapler)
1-2x Destroyer (Gistior Defiler/Haunter)

Wave 5 (Optional)

1-3x Frigate (Gistii Hunter/Raider)
0-2x Destroyer (Gistior Defiler)
