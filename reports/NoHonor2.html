No Honor, Level 2


Last edited by SolaAesir:

Tue, 25 Aug 2015 19:01 UTC

Faction: Mercenaries
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Thermal/Kinetic, Thermal Missiles
Recommended Damage Dealing: Kinetic
Recommended Ships: Hawk (Or any assault frigate)

Single pocket


Single Group (17 km) (Auto Aggro)
4x Mercenary Elite Fighter
1x Mercenary Commander
1x Federation Industrial

Mission completes when you destroy the Federation Industrial. Remember to collect the stolen goods (80 m3). The hauler probably wont be on your overview but will have a red + like the other mission rats.

Blitz: destroy the industrial, loot the container, ignore the mercs.
