Vengeance, level 4


Last edited by RoboZed
Mon, 24 Mar 2014 12:23 UTC

Faction: Blood Raider
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage Dealt: EM/Therm
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM
Webifiers/Scramblers: Dark Corpii Seeker, Blood Wraith, Blood Disciple
Recommended ships: Apocalypse, Raven
Video: http://youtu.be/euAaxv68dcU , Golem , Maelstrom , Bastion Kronos

Group 3 in Pocket 1 may aggro your drones, it's safe to take them out early.
Other than that it should be safe to deploy drones in all pockets.

Pocket 1

Group 2 may or may not aggro at warp-in. One webber in the whole space. All groups within 15-30km.

Group 1

5x Frigates (Corpii Reaver/Engraver)
3x Cruisers (Corpum Priest/Sage) NOS
1x Battleship (Corpus Oracle)

Group 2

3x Battlecruisers (Corpatis Phantom/Exorcist)

Group 3

3x Destroyers (Corpior Friar/Devoter)
4x Battlecruisers (Corpatis Bishop/Seer) NOS

Group 4

4x Cruisers (Corpum Dark Priest/Shadow Sage) NOS
1x Battleship (Corpus Apostle)

Group 5

6x Cruisers (Corpum Arch Sage/Arch Priest) NOS
1x Battleship (Corpus Prophet)


Pocket 2

All groups within 15-20km.

Group 1

3x Battlecruisers (Corpatis Seer) Agg when you first enter

Group 2

3x Frigates (Blood Wraith/Disciple) WEB
4x Cruisers (Corpum Arch Sage/Arch Priest) NOS
5x Battlecruisers (Corpatis Seer/Bishop) NOS
3x Battleships (Corpus Oracle/Prophet)

Group 3

3x Frigates (Corpii Reaver/Engraver)
2x Destroyers (Corpior Devoter)
5x Cruisers (Corpum Shadow Sage/Dark Priest) NOS
1x Battleship (Corpus Archbishop)

Group 4

5x Battlecruiser (Corpatis Bishop/Seer) NOS

*Cathedral drops dairy products, consumer electronics, slaver, slaver hound

Pocket 3


Group 1

(15-20km including Sentries) Auto Aggro
3x Tower Sentrys
3x Destroyers (Corpior Templar/Devoter) (Last one destroyed is Trigger for Group 3)
4x Cruisers (Corpum Arch Priest/Arch Sage)
1x Battleship (Corpus Oracle) (Trigger for Group 2)

Group 2 (50km)

6x Frigates (Blood Wraith/Disciple) WEB
4x Battlecruisers (Corpatis Shade/Fanatic)
1x Battleship (Corpus Monsignor)
1x Battleship (Faramon Mundan)

Group 3 (60km)

6x Destroyers (Corpior Visionary)
4x Battlecruisers (Corpatis Bishop)
1x Battleship (Corpus Archon)


Mineable Asteroid info
Pocket 1: 1.290.000 Veldspar
Pocket 2: 705.000 Veldspar

You warp in between sentry guns and a EM forcefield, with a small group of cruisers and a battleship firing at you. Get past the sentries, and take them out. Then the aggroing group.

There are two more groups, but they might seem a bit mixed. Fire at one rat and get aggro from one group at a time.

If you get aggro from everything, and you are not tanked vs kin, make sure you take out the named rat, as he deals a lot of damage. Had to use two repairers to keep up first time I tried this mission.

Chief Rat: Faramon Mundan: DPS: 230.20hp/s (EM: 85hp/s, Expl: 0hp/s, Kin: 43.20hp/s, Therm: 102hp/s) + Wrath cruise missile.

Blitz
Kill all rats in first two pockets. In Pocket 3 you need to kill only Corpus Oracle from group 1 (to trigger group 2) and killing every rat in group 2 will flag the mission complete.
