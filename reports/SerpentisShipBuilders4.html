Serpentis Ship Builders, level 4 Important


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sun, 29 Jul 2012 17:28 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace, gate
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm
Extras: Light Damp
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic
Recommended ships: Ishtar

Single Pocket

Kill all the Serpentis ships and the "Partially constructed Megathron" to complete the mission.
Everything is disguised as "Serpentis Looter" except the Lord Admiral which is disguised as "Colonel 'Bat' Guano".
Everything aggroes at jump-in. Easier than most standard missions. Similar to Duo of Death.

Group 1:

2x Serpentis Cruise Missile Batteries 20km from initial warp in
5x Corelior Cannoneer [Destroyer]
4x Serpentis Looters [Battlecriuser] orbiting at 24km (Damp)
2x Core Admiral [Battleship]
1x Colonel 'Bat' Guano [Battleship]

As Saschas stated, this will decrease yur caldari faction standing, which is normal for any storyline mission that you do for an agent that is part of a corp that is part of the Gallente or Minmatar factions. If you were to skip this mission simply because it decreased your Caldari standing, you might as well skip doing ALL storyline missions.

On a side note, I did this mission after the Incursion 1.5 patch (patch that removed agent quality). The mission was given to me by a Astral Mining Inc. agent, and it gave me +11.2204% with Gallente faction, +28.051% with Astral Mining, Inc., +56.102% with the agent, and -4.0727% with Caldari faction. IMHO, the increases outway the decreases and long as your Caldari faction isn't so bad you can't go to Jita to shop (you can always use alts if that happens).
