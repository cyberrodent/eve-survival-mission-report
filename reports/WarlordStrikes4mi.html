Warlord Strikes (Storyline), Level 4

Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sun, 29 Jul 2012 17:39 UTC

Faction: Minmatar
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Type not confirmed, done with afterburner fit, with gate
Damage dealt: Exp/Kin
Web/scramble: Republic Fleet C-2 Support Frigate (Web)
Recommended damage dealing: Exp

Single Pocket

After warping you will end up in the Broken Blue Crystal Asteroid.
Group 1 will aggro soon.

Group 1

4x Frigate (Republic Fleet C-2 Support Frigate) (Web)
3x Cruiser (Republic Fleet Officer)
2x Battleship (Republic Fleet Typhoon)
1x Battleship (Warlord Ixon Kruz)

Tip:
Exit from the Broken Blue Crystal Asteroid immediately and kill C-2 web frigates first.
