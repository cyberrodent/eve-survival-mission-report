Recon (1 of 3)


Faction: Serpentis
Mission Type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage Dealt: Kinetic, Thermal
Web/scramble: Guardian Frigs
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic, Thermal
Recommended ships: Shuttle (blitz), Rattlesnake, Dominix, Megathron
Video: Kronos Part 1,2 & 3

The gates are not locked, so you can blitz this mission without a single kill.
Respawns are time based (time between spawns is approx 1 minute).

Pocket 1


Gate is 25km from gate-in.

4 Spawns, Total spawnlist:

1x Battlecruiser (Corelatis Captain Sentry/High Captain)
2x EW Cruisers (Corelum Guardian Chief Guard) (damping)
3x Heavy Assault Cruisers (Corelum Guardian Chief Sentinel/Infantry)
6x Battleships (Core Lord Admiral/Grand Admiral)

No drop from bunkers.

Pocket 2


2 spawns, total spawnlist:

3 x Interceptors (Coreli Guardian Agent) (Web/scramble)
3 x EW Cruisers (Corelum Guardian Chief Patroller) (Heavy damps)
5 x Battleships (Core Admiral/High Admiral)



Blitz

Go to warp gate (25km from gate-in), activate it, when you reach the next pocket you are done!

Bounty

11,790,450.00 (full clear)
