Chapter 1


Chapter 2 - For Fun and Profit


Mission 6 - Miscommunication


Faction: Caldari State
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Kin (best), Therm (secondary)

You will recieve 1x Data Analyzer I upon accepting this mission so you do not need to bring one from high sec.

Initial Group:
10x Frigate (Caldari Navy Patrol Frigate)
2x Elite Frigate (Caldari Navy Special Operations Patrol)
2x Destroyer (Caldari Navy Patrol Destroyer)

Reinforcement Wave 1:
Spawns upon a successful hack.
2x Frigate (Caldari Navy Patrol Frigate)
2x Destroyer (Caldari Navy Patrol Destroyer)

Upon hacking the Venal Regional Comms Tower do not forget to loot the holoreel inside.

Blitz: If you can tank the Initial Group, the mission completes as soon as you hack the Venal Regional Comms Tower and grab the item inside.

Mission 7 - Fuel Gauge


Faction: Caldari State
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/Scramble: Web/Scram
Recommended damage dealing: Kin (best), Therm (secondary)

Gate Guards:
7x Frigate (Fueling Depot Guard) Scram & Web
2x Destroyer (Fueling Depot Guard)
2x Stasis Tower (Caldari Stasis Tower)

First Room

There are a bunch of battleships in here, but they will not engage you for a bit, blow the Fuel Depot Alpha/Beta/Gamma to destroy them all.
The frigate and destroyers will immediately engage you.

Initial Group:
18x Battleship (State Tenkyu/Utaisho/Yojimbo/Zewn)
7x Destroyer (Fueling Depot Guard)
7x Frigate (Fueling Depot Guard) Scram & Web

Mission 8 - Knockout Punch


There will be a lot of exploding ships and death in this mission, but nothing will damage you.

You are given a caldari shuttle which you have to fly for this mission.

First Room
Shortly after entering this room all enemy ships will explode. Proceed to the damaged caldari shuttle and pickup the explosives from it.

Second Room
Approach the carrier and it and your shuttle will explode and you will be left in your pod. The mission is now complete.

This mission gives the first major reward of the arc: 1x Dread Guristas ECM Multispectral Jammer
