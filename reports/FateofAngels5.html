Fate of Angels, Level 5

Last edited by LevelFive:
mission is not blitzable, all ships need to die
Wed, 21 Jan 2015 12:13 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace – No Gates
Web/Scramble: Arch Gistii
Extras: Energy Neutralizer Sentry, Target Painter
Damage dealt: Exp: 40%, Kin: 29%, Therm: 24%, EM: 6%
Recommended damage dealing: Expl/Kin
Recommended ships: Maelstrom w/ Passive Tank plus Maelstrom w/ Shield Transporters

Direct Warp into Deadspace. No Gate

Warp-In

Ships at 50-100km, instant aggro

15 x Cruisers (Arch Gistum Centurion/Liquidator/Marauder/Crusher*/Smasher*) orbits of 4-25km, *Target Painters
11 x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Legatus/Tribunus) orbits at 23-28km
4 x Battleships (Gist Cherubim/Seraphim) orbits at 36-39km
4 x Tower Sentry Angel III
5 x Angel Cruise Missile Batteries
1 x Angel Stasis Tower
1 x Angel Energy Neutralizer Sentry III Trigger



Back Group Spawns

When the first ship in the Back Left, and the first ship in the Back Right group is attacked, they each spawn:

4-6 x Frigates (Gistii Ambusher/Arch Gistii Outlaw/Thug) Web/Scramble

Angel Battlestation Spawns:


When the Angel Battlestation (350,000 hp) is reduced to 95% Shields, there spawns:
6 x Frigates (Arch Gistii Impaler) orbits at 12km, Web/Scramble

When reduced to 20% Armor, there spawns:
4 x Cruisers (Arch Gistum Liquidator) orbits at 22km
4 x Battlecruisers (Gistatis Legatus) orbits at 23km

When reduced to 30% Structure, there spawns:
3 x Cruisers (Arch Gistum Defeater) 4km
3 x Battleships (Gist Seraphim) orbits at 36km

Mission is flagged complete when Angel Battlestation and all ships are destroyed. In Wrath of angels, only the drug lab needs to be destroyed, and the scientists picked up.


Optional Spawns:

Not required for mission completion

Attacking the (3) Repair Outposts, each spawn:
2 x Faction Battleships (Gist Domination Saint/Nephilim) orbits at 31-34km

Attacking Energy Neutralizer Sentry spawns:
3 x Frigates (Arch Gistii Ambusher) orbits at 12km, Web/Scramble
3 x Cruisers (Gistum Centurion) orbits at 17km

Tips
  • Use a Passive Tank, or plan a capacitor that receives an extra 40 units/sec
  • Maximum damage dealt of initial groups if all get into range is 2,602 hp/s (Exp: 40%, Kin: 29%, Therm: 24%, EM: 6%)
  • Please note, If using logistic ships the spawns will attack the logistic ships before anything else.
