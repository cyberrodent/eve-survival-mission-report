Guristas Extravaganza, level 3


Last edited by AmnesiaC:
Added Loot and Salvage info
Wed, 01 Jun 2016 19:42 UTC

Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kin, therm
Completed with: Prophecy, Ishtar

Tip: Chance for valuable implant drop from Storage Silo in Pocket 5

Pocket 1:

Group 1 (near Acceleration Gate):

4x Cruiser (Pithum Inferno/Mortifier)

Group 2 (near radio telescope):

1x Frigate (Guristas Kyoukan/Webifier) Web
1x Battlecruiser (Pithatis Assaulter/Enforcer/Executor)
1x Cruiser (Pithium Inferno/Mortifier)

Group 3 (near Gas silo):

1x Frigate (Guristas Kyoukan/Webifier) Web
2x Battlecruiser (Pithatis Enforcer/Executor)

Group 4 (near Guristas Lookout):

1x Frigate (Guristas Kyoukan/Webifier) Web
3x Destroyer (Pithior Anarchist/Renegade)
1x Cruiser (Pithum Inferno/Mortifier)

Pocket 2:

3x Tower Sentry Guristas I/II
3x Guristas Light Missile Battery
1x Cruiser (Pithum Silencer/Ascriber)

Blitz: Kill just the Cruiser, leave the turrets alone.

Pocket 3:

1x EM Forcefield (Large Collidable Structure)
2x Guristas Heavy Missile Battery
2x Industrial (Guristas Transport)
2x Destroyer (Pithior Anarchist/Renegade)
5x Cruiser (Pithum Abolisher/Ascriber/Eraser/Silencer)
1x Battlecruiser (Pithatis Executor)

Tip: To kill the forcefield you will need to right click the station battery and choose Add Collidable Structure to Overview.

Blitz: Kill everything but the turrets.

Pocket 4:

1x Industrial (Guristas Personnel Transport)
3x Frigate (Guristas Kyoukan/Webifier) Web
4x Frigate (Pithi Demolisher/Destructor)
7x Cruiser (Pithum Ascriber/Inferno/Mortifier/Silencer)
2x Battlecruiser (Pithatis Enforcer/Executor)

Structures with loot: Guristas Outpost Residential Hub, Narcotics Warehouse, Major Assembly Array

Pocket 5:

1x Spider Drone I
5x Frigate (Pithi Demolisher/Destructor)
6x Cruiser (Pithum Abolisher/Eraser/Inferno/Mortifier)
1x Battlecruiser (Satori Kardun)

Structures with loot: Storage Silo

Blitz: Kill Satori and the Spider Drone. Might as well loot the Storage Silo, too.


Bonus Pocket 6:

Bonus Stage - Acceleration Gate needs a Guristas Palladium Tag (not consumed)

Difficulty: Hard (requires full tanking abilities)

4x Frigates (Pithi Destructor/Guristas Kyoukan/Pithi Demolisher)
2x Battlecruisers (Pithatis Assassin/Pithatis Death Dealer)
1x Battleship (Pith Dismantler)
3x Tower Sentry Guristas II
2x Guristas Heavy Missile Battery

Structures with loot: Guristas War Installation (drops are not valuable, some people reported about 1-2m, others got as little as 2000 isk, including me - really not worth the time.)

Loot
Loot: ~2,000,000 (10m or more with good implant drop from Storage Silo)
Bounties: ~4,000,000
Salvage: ~1,500,000
