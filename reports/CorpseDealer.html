This is a 3-part COSMOS mission given by Rozor Mothrus, a Brutor Tribe agent in Barkrik solar system, Ani constellation of the Metropolis region.

Corpse Dealer - The Bodypart Trade (part 1 of 3), Level 3 COSMOS


Mission type: courier

Corpse Dealer - Bring Back the Goods (part 2 of 3), Level 3 COSMOS


Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: Explosive / kinetic / thermal
Web/scramble: Web even from some Gistum Liquidator cruisers.
Recommended damage dealing: Explosive
Recommended ships classes: Battlecruiser, Heavy Assault Ship
Recommended generic setup: Sniping with tank and light drones.

Pocket 1


Groups aggro separately and there is no autoaggro except for 2 light missile towers in the second pocket.

Group 1 (20km)


1x battlecruiser (Gistatis Primus)
1x battlecruiser (Gistatis Tribuni)
4x destroyer (Gistior Haunter)

Group 2 (38km)


2x cruisers (Gistum Marauder)
3x cruisers (Gistum Liquidator) Stasis web
3x frigates (Gistii Ruffian)

Group 3 (45km)


1x battlecruiser (Gistatis Tribunus)
2x destroyers (Gistior Shatterer)
4x detroyers (Gistior Defacer)

Group 4 (50km)


1x battlecruiser (Gistatis Legionnaire)
5x destroyers (Gistior Defiler)

Pocket 2


2x Angel Light Missile Battery, Auto-aggro (and shooting at these might trigger increased aggro)

You'll get the mission objective loot from the wreck. You need to pop the rats here to open it.

Group 1 (30km)


1x cruisers (Gistum Liquidator)
2x cruisers (Gistum Marauder)
2x frigates (Gistii Ambusher)
1x frigates (Gistii Raider)

Group 2 (30km)


1x cruisers (Gistum Marauder)
2x cruisers (Gistum Liquidator)
2x frigates (Gistii Ambusher)
1x frigates (Gistii Raider)

Group 3 (70km)


3x cruisers (Gistum Marauder)
2x frigates (Gistii Ambusher)
1x frigate (Gistii Raider)

Group 4 (70km)


2x cruisers (Gistum Liquidator)
1x cruiser (Gistum Marauder)
2x frigates (Gistii Raider)
1x frigate (Gistii Ambusher)

Group 5 (orbiting the wreck)


1x battlecruiser (Gistatis Tribuni)
1x battlecruiser (Gistatis Praefectus)
2x detroyers (Gistior Defiler)

Corpse Dealer - Kutill's Hacker Program (part 3 of 3), Level 3 COSMOS


Mission type: COSMOS inter-linking mission.

For this, you need to have completed the mission Kutill's Warez - Hacker's Equipment (part 1 of 3) for Dagras Kutill at the same COSMOS mission site, otherwise you will not have access to Kutill's Data Chip, the mission objective item. These can be bought off contracts but the market for them is unreliable.

After you have the Data Chip in your cargohold, speak to the agent, accept the mission and immediately complete it.

It is possible to complete all 3 Kutill Warez missions and all 3 Rozor Mothrus. You do not need to fail KW's second mission (this requirement is widely reported). Complete KW's missions and then RM's missions. Once 'Corpse Dealer - Kutill's Hacker Program (part 3 of 3)' is accepted you get the additional hint that you could just attack KW, when KW is destroyed you get a can drop of 1 Kutill's Data Chip.
