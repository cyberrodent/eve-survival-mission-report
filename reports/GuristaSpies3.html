Gurista Spies, Level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:43 UTC

Faction: Gurista Pirates
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Extras: None
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/Therm
Recommended ships: Ishkur, Brutix, Drake, Rokh, Hurricane

Single Pocket

You warp directly into the pocket.
Group 2 is optional.
No structures drop loot.

Group 1: (30km)

3x Battlecruisers (Gursitas Spies)

Group 2: (60km)

1x Battlecruiser (Guristas Spy/Pithatis Assaulter)
3x Destroyers (Pithor Anarchist)

Blitz
Just kill Group 1.

Tip:
2nd group aggroed drones deployed upon 1st group.
