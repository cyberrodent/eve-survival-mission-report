Troop Build Up, Level 5 (Storyline)


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:21 UTC


Faction: Caldari State
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Extras: Sensor Dampening
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/therm
Recommended ships: anything that can tank 2000 ish DPS. (spider tank probably) and HUGE dps to take out the carrier if you want to. (approx 1500 dps tank on the carrier).

Single pocket, no gates.

Initial Spawn

1x Carrier (Sukuuvestaa Carrier) <-- NOS and Target Painting
6x Fighter (Sukuuvestaa Fighter) <-- Last kill triggers Spawn 2a

Timed Spawn 1 (90 seconds from warpin)

2x Battleship (Sukuuvestaa Battleship)
2x Industrial (Sukuuvestaa Crane)
1x Freighter (Sukuuvestaa Charon) <-- Possible completion trigger (one or the other)

Timed Spawn 2 (180 seconds from warpin)

2x Battleship (Sukuuvestaa Battleship)
3x Industrial (Sukuuvestaa Crane)
1x Freighter (Sukuuvestaa Charon) <-- Possible completion trigger (one or the other)

Spawn 2a

6x Fighter (Sukuuvestaa Fighter) <-- Last kill triggers Spawn 2b

Spawn 2b

6x Fighter (Sukuuvestaa Fighter) <-- Last kill triggers Spawn 2c

Spawn 2c

6x Fighter (Sukuuvestaa Fighter)


Notes:

- Did not attempt blowing up structures; there was an ORCA, 2 Ravents, 4 Cranes, and 4 Silos. (all large collidable)
- Heavy nos and target painting from the carrier. Didn't see any web/scram/other NOS.
- Carriers tank DPS was greather than my DPS; left it un-destroyed; 10 warden I's and 5 hammerhead II's could not kill it
- Blitz: Tank the room (approx 2000 dps, standard caldari damage profile); kill the charons. (they orbit at 70km).
- Loot and salvage was TERRIBLE. In total, less than 4 million. Highly suggest blitzing this.
- Charrons only dropped trade goods (600 rocket fuel, 1700 oxygen, 23 scientists, 30 marines)
