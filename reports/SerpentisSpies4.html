Serpentis Spies, level 4


Last edited by AceEightySix:
cleaned up
Sat, 16 Apr 2016 19:41 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: Therm, Kinetic
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic or Thermal
Recommended ships: Ishtar, Deimos, Raven, Dominix, Abaddon
Video: DominixNightmare


Single Pocket:


Sentries (Aggro)

2x Tower Sentry Serpentis III
2x Tower Sentry Serpentis II
3x Tower Sentry Serpentis I

Sentries are pretty spread out.

50 KM - 100 KM

Single group of ships (135-140km)


1x Battleship (Core Port Admiral)
3x Battlecruisers (Serpentis Spies)
4x Destroyers (Corelior Soldiers/Infantry)

Tip:
  • Damage per second from sentry III: EM: 3hp/s, Expl: 3hp/s, Kin: 9hp/s, Therm: 15hp/s
  • Attacking the sentries will aggro the group.

Structures
No loot from any of the structures.

Blitz
Take out all the ships. You don't have to take out the towers.
