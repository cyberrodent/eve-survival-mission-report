Last edited by EveSurvival

Faction: Ammatar Mandate
Mission type: Encounter
Damage dealt: EM, Therm (about 50%/50%)
Web/scramble: Ammatar Navy Support Frigates
Recommended damage dealing: Exp, Kin
Recommended ships: Drake
Video: Machariel, Sniper Maelstrom

Pocket 1

Gate to pocket 2 is 60 km from arrival point
Two groups, 35-85 km from arrival point

Group 1

2x Elite Frigate (Ammatar Navy Gamma I Support)
4x Cruiser (Ammatar Navy Maller)
1x Battleship (Ammatar Navy Armageddon)

Group 2

4x Elite Frigate (Ammatar Navy Delta I Support Frigate / Delta II Support Frigate) Web
10x Frigate (Ammatar Navy Soldier)
4x Cruiser (Ammatar Navy Augoror)
3x Battleship (Ammatar Navy Apocalypse / Armageddon)

4x Cruiser (Ammatar Navy Augoror)
