Convoy Attack, Level 5


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:56 UTC


Faction: Minmatar Republic
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (mwd does not work) with single entry gate
Damage dealt: Exp/Em/Kin/Therm
Extras: NOS (Republic Tribal Akila) / Target Painters (Chief Republic Gleeda, Republic Tribal Akila, Republic Tribal Pytara)
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Explosive
Recommended ships: CNR, Golem

Single Pocket

When you warp in, there is a single mixed group.

Aggro is drawn at first shot.

3x Frigates (Chief Republic Gleeda) (Target Painters)(Lock Jamming)
4x Freighters (Republic Freighter)
17x Battleships (Republic Tribal Akila/Jarl/Kinai/Pytara/Sigur/Ymir)


Tip
It is strongly suggested that you use Logistics support to keep your tank alive.
Ships will de-aggro if you get far enough away from the pocket start, except for the closer ones.

Loot
Freighters drop mostly trade items. Battleships drop heavy equipment. Loot is poor at best.
