Anomic Team: Enyo Burner, Level 4


Last edited by CirimCollective:
burner category helps
Fri, 20 Feb 2015 17:36 UTC

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kinetic/Thermal
Web/scramble: Burner Enyo (14km)
Extras: You can not enter mission site in anything larger than a Frigate hull.
Recommended damage dealing: Explosive
Recommended ship types: Kestrel, Harpy, kiting long range frigate (25km+)
Video: Kestrel (T1/T2 fit)


Pocket 1


All ships aggro on warp-in. Burner Navitates are only remote repairing each other and Burner Enyo and will not deal any damage to you.

Hostile Ships (40-45 km)

2x Frigates (Burner Navitas) (Remote Armor Repair)
1x Elite Frigate (Burner Enyo)


Kiting Tips
  • set the default orbit distance before you enter the mission site;
  • set the orbit to whatever the range of your guns/launchers can support, but do not set it inside Burner Enyo's webbing range;
  • Burners can and usually do have "faction" webs, so watch out when choosing your orbit range;
  • Activate your Microwarp Drive and start orbiting the Burner Enyo as soon as you land. Otherwise, you'll end up being webbed and scrambled because Burner Enyo is pretty fast (750-820 m/s);
  • Destroy Burner Navitates first as they remote repair each other and the Burner Enyo;
  • Burner Enyo uses Defender Missiles. If you notice that you are not hitting it with your own missiles, try turning off your launchers and turning them on again. This may place your firing cycle between the firing cycles of Burner Enyo's Defender missiles;


Loot
CCP said that there is a slight chance of getting dedspace stuff in the loot. If you don't get dedspace modules, the loot is mostly made of Meta 4 modules.

Bounty
5,000,000 ISK for the Burner Enyo
