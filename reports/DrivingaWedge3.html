Driving A Wedge, Level 3


Last edited by JangoStone
Thu, 28 Mar 2013 20:56 UTC

Faction: Gallente Federation/Minmatar Republic
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm/Exp
Extras: Target Painters
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Part 1 - Therm; Part 2 - Exp
Recommended ships: Nighthawk, Drake
Can drop very good loot. Federation Tags-High Captain & Admiral

Get the Gallente (1 of 2)


Spawns appear as you warp in.

Pocket 1

Group 1 - Gallente Federation (Aggro)
Distance: 50km
4-6x Frigates/Destroyers
4-6x Cruisers
3x Battlecruisers

Group 2 - Minmatar Republic (Aggro)
Distance: 50km
4-6x Frigates/Destroyers
4-6x Cruisers
3x Battlecruisers

Group 3 - Gallente Federation/Minmatar Republic
Distance: 55km
1x Iteron Mark V (Needed to be killed or you will fail the mission)
1x Mammoth (Do not attack Minmatar Diplomat)
!Note : Careful sometimes the mission is kill the Minmatar and do not kill the Gallente !!

Blitz: Kill Gallente Diplomat.
Not really hard-shielded, but combined damage from two groups could likely kill you in process, so - bring some certain amount of firepower or be able to outrun initial aggro. Single-repped Myrm with ~50 resists all-around was held just long enough for a kill score, warped off with 5% armor left.

Delegation Destruction (2 of 2)


Spawns appear as you warp in.

Pocket 1

Group 1 - Minmatar Republic (Aggro)
Distance: 50km
3x Frigates (target painting)
6x Destroyers
3x Cruisers
1x Battleship (Flagship)

Blitz:
Kill the Flagship first, because it may warp off, but not 100% sure of this.
It's very, VERY hard to crack, unless you're in battleship yourself, and incoming damage from whole spawn is very high also.

If you can't tank 500+ DPS EX/TM, I suggest you forget about blitz.
Take destroyers down fast. They are (relatively) slow and short-ranged, not particularly hard to outrun, so you have plently of time for your drones to chew through them one by one. Use light drones, however...
It didn't warped off for me, so I vent on with Dualrep+AB Myrmidon, cleared adds, then refitted for usual 1rep+2Gyro and took a liberty of stocking heavy drones in the bay. Still, it was slow kill.

Disclaimer: Should your multi-million isk ship explode in a dramatic fashion while attempting this, it's all... uh... ChorusBeck's fault. Not for any good reason, it's just the first name I saw.

Step 1. Buy more insurance.

Step 2. Clear out everyone except the battleship. Warp in and out as required.

Step 3. The worst thing about the battleship is its shield regen, right? So don't let it. Fit an AB and an *energy destabilizer*. Get in nice and close, firing all the way. I hope this helps some newer players.

I run L3 missions in a Drake, and only got 1.5 million SP, and I have a different take on part 2 of this mission for a new player.

1. Kill the Target painting frigates first. Remember to launch drones.
2. Let your drones work the Dessies, while you take out a few cruisers until you can tank the combined DPS.
3. Reload missiles (Novas), approach the flagship while bombarding it continuosly.
4. Profit

Problem for a low SP player is that it takes a long time to take down the flagship (somewhere between 5-10 minutes - the shield is tough to crack), and you can't tank the combined damage of the NPC's for that long - but if you lower the number of ships firing at you you can buy yourself enough time to take down the Ymir.
