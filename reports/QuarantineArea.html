Quarantine Area, Class 5

Last edited by Markj2
Sun, 05 Jul 2015 21:41 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Cosmic Anomaly
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Omni
Webbers: Emergent Sentinel, Emergent Escort, Awakened Keeper, Awakened Sentinel, Sleepless Sentinel, Sleepless Guardian
Scramblers: Emergent Sentinel, Awakened Keeper, Sleepless Sentinel, Sleepless Guardian
Recommended ship classes: Capital, Battleship, T3 (7+ Remote Repair Modules)
Recommended generic setup: Multi-range, Heavy Omni-tank, Remote Repair (Spider Tanking)

All sleepers will auto aggress; Sleepers will switch targets, drones and support ships are not immune.
Triggers on this site are **confirmed** as still being random as of 10/April/2015 and can spawn on any NPC kill (including Sentry Guns)


Single Pocket


Capital escalation waves spawn 43km from warp to 0 and 23 from the veldspar asteroid on the left side of the structures, looking at them from the warp to 0. Show sensor overlay to see the initial warp to 0 location. This bookmark is hard to make correctly, so warping the initial carrier at 200km to spawn the first wave is recommended before making the bookmark.

Capital Escalation Wave 1

4164 DPS
6x Battleship (Sleepless Guardian) [scram, web, nos]

Capital Escalation Wave 2

5552 DPS
8x Battleship (Sleepless Guardian) [scram, web, nos]

Initial Group

1022 DPS
4x Sentry Guns (Orthrus)
3x Frigates (Emergent Escort) [web]
2x Cruisers (Awakened Keeper) [scram, web, nos]
1x Battleship (Sleepless Sentinel) [scram, web, nos] - NOT the trigger. Kill this 1st.

Reinforcement Wave 1

1220 DPS
4x Cruisers (Awakened Sentinel) [web]
2x Battleships (Sleepless Sentinel) [scram, web, nos] - TRIGGER.


Reinforcement Wave 2

1586 DPS
5x Frigates (Emergent Sentinel) [scram, web]
3x Cruisers (Awakened Warden) [rr]
2x Battleships (Sleepless Keeper) [nos]




Marauder Instructions

Initial Group

1022 DPS
4x Sentry Guns (Orthrus)
3x Frigates (Emergent Escort) [web]
2x Cruisers (Awakened Keeper) [scram, web, nos]
1x Battleship (Sleepless Sentinel) [scram, web, nos]



You Should bookmark the big oval structure (Talocan Silo) and warp about @ 10km.

Blap the Battleship first as it is NOT the trigger. (20/Dec/2013)



Reinforcement Wave 1

1220 DPS
4x Cruisers (Awakened Sentinel) [web]
2x Battleships (Sleepless Sentinel) [scram, web, nos]



1st wave spawns far away but they will burn quickly towards you.
This time, Battleship is the trigger so save them for last.

Before killing the last battleship, go towards near the position of the next spawn, since battleships and cruisers will NOT burn towards you.


Reinforcement Wave 2

1586 DPS
5x Frigates (Emergent Sentinel) [scram, web]
3x Cruisers (Awakened Warden) [rr]
2x Battleships (Sleepless Keeper) [nos]
