Search and Rescue, Level 1


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:40 UTC

Faction: Guristas Pirates
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (no gates)
Damage dealt: Kinetic
Extras: Jamming (from a random ship around half way through the mission)
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic
Recommended ships: Cormorant

Single pocket

Mission objective is to loot a disabled Badger 20 km from the warp in point
The badger can only be opened when all of the defending rats are dead
All rats spawn within 5km of your ship and auto aggro

Group 1

7x Frigate (Pithi Infiltrator / Invader / Arrogator)
When each pair of these ships is killed, 2 more defenders may or may not spawn

Group 2

Spawns after killing the whole of spawn 1

6x Frigate (Pithi Arrogator / Imputor / Saboteur / Despoiler)
After killing 3 ships, 2 more may spawn

After all of the defenders are dead, you can open the Badger and loot the 5 VIPs from it.
