Seek And Destroy, Level 3


Last edited by TechReviewsAndHelp
Thu, 14 Mar 2013 22:39 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Exp, Kin
Web/scramble: Viper & Webifier
Recommended damage dealing: Exp, Kin
Recommended ships: Hurricane, Drake (almost any battlecruiser should be more than adequate)
Completed in: Hurricane (Was an easy mission for low skill set)
Video guide: http://youtu.be/4OxtHLxdMCs


No hostiles at the initial gate

Single Pocket


Group 1

18-25km
aggro upon warp in
1x Frigate (Angel Viper/Webifier) (web)
2x Destroyers (Gistior Defacer/Haunter)
1x Cruiser (Gistum Liquidator/Marauder)

Group 2

45-55km
4x Frigates (Gistii Ambusher/Raider)
1x Battlecruiser (Gistatis Primus)

Structures
Radiating Telescope Structure drops loot @ about 50km

Video
The video in the following link shows you where everyone will show up, and their range. This guide also only shows one of many ways to take out the bad guys.
http://youtu.be/4OxtHLxdMCs
