The Score, level 4


Last edited by PawelloSolette
Sun, 28 Dec 2014 14:47 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: TH, KI
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic, Thermal
Recommended ships: Rattlesnake, Machariel, Sleipnir, Megathron, Ishtar, Dominix, Drake, Raven, Astarte, Maelstrom, Abaddon, Tengu
Recommended fit: Afterburners for warp gates 61km and 44km away.
Video: Cerberus, Machariel, Sniper Maelstrom


Pocket 1

No aggro.
61km to warp gate. GATE is locked

Group 1: (35-55km)

4x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender/Corelum Chief Protector)
2x Destroyers (Corelior Infantry/Corelior Soldier)
1x Battlecruiser (Corelatis Platoon Leader)

Group 2: (55-70km)

8x Frigates (Coreli Guard/Coreli Safeguard)
3x Destroyers (Corelior Infantry/Corelior Soldier)
4x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender/Corelum Chief Protector)
3x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Platoon Leader/Corelatis Squad Leader)

Mining
Pyroxeres Roids (approx 1.47 Million units)

Pocket 2

No aggro.
44km to warp gate.

Group 1: (40km)

2x Destroyers (Corelior Infantry/Corelior Soldier)
6x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender/Corelum Chief Protector)
2x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Squad Leader)

Group 2: (55km)

4x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender)
1x Battleship (Core Rear Admiral)

Group 3: (55km)

3x Battleships (Core Commodore)
(auto aggro from 50km)

Pocket 3

No aggro.

Group 1: (65-90km)

5x Frigates (Coreli Guard/Coreli Safeguard)
2x Destroyers (Corelior Soldier)
4x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender/Corelum Chief Protector)
2x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Squad Leader/Corelatis Platoon Leader)
4x Battleships (Core Baron/Core Commodore/Core Port Admiral)

Blitz
Kill the 4 battleships in the final room, the mission is flagged complete when all have been destroyed. You don't need to kill the battlecruisers, cruisers or frigates.

Profit
Bounty - ~6,400,400.00 (15.11.2013)
Loot/salvage - ~8,140,000.00 (15.11.2013)

You can aggro only part of each room by shooting far left on the first section and then at the frigs down and right on the second.

Move away first and use tungsten to engage at maximum range and pick them off. 2 guns on each frig should insta pop them or take 2 volleys at the most. Don't just use Anti Matter. Stuff gets too close in large groups and its hard to take them all down.
