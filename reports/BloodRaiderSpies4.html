The Blood Raider Spies, level 4


Last edited by FunSurvivor
Tue, 14 Oct 2014 12:30 UTC

Faction: Blood Raiders
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM/Therm
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ship classes: Battleship, Command Ship, HAC

Single Pocket

Group 1: (Auto-Aggro 5km)

3x Battlecruisers (Corpatis Bishop/Seer)
1x Battleship (Corpus Harbinger/Oracle) (NOS)

Group 2: (30-40km)

3x Destroyer (Corpior Visionary/Converter)
3x Cruiser (Corpum Shadow Sage/Dark Priest)
3x Battleships (Corpus Archon/Prophet)

Group 3: (50-60km)

3x Destroyer (Corpior Cleric/Friar)
3x Battlecruisers (Blood Raider Spy - Corpatis Exorcist)

Tip:
Drones may get aggro
Structures drop no loot

Blitz
Killing group 3 will flag mission completion.
