Faction: Blood Raiders
Mission type: Encounter (Storyline)
Space type: Normal Space, without gates
Damage dealt: EM/Therm
Extras: Heavy tracking disruption from elite frigates
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ship classes: Drake, apoc

Single Pocket


Wave 1

Aggro on Warp-in (10-15 seconds)
2x Frigates (Corpii Engraver/Raider/Reaver)
3x Battleships (Corpus Cardinal/Monsignor) TRIGGER

Wave 2

Trigger - Last BS Wave 1
2x Elite Frigates (Elder Corpii Collector/Seeker) TD
3x Battleships (Corpus Cardinal/Monsignor) TRIGGER

Wave 3

Trigger - Last BS Wave 2
2x Cruisers (Corpum Arch Priest/Arch Sage/Dark Priest/Shadow Sage)
3x Battleships (Corpus Cardinal/Monsignor) TRIGGER

Wave 4

Trigger - Last BS Wave 3
2-3x Cruisers (Corpum Arch Priest/Arch Sage/Dark Priest/Shadow Sage)
2-3x Battlecruisers (Corpum Bishop/Exorcist/Fanatic/Seer)
6x Battleships (Corpus Cardinal/Monsignor)

Mineable Asteroids
20x Viscous Pyroxeres (~1.2M units)

Notes:
Cardinals orbit @ 49km
Monsignors orbit @ 44km
Corp standing increase: 45%
Faction standing increase: 9%
Total bounties ~14M ISK
