Foil The Military Preparations, Level 4


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sun, 29 Jul 2012 16:27 UTC

Faction: Caldari
Mission type: Kill
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Web/scramble: Caldari Navy Delta II Support Frigate
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/Therm

Single Pocket
Group 1

Auto aggro
50km from warp

2x frigates (Caldari Navy Officer, fires Thunderbolt heavy missiles)
1x BS (Caldari Navy Raven)

Group 2

Timed spawn (~3 minutes from warpin), auto aggro

3x frigates (Caldari Navy Delta II Support Frigate)
3x cruisers (Caldari Navy Moa)
4x BS (Construction Freight) - very low damage guns, extremely long lock time
2x BS (Caldari Navy Scorpion) - despite the name, does not appear to (or did not successfully) jam

Blitz:
Wait for the Construction Freight ships to spawn, kill them ignoring the rest of the spawn and warp out.
-2.4% standing loss to Caldari

Loot:
Caldari Navy Raven dropped CN cruise missiles. Structures can drop trade goods.
