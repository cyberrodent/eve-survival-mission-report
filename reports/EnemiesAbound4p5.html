Part 5 of 5


Faction: Minmatar Republic
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (MWD Works)
Damage dealt: exp 48% / kin 25% / em 16% / therm 10%
Web/scramble: Republic Fleet C-1 (and D-1) Support Frigates
Extras: NOS (Freedom Patriot, Freedom Liberty)
Recommended damage dealing: therm/kin (exp for RF Darkana)
Recommended Ship Classes: T3, Command Ship, Battleship, Marauder
Difficulty: Very Hard
*Drones will be aggroed by reinforcement waves*
HINT: Republic Fleet Darkana Have extremely high shield resist to EM (80%) Therm (70%) Kin (60%) Exp (50%) equip Explosive damage ammo to deal with these ships. They have 0% armor/hull resist to Exp; EM damage is virtually useless and they will regen faster than you can kill them. Use Explosive on the Smugglers Stargate.

Video: Navy RavenTenguCNR


Single Pocket


6 waves of Republic Fleet ships that warp-in at 4-minute intervals. The first wave (on warp-in) may be split into two groups with the 2nd group warping in shortly after the 1st.

Only the Stargate drops loot (standard commodities and some contraband)

A Bunker and Heavy Missile Battery will hit you with -2.4% faction hit if you destroy them. Only needed hit is -0.02% that occurs with 1 of the Republic Fleet Typhoons

This is one of the toughest Level 4 missions you'll deal with, simply because of the relative quickness that each wave warps in. If you ever need to warp out, you'll either have to come back with a few wingmates, or wait until the mission resets after downtime. The trick is to balance your tank and your firepower so that you don't get swamped. The RF Darkanas and the Freedom Patriots should be priority once they pop-up. The Darkanas fire high-damage Mojlnir (EM) torpedoes, and the Patriots use lasers. My Maelstrom's setup consisted of:

HI > 8x T2 1400mm Artillery (Phased Plasma & Fusion)
MID > LSB II, T2 SB Amp, 2x T2 Invuln. Fields, 1x T2 EM hardener, 1x T2 EX hardener
LO > 3x T2 Gyros, 2x Cap Fluxes
RIGS > 3x CCC
DRONES > 5x Hammerhead II's, 5x Valkyrie II's

The setup gave me 550-600dps and a 516dps tank.

-- TheAssimilator (2010-09-24 09:18:59)
Here's information about my first (and successful) attempt on this mission, I hope it'll help you.

First off, I earned around 42m ISK in 90 minutes with this mission, salvaging and looting included. That's pretty awesome and probably the best ISK/time ratio in any L4 I ever flew.
Pilots with more SP than me (got humble 2.7m as of now) can probably do it much faster, thus increasing ISK/time ratio. Work it out for yourself.
Note that I sold the loot directly at the mission hub, if I had hauled it to Jita with my alt it'd certainly have yielded like 20-30% more. That's >55m in 90 minutes, then.
I flew a Navy Scorpion with faction cruise launchers, 3 damage mods (T2) and faction X-Large shield booster. The important thing here is that I always take a cap booster with me on L4s. During this mission I used up all my 800-boosters, I think 15 in total ^^

Now regarding the mission itself:
I warped out a single time to buy more cap boosters.
The thing about a Navy Scorpion is that it has a gigantic target range. I have low skills so "only" 99 km which is really really extremely useful in this mission. If you have problems taking too much damage... with 99 km target range you can happily out-range those baad Darkanas. With a CNR you're probably in their range all the time and since Darkanas spam EM torpedoes... well, it's not nice.

So in conclusion what you do to finish this mission without too much trouble:
1. always fly away with afterburner. Only turn around once everything in target range is down.
2. use your drones to get rid of C-1/D-1 webbers and to kill cruiser-sized craft (especially in the first wave)
3. use cap-boosters!! I used more than 2.5 times my actual cap, probably even more thanks to cap-boosters. They're really really useful in this mission and will help you not only survive the nasty first wave but also reduce the number of times you have to warp out (mind you, pilots with more than my 2.7m SP and about the same target range won't have to at all)
4. as mentioned in the mission info above, Darkana are best killed with explosive damage so bring it or be doomed ^^
5. take care of your drones, consider calling them back and putting em into fight again each time a new wave spawns
6. On a sidenote, Freedom Patriots really hurt, kill them as fast as possible and before they're in optimum range. This should have priority even to battleships.


If you follow those rules the mission won't be easy but definitely not too bad.
I personally think this is the second-toughest L4 mission (after 'Worlds Collide', of course) and that shouldn't surprise. There are 5 or more elite BS in it, lots of other BS, webbers and cruisers that hit abnormally hard.
Hardest part was wave one but I didn't get into real problems. Just afterburn away from the spawn point and kill the cruisers as fast as possible, they really hurt.

::: ------ UPDATE ------- :::

I now did this mission the second time. And it has become rather easy! When you've finished it once you'll always know better which enemies to kill first and how to behave. This time I didn't even have to warp out to buy more boosters, actually I used only 11.
I guess killing those Freedom Patriots as soon as they were in max target range helped a lot. Outrunning Darkana torpedo range, it was quite an easy job. Maybe with faction weapons and shieldbooster you're already in a position to relax in every L4 mission, even with humble 2.75m SP ^^
