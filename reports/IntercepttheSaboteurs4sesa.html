Intercept The Saboteurs, level 4


Last edited by TechReviewsAndHelp
Wed, 06 Nov 2013 16:14 UTC

Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Faction: Serpentis, minor Sansha presence
Damage dealt: Kin / Therm (minor EM/Therm from Centii ships)
Extras: Dampening from Corelum Chief Guards/Safeguards
Web/scramble: Coreli Guardian Spy/Agents will warp scramble.
Recommended damage dealing: Kin, Thermal
Recommended ships: Hyperion, CNR, Abbadon, Astarte, Nighthawk, Ishtar, Gila, Dominix
Video: http://youtu.be/UzYCT6SPbeE


At the gate


Auto-Aggro. 10-15km

3x Frigates (Coreli Protector/Defender)
2x Destroyers (Corelior Sentinel/Cannoneer)

Single pocket


No aggro on entry.
Engaging any portion of the Right-Center group aggroes all of them, despite differing ranges and locations.

Right group:

(about 60km out) Can be attacked separately.

3x Frigates (Coreli Defender/Protector)
3x Interceptors (Coreli Guardian Agent/Spy) (Web/scramble)

Right-Center group:

(40-80km)

2x Frigates (Coreli Defender)
4x Destroyers (Corelior Sentinel/Cannoneer)
3x EW Cruisers (Corelum Chief Safeguard/Guard) (Damp)
3x Heavy Assault Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender/Protector)
4x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Squad Leader)
2x Battleships (Core Commodore/Rear Admiral/Flotilla Admiral)
3x Industrials (Serpentis Transport Ship)

The can will drop from the last ship in this center/right group. (such as the Core Rear Admiral or Serpentis transport) See: Blitz

Center-Left Group:

(70km) Can be attacked separately. Need not be engaged to complete mission.

2x Frigates (Coreli Protector)
4x Interceptors (Coreli Guardian Spy/Agent) (Web/Scramble)

Far Left Group:

(80km) Need not be engaged to complete mission.

2x Destroyers (Corelior Sentinel)
3x EW Cruisers (Corelum Chief Guard) (Damp)
2x Battlecruisers (Corelatis Squad Leader/Platoon Leader)
1x Battleship (Core Baron)

Structures: Habitation modules have chance to drop generic things, like VIPs. Small Armories have a chance to drop generic ammo.

Blitz:
The viral agent drops from last ship in the center/right group. Please provide any information in commentary for now.
(NOTE: I wasn't able to blitz this mission in the video)

Video
http://youtu.be/UzYCT6SPbeE

Standing hits
Killing one of the transporters incurs a 2.4% standing penalty
