Avenge a Fallen Comrade, Level 1


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:17 UTC


Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Exp
Recommended damage dealing: Exp
Recommended ships: Kestrel

First Pocket

Acceleration gate.

Second Pocket

Warp in right into group 1, group 2-4 aggro individually on attack.

Group 1: (Auto-Aggro)

2x Frigates (Gistii Hijacker)

Group 2: (40-50km)

3x Frigates (Gistii Rogue)

Group 3: (50-60km)

1x Frigate (Gistii Ambusher)

Group 4: (50-60km)

8x Frigate (Gistii Hijacker)

Mission objective: Habitat at about 75km
