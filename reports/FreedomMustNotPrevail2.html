Seven Minmatar Slashers approached me to 7,5km. All of them had to be killed for completion. Each wreck has insignia and some loot, some have freed slaves. Beware that each batch of slaves takes 60 m3 so you can get filled when taking them up. Total bounty was 35,000 ISK and Minmatar standings dropped by 0.22 points.
