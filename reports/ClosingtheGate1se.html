Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (mwd does not work) with gates
Damage dealt: Kinetic / Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Thermal
Recommended ships: Best frigate class

Objective: Destroy the mysterious jump gate

Pocket 1


Direct warp in on this pocket. No aggro.

1x Frigate (Coreli Scout) 15km

Pocket 2


Aggro on warp in.

1x Frigate (Coreli Agent) 20km
1x Frigate (Coreli Scout) 30km

Pocket 3


Aggro on warp in.
Smuggler Stargate in this pocket.

Group


1x Serpentis Sentry Gun 25km
2x Frigate (Coreli Initiate) 20-25km (Trigger)

Spawn 1


1x Destroyer (Corelior Soldier)

Spawn 2


When the Stargate hits armor this spawn appears:

3x Frigates (Coreli Initiates)
2x Frigates (Coreli Scout)



Asteroids

None.
