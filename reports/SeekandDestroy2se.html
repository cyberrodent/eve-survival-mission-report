Last edited by TechReviewsAndHelp

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Thermal/Kinetic
Recommended ships: Destroyer, Cruiser (Normal Ship Restrictions)
Recommended generic setup: Quick DPS
Video: http://youtu.be/tVLXsk7Q5Xk

Single Pocket


Group 1 (25km) (Auto Aggro)

4x Frigate (Coreli Guard, Coreli Safeguard)

Group 2 (75km)

3x Frigate (Coreli Guard, Coreli Safeguard)
1x Frigate (Coreli Defender)

Blitz
Complete on Group 2 destroyed, do not need to kill Group 1

Mineable Asteroids
None

Structures
Reactor Array, dropped nothing

Hint
Due to 75km to Group 2, an AB may be useful for slow, short range ships
