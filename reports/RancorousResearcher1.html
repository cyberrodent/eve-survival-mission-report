Rancorous Researcher, Level 1


Part 1 of 5

Mission type: Courier

Pick up data chip and return to agent. Mission reward will include Device for Parts 2 & 3.

Part 2 of 5

Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with acceleration gate
Damage dealt: None

Researcher is trapped in an Iteron marooned near a rock formation 18 km away from warp in point. Approach the ship with Device in your cargo hold and research will be freed, completing mission objective.

Part 3 of 5

Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with acceleration gate
Damage dealt: None
Recommended ships: Shuttle, anything fast to save some time.

Collect data from a series of 5 cloaked beacons. Approach each beacon with Device in your cargo hold to collect the data and complete the mission.
Beacon 1 is 14 km from warp in point.
Beacon 2 is 16 km from Beacon 1.
Beacon 3 is 19 km from Beacon 2.
Beacon 4 is 21 km from Beacon 3.
Beacon 5 is 23 km from Beacon 4.

To spot the beacons: Select "Open Overview Settings" and click Select All. The first beacon will be seen in the list. After you have visited that one the next will be shown etc. AFter the fifth beacon the Journal will update the mission.

Part 4 of 5

Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with acceleration gate
Damage dealt: None

Collect physical sample from cargo container and return to agent to complete mission. Cargo container is 50 km from warp in point.

Part 5 of 5

Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: None

Destroy center and return to agent to complete mission. To destroy the center: Shoot at the Large Collidable Object labled 'explosives'.
