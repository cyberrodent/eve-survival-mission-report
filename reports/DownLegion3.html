Down With The Legion, Level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:38 UTC

Faction: Mordus Legion
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space
Damage dealt: kin missiles/therm
Recommended damage dealing: kin/therm
Recommended ships: Drake, Hurricane
Difficulty: Easy

Pocket 1


Group 1


Group at 50km will attack and destroy Gallente Towers and aggro shortly after.

4x Frigate (Katana)
4x Cruisers (Cheetah/Puma/Bobcat)

Group 2 spawns about five minutes after the beginning of the mission.

Group 2


At 50km:
3x Frigates (Gladius)
4x Cruisers (Lion/Puma)
1x Battleship (Phanti)

Blitz
Kill the BS, warp out.

Tip
No standing loss with Caldari, despite what the agent says.
