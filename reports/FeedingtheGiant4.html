Feeding the Giant



Last edited by BuilderAlphaOne:
deleted reference to Scord/Veld, which do not exist.
Sun, 18 Jan 2009 21:26 UTC


Faction: The same as the system's belt rats
Mission type: Mining / Encounter
Space type: open space
Damage dealt: Same as the system's belt rats
Recommended damage dealing: Same as the system's belt rats

Acquire 5600 units of Oeryl (44800 m3), which is a variant of Dark Ochre

There are exactly 5600 units of Oeryl to mine. No other 'roids in the area.

Occasionally some rats will spawn, as if it was a normal asteroid belt in that system.

Tip: You may find it helpful to visit the site in a shuttle and bookmark the single Oeryl asteroid so that your barge/exhumer and indy can warp directly to it.
