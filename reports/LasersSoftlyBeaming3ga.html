Lasers Softly Beaming, Level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:52 UTC

Faction: Gallente Navy & one Amarr Cruiser
Mission Type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm/EM/
Recommended damage dealing: Therm, EM, Kin
Recommended ships: Caracal and upward


Single Pocket


Group 1
1x Cruiser (Geebel Tash-Murkon) TRIGGER
Instant aggro.
Range: 8km.

Spawn 1
5x Gallente Navy frigates
1x Gallente Navy cruiser

Tip
Amarr owned starbase structures [POS structures] in the area. Suggest NOT attacking them as you're in Amarr space.
I left the Amarr Colonel's insignia in space, not wanting to provoke their wrath on my cruiser.

Blitz
Kill Geebel's cruiser and tank the rest. Grab him and warp out. Likely easy to do in a Drake ... add a tractor beam to speed up.
