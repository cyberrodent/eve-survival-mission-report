Retribution, level 3


Last edited by ErikKalkoken:
clarification
Sun, 28 Feb 2016 10:57 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace
Damage Received: Explosive, Kinetic (minor Therm)
Recommended Damage Dealing: Explosive, Kinetic
Recommended Ships: Battlecruiser or higher / Gila
Video: http://youtu.be/fbNgwfnuTuM

No hostiles at initial acceleration gate.

Single Pocket

Four groups, no aggro
Attacking any one group will not aggro other groups

Group 1

25-30km
2x Frigates (Gistii Nomad/Ruffian)
2x Cruisers (Gistum Breaker/Defeater)

Group 2

35-45km
3x Cruisers (Gistum Centurion/Phalanx)

Group 3

45-50km
6x Frigates (Gistii Hijacker/Rogue)
3x Cruisers (Gistum Depredator/Predator) (thermal damage)

Group 4

70-75km
3x Frigates (Angel Viper/Webifier) (web/scram)
1x Battlecruiser (Gistatis Legionnaire/Primus)

Blitz
Destroy the Angel outpost.

Bounty
717,876 ISK (takes about 10-15 minutes in a drone Gila)

Standing Loss
-3 %
  • This penalty was incurred for destroying Angel Cartel's Minmatar Deadspace Tactical Outpost

Structures
Small Armory drops an assortment of rockets, cruise missiles, missiles, and medium to large ammo
Other structures drop no loot

Video
This video shows one of many ways to do this. (Blitz)
http://youtu.be/fbNgwfnuTuM
