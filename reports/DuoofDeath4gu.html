Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm (some exp and EM from the sentries)
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic, Thermal
Recommended Ship Class: Marauder, Battleship, Battlecrusier
Video: Cerberus CNR

Single Pocket


Single Group

3x Guristas Cruise Missile Batteries
2x Spider Drone II (orbit range: 7,5km. 100% chance to web, 5% chance to scramble.)
2x Battleship (Pith Usurper)

Blitz
Warp in, destroy the Pith Usurpers, pick up the Special Delivery that the last one drops, warp out.

Structures
All structures except for Power Generator drop random T1 ammo and commodities.
