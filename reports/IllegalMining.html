Illegal Mining, Level 4


Last edited by NoamChomsky
Thu, 24 Jul 2014 22:26 UTC

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter 'Storyline'
Space type: Normal MWD works
Damage dealt: Therm/Kin
Web/scramble: Elite Federation Manica/Libertus
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/Therm
Recommended ships: CNR, Golem, Dominix

Warp-In (50km)
•No auto-aggro on warp-in, attacking anything aggros initial spawn.
•Wave 1 Spawns when the Mobile Refinery goes below 25% shield.
•Wave 2 Spawns when the Mobile Refinery goes below 50% hull.

Initial Spawn
1x Battleship (Federation Praktor Phanix/Praeses)
2x Battleships (Federation Praktor Hexeris)
2x Crusiers (Federation Praktor Belos)
4x Destroyers (Federation Arcus/Pelicus)
2x Frigates (Elite Federation Manica/Libertus) Web
10x Gallente Miners (Atron)

Wave 1
3x Battleships (Federation Praktor Phanix/Praeses)
3x Battleships (Federation Praktor Hexeris)
3x Crusiers (Federation Praktor Legionarus/Centurion)
1x Crusier (Federation Praktor Harpago)
4x Destroyers (Federation Arcus/Pelicus)

Wave 2
3x Battleships (Federation Praktor Phanix/Praeses)
3x Battleships (Federation Praktor Helipolis\Covinus)
4x Battlecruisers (Federation Praktor Arx/Auxillia)
4x Battlecruisers (Federation Praktor Bearcus/Calo)
4x Battlecruisers (Federation Praktor Diablic)

Blitz:
•You don't need to kill the miners. Destroying the Mobile refinery completes mission.

Tip:
•Warping in at 100 km by warping to 0, stopping your ship, and then warping to 100 km does not work. Instead, use a bookmark.

I don't run anti faction missions, but I was interested in this one. First, killing the mobile refinery alone does complete the mission. Second, I wanted to run this mission without any faction drop: flying a (maxed out skills) snake with 3084 dps tank against the max of 2787 dps incoming (62% therm/38% kinetic) including a maxed out booster and 3 faction BS to come in to save me if I was going to die. Mission took 3 minutes to get into position and about 3 to kill the refinery, then mission over. ZERO standings hit other than the normal for a storyline.

My suggestion:
If you want to tank it, it has to be an overtank set to specific damage AND remember, until you're in optimal, you're getting damaged.
If you want to take the standings hit, it's just another "lots of ships" mission.
