Guardian


Role
  • Armor logistic that relies on energy transfer chaining to regenerate its capacitor.
  • Provides fleet with capacitor on request.

Skills
  • Certificate : Core Capacitor Standard
  • Logistics 4
  • Remote Armor Repair Systems 5
  • Energy Emissions Systems 4
  • Multitasking 3
  • Sensor Linking 4

Fitting Notes

Regular
  • Mandatory 4 large armor remote repair systems (minimum). DO NOT fit any medium armor remote repair systems.
  • Rig exlusively Ancillary Current Routers.
  • Standard 4 slot tank (3 resist slots and 1 plate slot) for basic fits.
  • EM and Thermal armor hardeners installed for better defence against more damaging laser hits.
  • NO Kinetic/Explosive resist modules needed. The ship's natural resistance profile AND signature radius will negate a major amount of Kin-Exp missile damage.

MWD
  • Similar to the Standard version but with MWD fitted.
  • EM Plating replaces the EM hardener to increase available CPU.

Stable
  • Mandatory 4 large armor remote repair systems (minimum). DO NOT fit any medium armor remote repair systems.
  • Rig exlusively Ancillary Current Router + Remote Repair Augmentor.
  • Standard 4 slot tank (3 resist slots and 1 plate slot) for basic fits.
  • EM and Thermal passive armor membranes installed for better defence against laser hits.
  • Cap stable repair for 2-4 large armor repair systems when/if energy transfer chain breaks.
  • Logistic 4 pilots able to convert to 5/1 configuration without any problems.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • Afterburner fitted (typical).
  • Typical mid slot : Remote Sensor Booster / Tracking Link.
  • The MWD/Stable variants may be preferred by certain fleets.

Assault/HQ
  • Afterburner fitted (improves survivability).
  • 4/2 configuration is typical to support fleet's cap requests.
  • Sensor booster fitted for quick target locks.
