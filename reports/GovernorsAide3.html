Part 3 of 5

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Deadspace(?)
Damage dealt: Therm, Kin
Web/scramble: ???
Recommended damage dealing: Therm, Kin
Recommended ships classes: Battlecruiser, Heavy tanked cruiser (150+ Therm+Kin)
Done with: Arbitrator (TM+KI+DC+MAR2 perfect)

Encounter
You warp near Serpentis Shipyard in the small asteroid belt (7x Omber asteroids)
All rats spawn near shipyard, then close/disengage apropriately.
If you are using sniper setup, you have plently of time to pad out of spawn point.

Wave 1
4x Frigates (Tristan kind)
2x Cruisers (Thorax kind, high damage in close)

Wave 2
2x Cruisers (Exequror kind)
3x Cruisers (Celestis kind)

Wave 3
1x Battlecruiser (Long-range)
3x Destroyers (High damage in close, but paper-thin. Get rid of them fast)

Wave 4
3x Destroyers (See above)
3x Cruisers (Thorax class, long-range (25km+))

Wave 5 (Spawn when most of Wave 4 defeated)
1x Battlecruiser (Long-range)
3x Destroyers (See above)
3x Transports (Carrying random drugs)
