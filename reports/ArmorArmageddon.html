Armageddon


Role
  • Excellent close range and midrange dps with pulse lasers and sentry drones.
  • Bad sniper. Suffers from power grid fitting issues, capacitor issues and inadequate range for Aurora crystals.

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Large Pulse Laser Specialization 3
  • Large Beam Laser Specialization 3
  • Controlled Bursts 5
  • Advanced Weapon Upgrades 5
  • Amarr Battleship 4
  • Sentry Drone Interfacing/Heavy Drone Operation 4
  • Energy Weapon Rigging 4

Fitting Notes
  • Ability to swap between close range and sniper configurations without changing (or wasting) rigs is heavily emphasized.
  • Mega Pulse / Mega Beam fitted.
  • Full gun tachyon variants require fitting an Ancillary Current Router.
  • Typical rigs : Energy Discharge Elutriation / Energy Burst or Collision Accelerator / Ancillary Current Router / Anti-Thermic Pump / Anti-Kinetic Pump.
  • Utility high slot is typically fitted with an Auto Targeter due to the CPU shortage.
  • Ship's Kin-Exp resistance is given more priority for better defence against more damaging missile hits.
  • Damage Control II + EANM combination avoids triple stacked resist penalty if an OGB bonus is applied.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • No afterburner (typical).
  • Mega Pulse Laser fitted (typical).
  • Full complement of sentry drones (Garde).

Assault/HQ
  • No afterburner (typical).
  • Mega Beam Laser fitted (typical).
  • Mid slot requires 2 Sensor Booster to target 150km distance.
