The Blockade, Level 4


Faction: CONCORD
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm (EM & Kinetic missiles)
Extras: None
Web/Scramble: Yes, frigs web and may scram
Reccomended damage dealing: Any (DED have even resists, use whatever you do highest damage with)
Mission completes when DED Army General in 5th wave is destroyed.
Recommended ships: Ishtar

Single Pocket


Multiple Spawns. Next spawn triggered by killing the DED Army General Battleship.

Initial spawn:

1 Stasis Tower
4 Cruisers (DED Officer 1st Class)
4 Battleships (3*DED Army Colonel, 1*DED Army General (Trigger) )

2nd Wave:

4 Frigates (DED Special Ops Panther)
3 Cruisers (DED Special Ops Raptor)
4 Battleships (3*DED Army Colonel, 1*DED Army General (Trigger) )

3rd Wave:

5 Frigates (DED Special Ops Piranha)
4 Cruisers (DED Army Captain)
4 Battleships (3*DED Army Colonel, 1*DED Army General (Trigger) )

4th Wave:

3 Cruisers (DED Officer 3rd Class)
4 Battleships (3*DED Army Colonel, 1*DED Army General (Trigger) )

5th Wave:

4 Frigates (DED Special Ops Panther)
4 Battleships (3*DED Army Colonel, 1*DED Army General (Mission objective) )

Frigates in waves 2 & 4 web & close distance fast. They orbit at around 7.5km.
All Cruisers fire Kinetic Heavy Missiles.
DED Army Colonels fire EM Cruise Missiles.
DED Army Generals fire EM Torpedos.
Next wave is spawned when the DED Army General is killed.
Drones appear to be targeted by cruisers only.

Blitz would be to kill the DED Army General in every wave until the 5th. Good luck doing that & living to tell the tale in anything smaller than a capital. Not much point in Blitzing it as the tags the enemy drop make you 28 million when sold to NPC orders.
