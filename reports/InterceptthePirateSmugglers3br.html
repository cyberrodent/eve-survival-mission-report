Intercept the Pirate Smugglers, Level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:46 UTC

Faction: Blood Raider
Mission type: Encounter
Damage dealt: EM, Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Em, Therm
Recommended ship classes: Battlecruiser, Recon, Faction Cruiser, Cruiser, Assault Frigate.

Objective
Fly to the Space-Port, ambush the convoy when it arrives, and then report back to your agent.

Single Pocket


Group 1 (40km)
6x "Space Port Guardian" (4x Corpior Cleric and 2x Mercenary Elite Fighter)
2x Sentry Guns

Group 2
Will spawn around 5 minutes after warp in.
4x "Convoy Guards" (4x Mercenary Elite Fighter)
1x "Convoy Guard Captain" (1x Corpior Exorcist)
2x Blood Raider Transport Ship

No loot in structure

Standing hits
Killing one of the transporters incurs a 2.4% standing penalty
