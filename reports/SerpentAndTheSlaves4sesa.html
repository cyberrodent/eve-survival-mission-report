ran this mission in a domi droneboat, didnt have any problems.
the lookout was 24km away on warp-in.

4 frigates, 2 cruisers, and 3 BS were in two formations in the first pocket, but aggro as one group. range was from 40km to 60km. (2 frigates and 3 BS to left, 2 frigates and 2 cruisers to right)

structures are abandoned residential habitation modules, freight pads, and serpentis slave transports.

wave one spawns immediately at 30km. wave 2 follows this pattern, spawning as the last of wave 1 dies. wave 2 was 3 frigates and 2 BS for me.
wave 3 was 3 BS and 2 BC.

drone aggro appeared normal, compared to other current L4 combat missions.
