Cleaning House, Level 5

Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 06:55 UTC

Faction: Sansha’s Nation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace, no gates
Web/Scramble: Stasis Towers
Extras: Energy Neutralizer Sentries, Tracking Disrupters
Damage dealt: EM: 58% / Therm: 42%
Recommended damage dealing: EM / Therm
Recommended ships: Maelstrom w/ Passive Tank plus logistics.

Direct warp into Deadspace. No gate.

Ships Summary Total -

24 x Frigates (Centii Loyal Butcher/Manslayer)
15 x Cruisers (Centum Loyal Execrator/Juggernaut/Mutilator/Slaughterer)
12 x Battlecruisers (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon/Specter/Wraith)
15 x Battleships (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant)
8 x Tower Sentry Sansha III
4 x Sansha Stasis Tower
2 x Sansha Energy Neutralizer Sentry III

Warp-In

Ships at 35k from warp-in to the left and right. Immediate Aggro



24 x Frigates (Centii Loyal Butcher/Manslayer) orbits at 8-16km, -- Triggers 1-7 --
8 x Tower Sentry Sansha III
4 x Sansha Stasis Tower
2 x Sansha Energy Neutralizer Sentry III

Destroying Frigates will create the following spawns:
Numbers and names can vary

- Spawn 1

3 x Cruisers (Centum Loyal Mutilator) orbits at 14km
3 x Battlecruisers (Centatis Behemoth) orbits at 22km

- Spawn 2

8 x Sansha Sentry Guns (spawn 2 at a time at the outpost)

- Spawn 3

2 x Battlecruisers (Centatis Wraith) orbits at 10km

- Spawn 4

1 x Cruiser (Centum Loyal Juggernaut) orbits at 19km, 40% Tracking Disrupter
3 x Battleships (Centus Dread Lord/Tyrant) orbits at 17-49km

- Spawn 5

4 Cruisers (Centum Loyal Slaughterer/Juggernaut*/Execrator*) orbits at 7-19km, *40-50% Tracking Disrupter
3 Battleships (Centus Tyrants) orbits at 49km

- Spawn 6

2 x Battleships (Dread Lords) orbits at 17km

- Spawn 7

2 x Cruisers (Centum Loyal Slaughterer) orbits at 7km
1 x Battleship (Centus Tyrant) orbits at 49km


Destroying some Cruisers, Battlecruisers and Battleships can create spawns

- Spawn 8

1 x Cruisers (Centum Loyal Juggernaut) orbits at 19km, 40% Tracking Disrupter
2 x Battleships (Centus Dread Lord) orbits at 17km

- Spawn 9

4 x Battlecruisers (Centatis Specter) orbits at 22km
4 x Battleships (Centus Dread Lord) orbits at 17km

- Spawn 10

When Sansha Outpost is reduced to 50% shields there spawns

3 x Battlecruisers (Centatis Phantasm) orbits at 10km
3 x Battleships (Centus Dread Lord) orbits at 17km

Tips
  • Use a Passive Tank, or plan a capacitor that receives an additional 80 units/sec
  • This mission looks identical to Sansha on the Horizon but seems to spawn many more ships
  • Despite instructions, mission often flags Complete when a certain Battleship is destroyed
  • Blitz: The centi manslayers and any of their sequential spanws do not need to be killed. Blow up the outpost, and the spawn triggered by attacking the outpost. (Battleships, cruisers, and frigates (horror))
