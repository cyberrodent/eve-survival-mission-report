Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Cosmic Anomaly
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: (EM/The 23%, Kin/Exp 27%)
Recommended ship classes: RR Nestors, RR Battleships, T3 Cruisers, Kitchen Sink + Logi (4+ Remote Repair Modules)
Recommended generic setup: Heavy Omni-tank, Remote Repair (Spider Tanking)
Average Payout: 97 Million Isk

All sleepers will auto aggress; Sleepers will switch targets, drones and support ships are not immune.

I'm updating this post due to questions I've otten in game. I made this comment previously:

"This site, along with all c4 anoms, can be solo'd in a tengu. This one deals the most dps, but has the advantage of very minor RR which is a huge plus for soloing. I have an active tank of about 1200 with a stability of about 65% (~100 cap a second) which takes care of the neuting in this anom and all the others. The 4 BS spawn being the trouble, you generally will be caught by the webs, and you are scrammed (and 1x neut), so be sure you tank enough.

This takes a long time solo but there isn't much RR to worry about so it's generally worthwhile. The otheranoms you have to get clever with the RR and are almost always a greater isk/hour with 2 tengus."

However since the RR change (as in, they took it out) there is less reason to do this anom now over a Barracks, isk/hour wise. Also the tengu setup I was using has become dramatically more expensive since I posted that. Roughly 2 billion to build a tengu that wil solo this site, plus low grades.

I've seen two RR tengus burn in this anom but three have done just fine. Off the top of my head I can't think of any fleet of two that can accomplish this site (that otherwise couldn't have just done it solo), but I'm sure it is possible.
