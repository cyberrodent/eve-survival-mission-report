Drone Infestation, The, Level 2


Last edited by ShintuFelper:
Added Category
Wed, 04 Feb 2015 17:48 UTC

Faction: Rogue Drones
Mission type: Normal Space MWD: Yes
Damage dealt: Thermal, EM
Recommended damage dealing: EM
Recommended ships: Frigate, Cruiser
Recommended generic setup: Long Range Drones or Close Range DPS
Misson: Take out the Drone Silo, and report back to the agent.
Remote Complete: No
Video: http://youtu.be/tzTk47xh3eY

Hint
Objective requires Large Collidable Structures to see on overview.
Ok to use T1 Drones.

Single pocket


Group 1 (45km)

4x Drones (Infester Alvi, Belphegor Apis)

Group 2 (50km)

4x Drones (Sunder Alvi, Decimator Alvi)

Group 3 (70km)

4x Drones (Render Alvi, Splinter Alvi)
2x Drone Light Missle Battery, Flameburst Light Missles

Group 4

4x Drones (Shatter Alvior, Destructor Alvum)
  • May warp in when you destroy the silo and engage the other drones

Blitz
Warp in, Destroy silo (53km), Warp out
  • Can use drones from warp in and not draw any aggro

Mineable Asteroids
11x Veldspar, 1x Scordite

Structures
Drone Silo: OBJECTIVE
Ruined Stargate, Tower, Walls: None drop loot

Time
Blitz:30 seconds
No Blitz: 2 minutes

Video

This video shows you how to do this mission.
http://youtu.be/tzTk47xh3eY
