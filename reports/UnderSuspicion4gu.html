Under Suspicion, Level 4 Storyline


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sun, 29 Jul 2012 17:33 UTC

Faction: Guristas
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/Therm
Recommended ships classes: Ishtar, Drake, Raven

Warp In

Everything aggroes

Group 1

2x Frigates (Pithi Plunderer)
2x Cruisers (Pithum Eraser)
5x Battleships (Pith Dismantler) (Trigger Spawn 1 & 2)

Spawn 1

2x Frigates (Pithi Destructor)
2x Battleships (Pith Dismantler)

Spawn 2

2x Cruisers (Pithum Abolisher) (Trigger Spawn 3)
2x Battleships (Pith Dismantler )

Spawn 3

2x Battleships (Pith Dismantler )
2x Battlecruisers (Pithatis Executor)

You have to salvage a ship wreck to acquire the mission objective, so don't forget to fit a salvager.
