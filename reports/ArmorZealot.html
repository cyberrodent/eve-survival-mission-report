Zealot


Role
  • Good close range and mid range dps with pulse lasers.
  • No sniper role.

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Medium Pulse Laser Specialization 3
  • Medium Beam Laser Specialization 3
  • Controlled Bursts 5
  • Heavy Assault Ships 4

Fitting Notes
  • Energy Locus Coordinator rig fitted : Multifrequency/Conflagration range up to 25km, Scorch range up to 58km.
  • Heavy Pulse fitted.
  • Ship's naturally high Kin-Exp resistance profile and low signature radius removes the need for any specific kinetic or explosive resist modules.
  • Ship's EM-Thermal resistance profile raised for better defence against more damaging laser hits.
  • Typical rigs : Energy Locus Coordinator / Energy Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator / Ancillary Current Router / Anti-EM Pump.
  • Faction EM armor hardener deliberately fitted to save CPU and minimize EM-Thermal resist loss if turned offline.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • Stationary fleets do not require for afterburner to be fitted.
  • Mobile fleets require either an afterburner or MWD to be fitted, plus sometimes a Code Breaker module.
  • Heavy Pulse Laser fitted (typical).

Assault/HQ
  • Afterburner fitted (typical).
  • Heavy Pulse Laser fitted (typical).
