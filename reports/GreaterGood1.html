Faction: Amarr
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal
Damage dealt: EM, Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Therm, Kin
Recommended ship type: Cruiser

1x Fighter (concord committee vessel)

Faction: Amarr
Mission Type: Encounter

Damage dealt: EM, Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Therm, Kin
Recommended ship type: Cruiser

Pocket 1

(agro on warp in 35km)

Pocket 2


10x Frigates (Imperial Disciple, Haran, Matendi)
All agro on warp in from about 40km
