Gate Blitz, Level 3


Last edited by ErikKalkoken
Sat, 19 Jul 2014 04:07 UTC

Faction: Gallente Federation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Extras: Sensor Dampening
Recommended damage dealing: kin/therm
Recommended ship classes: Stealth Bomber(to blitz), Battlecruiser, Cruiser
Recommended generic setup: Normal tank

Single Pocket

No ships at gate. Group 1 and 2 initially present in the pocket.
Spawn 1 appears after the first Industrial is destroyed. To complete the mission, destroy the three Federation Industrials (67km).

Group 1 (50km, auto-aggro)
5x Cruisers (Federation Praktor Centurion/Praktor Legionarius/Calo)
2x Cruisers (Federation Praktor Hippeus/Nauclarius) Sensor Dampening
3x Frigate (Federation Praktor Harpago/Praktor Belos)

Group 2 (67km)
3x Federation Industrial (Trigger, First Industrial destroyed)

Spawn 1 (35km, aggro on spawn)
1x Elite Cruiser (Elite Federation Arx) Sensor Dampening
4x Elite Frigates (Federation Praktor Belos)

Blitz
Destroy the three Federation Industrials.
