Epic Arc Overview


Starter agent: Aton Hordner
Agent corporation: Republic Security Service
Agent location: Egbinger (Minmatar Shipyards)
Standings required: 3.00 (Either Minmatar faction or Republic Security Service)

OR

Starter agent: Arajna Ashia
Agent corporation: Amarr Navy
Agent location: Sendaya (Abandoned Research Station Sigma Nine)
Standings required: 3.00 (Either Amarr faction or Amarr Navy)

OR

Starter agent: Ellar Stin
Agent corporation: Dominations
Agent location: Konora (The Coward Ellar Stin)
Standings required: 3.00 (Either Angel Pirate faction or Dominations)

Additional skills required: None

Important note :

Standing is only checked by the starter agent. After that all subsequent agents within the Epic Arc will not check standings.
Also only the following ships are allowed into the majority of sites: Assault Ship, Covert Ops, Destroyer, Electronic Attack Ship, Frigate, Interceptor, Rookie ship, Shuttle, and Stealth Bomber
Note: There are only three missions in this chapter.

Chapter 1 - Rapture


Mission 1 - The Balance of Power/Mistaken Identity/Headhunted


The Balance of Power given by Aton Hordner. Mistaken Identity given by Arajna Ashia. Headhunted given by Ellar Stin.

Report to Abdiel Verat in K-QWHE (K-QWHE V - Moon 4 - Archangels Assembly Plant).

Mission 2 - New Opportunities


Faction: Sansha
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: EM/Therm
Web/Scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: EM (best), Therm (secondary)

Ungated complex, mission completes upon warp-in, rats do not need to be killed.

Initial Group:
2x Frigate (Centii Loyal Enslaver/Manslayer)
2x Elite Cruiser (Centii Loyal Fiend/Hellhound)

Mission 3 - Fight or Flight(Story Arc Branch)


At this point you choose whether to fight mainly against Sansha and Guristas (Natural Consequences) or deliver a package to the Serpentis where you will be fighting Guristas and Mercs (Serpentis Fill-In). They both give the same reward and rejoin for Chapter 3 of the arc.


Chapter 2 : Utopia (Natural Consequences) / Chapter 2 : Heaven (Serpentis Fill-In)
