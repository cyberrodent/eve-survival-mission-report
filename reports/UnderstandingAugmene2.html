Faction: Normal belt pirates
Mission type: Mining
Space type: Deadspace, no gates
Damage dealt: Varies

Single Pocket


Rock formation, with seven Augmene (Pyroxeres) asteroids of varying sizes. Belt rats spawn at random, sometimes not at all.

Objective

Mine 7,500 units (2,625 m3) of Augmene.
