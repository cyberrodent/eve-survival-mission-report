Epic Arc Overview

Starter Agent: Karde Romu
Agent Corporation: Ministry of Internal Order
Agent Location: Kor-Azor Prime System at Karde Romu, MIO Agent (Celestial Beacon)
Standings Required: +5.0 Amarr Empire (Faction) or Ministry of Internal Order (Corporation)
Additional Requirements: Hacking III (skill) and Data Analyzer (module)

Important note :
  • Your standing is only checked by the starter agent. After starting the epic arc, subsequent agents will not check standings.
  • This means while running the epic arc, you can continue doing missions even if your standing drops below +5.00 yet remains above -2.00 Faction standing.
  • Karde Romu will remotely offer the first mission if you contact him by starting a conversation.

Final Rewards:
  • Choice of path Interrogation: Catching the Scent = Imperial Navy Modified 'Noble' Implant (Slot 10, +3% armor hit points,
+3% repair system repair amount) and Amarr Empire Faction Standing increase +10% base (+12.5% with Social 5)
  • Choice of path Silence Rahsa: The Nation's Path = Sansha Modified 'Gnome' Implant (Slot 7, +3% to shield capacity,
+3% shield recharge rate) and Sansha's Nation Faction Standing increase +10% base (+12.5% with Social 5)

    • Verified March 13, 2016- No Amarr Empire Faction Standing was received for completion of Interrogation: Catching the Scent path.

Mission 1 - Aiding an Investigator

Mission Type: Travel

Mission Objective:
  • Report to new Agent - Kandus Sandar - located in Nahyeen VI - Ministry of Internal Order Assembly Plant (Station).

Mission can be accepted remotely.

Note
  • Starting a conversation with Kandus Sandar after docking in station completes the mission objective.


Mission 2 - Late Reports

Faction: Sanshas Nation
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Damage Dealt: EM/Therm
Web/Scram: Elite Frigate
Extras: Heavy Tracking Disruption (Elite Cruiser & Battleship)
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM (best), Therm (secondary)

Mission Objective:
  • Retrieve 1x Reports (0.1 m3) and return to Agent.

Single Pocket

Note
  • Last ship destroyed in each group will trigger a new wave.

Initial Group: (28-37km)

3x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Ravener, Centii Loyal Scavenger) Web/Scram
4x Battleship (Centus Mutant Lord, Centus Savage Lord)

Wave 1: (27-32km)

4x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Beast, Centum Loyal Juggernaut, Centum Loyal Slaughterer, Centum Loyal Execrator) Tracking Disruptor
4x Battleship (Centus Beast Lord, Centus Plague Lord) Tracking Disruptor

Wave 2: (36-41km)

4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Manslayer, Centii Loyal Plague, Centii Loyal Enslaver, Centii Loyal Butcher) Web/Scram
4x Battleship (Centus Dark Lord, Centus Overlord)

Tip
  • 1x Reports (0.1 m3) spawns in can after last ship in Wave 2 is destroyed.

Mission 4 - Raging Sansha

Faction: Sanshas Nation
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Damage Dealt: EM/Therm
Web/Scram: Elite Frigate
Extras: Heavy Tracking Disruption (Elite Cruiser & Battleship)
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM (best), Therm (secondary)

Mission Objective:
  • Destroy all Sansha ships and capture their Commander if possible.

Mission can be accepted and completed remotely.

Single Pocket

Note
  • Wave 1 is triggered after the Sansha Commander receives damage and warps out.
  • Wave 2 is triggered by destroying the last Cruiser in the Initial Group.
  • Wave 3 is triggered by destroying the first Elite Frigate in Wave 2.
  • Wave 4 is triggered by destroying the Cruiser in Wave 3.

Initial Group: (31-54km)

4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Savage/Manslayer/Slavehunter) Tracking Disruptor
3x Cruiser (Centum Beast/Juggernaut) Tracking Disruptor Trigger (Wave 2)
2x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Mutilator/Fiend)
4x Battlecruiser (Centatis Daemon/Wraith/Phantasm)
5x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord/Dark Lord/Overlord)
1x Battleship (Sansha Commander - Centus Tyrant) Trigger (Wave 1)

Wave 1: (45km)

3x Battlecruiser (Centatis Specter)

Wave 2: (85-87km)

5x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Servant/Butcher/Enslaver) Web Trigger (Wave 3)
2x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Execrator/Slaughterer) Tracking Disruptor

Wave 3: (30km)

1x Cruiser (Centum Torturer) Trigger (Wave 4)

Wave 4: (32-38km)

1x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Manslayer/Enslaver)
2x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Torturer/Slaughterer/Hellhound) Tracking Disruptor
1x Battleship (Centus Plague Lord) Tracking Disruptor

Tips
  • Damage the Sansha Commander first (will warp away) and destroy Wave 1 for a quick reduction of incoming DPS.
  • Prioritize targets and eliminate EWAR threats asap.
  • Work each Trigger and its resulting Wave before engaging another.
  • Last Frigate destroyed in Wave 4 completes the mission objective.
  • Structures and Slave Transports have no loot.


Mission 5 - Cowardly Commander

Faction: Sanshas Nation/Amarr Empire
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Normal Space
Damage Dealt: EM/Therm
Web/Scram: Elite Frigate
Extras: Heavy Tracking Disruption (Elite Frigate, Elite Cruiser & Battleship); HEAVY NEUTING from Towers.
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM (best), Therm (secondary)
Recommended Ships: Tengu, Golem, Paladin, Vargur.

Mission Objective:
  • Destroy the Sansha Commander.

Mission can be accepted and completed remotely.

Warning
  • Be prepared for a large amount of EWAR and Very heavy DPS in this mission, more so if you get the triggers mixed up.
  • Make sure to prioritize targets and eliminate EWAR threats asap.
  • Neuting towers are always primary targets.

Blitz:
All Acceleration gates are open. Optionally you can bookmark the acceleration gates and warp-out/warp-in, activating them upon landing. Watch out for scrams in pocket 2.

Initial warp-in: MWD all the way through
Pocket 1: MWD all the way through
Pocket 2: Deal with the neuting tower first and the 3 scramming frigates before activating the gate. DPS on the room is very high and ignoring the neuting tower will render your ship dry.
Pocket 3: Proceed as normal (see below);


Acceleration Gate Entrance

Note
  • Last ship destroyed in the Initial Group will trigger Wave 1.

Initial Group: (15-28km)

3x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Minion, Centii Loyal Scavenger) Web
4x Battleship (Centus Plague Lord, Centus Beast Lord) Tracking Disruptor
2x Tower Sansha Sentry III (250km Range)

Wave 1: (22-28km)

4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Butcher, Centii Loyal Manslayer, Centii Loyal Enslaver) Web
4x Battleship (Centus Dark Lord, Centus Overlord)

Tip
  • Prioritize targets and eliminate EWAR threats asap.

Pocket 1

Note
  • The Acceleration Gate is NOT locked (55km away).
  • Last ship destroyed in the Initial Group will trigger Wave 1.
  • Microwarpdrive (MWD) can be used in pocket.

Warning
  • Energy Neut and Sentry Towers have 250km range.

Initial Group: (58-67km)

4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Slavehunter, Centii Loyal Savage) Tracking Disruptor
4x Battleship (Centus Dark Lord, Centus Overlord)
2x Tower Sansha Energy Neutralizer I (250km Range) Neut
2x Tower Sansha Sentry III (250km Range)

Wave 1: (62-64km)

3x Battlecruiser (Centatis Daemon, Centatis Devil)
4x Battleship (Centus Beast Lord, Centus Plague Lord) Tracking Disruptor

Tips
  • Prioritize destroying the Energy Neutralizer Towers first.
  • Can warp inside pocket to various locations.
  • If desired, quickly lead initial group out past 250km, then warp to towers and destroy them, then work on ships

Pocket 2

Note
  • The Acceleration Gate is NOT locked (15km away).
  • Wave 1 is triggered with destruction of last Elite Cruiser in the Initial Group.
  • Wave 2 is triggered with destruction of last Battlecruiser in the Initial Group.
  • Microwarpdrive (MWD) can be used in Pocket.

Warning
  • Energy Neut and Sentry Towers have 250km range.

Initial Group: (16-28km)

3x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Ravener) Web/Scram
4x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Torturer, Hellhound, Mutilator) Trigger (Wave 1)
4x Battlecruiser (Centatis Daemon, Behemoth) Trigger (Wave 2)
4x Battleship (Centus Plague Lord, Beast Lord) Tracking Disruptor
1x Tower Sansha Energy Neutralizer I (250km Range) Neut
2x Tower Sansha Sentry III (250km Range)

Wave 1: (40-51km)

3x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Scavenger, Ravener) Web
3x Battleship (Centus Dread Lord, Tyrant)

Wave 2: (23-32km)

4x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Butcher, Plague, Manslayer)
4x Battleship (Centus Dark Lord, Overlord)

Tip
  • Prioritize destroying the Energy Neutralizer Tower and any scram Elite Frigates first.
  • Focus attack on spawn after destroying trigger to help reduce incoming DPS.

Pocket 3

Note
  • Wave 1 is triggered with destruction of last Battleship in the Initial Group.
  • Wave 2 is triggered with destruction of last Battleship in the Wave 1 - A Group.
  • No standing loss for destroying Amarr ships in Pocket 3.
  • Microwarpdrive (MWD) can be used in pocket.

Initial Group: (43-46km)

2x Elite Frigate (Divine Imperial Imran) Web/Scram
3x Cruiser (Imperial Templar Tamir/Donus)
3x Battleship (Imperial Martyr/Dominator) Neut (10km range) Trigger (Wave 1)

Wave 1 (2 Groups):

A Group (35-39km)
2x Battlecruiser (Imperial Champion/Templar Justicar)
4x Battleship (Imperial Templar Ultara/Dominator) Trigger (Wave 2)

B Group (98-102km)
3x Elite Frigate (Divine Imperial Valok/Paladin)
3x Elite Cruiser (Divine Imperial Tamir) Tracking Disruptor/Neut
3x Battleship (Imperial Martyr/Ultara/Templar Torah) Neut (10km range)

Wave 2: (30-37km)

3x Destroyers (Imperial Templar Caius)
2x Battlecruisers (Imperial Templar Phalanx)
2x Battleships (Imperial Templar Martyr)

Tips
  • Prioritize targets and eliminate EWAR threats asap.
  • Focus attack on spawn after destroying trigger to reduce incoming DPS.
  • Last ship destroyed in Wave 2 completes the mission objective.
  • Structures contain no loot.
