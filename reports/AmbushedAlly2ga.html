Ambushed Ally, Level 2, Storyline


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 05:27 UTC

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace, no gates
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Extras: none
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/Therm

Warp-in


At warp-in you are faced with Sisters of EVE station, 4 x laser sentries and a Gallentean Federation Praktor Arx battlecruiser. Sentries do not open fire, nor does the objective until fired upon.

Ship drops tags only (Federation Navy fleet Captain and Major tags, one each).

Tip
  • Stayed 20km away from the The Federation Praktor and it never fired one shot.
