Scorpion


Role
  • Fleet ECM platform.

Skills
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Certificate : EWAR Operator Standard
  • Caldari Battleship 5
  • Multitasking 3

Fitting Notes
  • Auto Targeting System is fitted for maximum target locks.
  • Sensor booster may be removed if ship receives remote sensor boost from Oneiros/Guardian.
  • Cruise missile launchers may be removed to make room for more utility modules.
  • Typical utility high slots : Large Energy Transfer Array / Large Remote Armor Repair System / Auto Targeting System.
  • Damage Control II + EANM combination avoids triple stacked resist penalty if an OGB bonus is applied.

Site Doctrines

Assault/HQ
  • No MWD (typical).
  • Fleet Guardians will have to watch for repeated cap requests from the Scorpion to mitigate the heavy energy neutralizing that it will encounter.
  • Primary targets : Lirsautton Parichaya (bomber), Intaki Colliculus (heavy logistics), Outuni Mesen (heavy neutralizer), Arnon Epithalamus (ECM).
  • Secondary targets : Mara Paleo (logistics), Deltole Tegmentum (medium neutralizer), Ostingele Tectum (DPS).
  • Tertiary targets : Auga Hypophysis (DPS), Romi Thalamus (DPS).
