Cargo Delivery, level 3


Last edited by AmnesiaC:
Added loot values from my run.
Fri, 13 May 2016 15:47 UTC

Faction: Blood Raiders.
Mission Type: Encounter.
Space type: Deadspace.
Damage Dealt: Em/Thermal
Extras: Nos & Tracking Disruption
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Thermal
Recommended ships: Harbinger, or Prophecy.

Single Pocket


Spawn 1

Spawns may vary.
1-2x Elite Frigate (Elder Corpii Collector/Seeker) (Tracking Disruption)
1-2x Destroyer (Corpior Cleric/Friar) (Trigger)
2-3x Cruiser (Corpum Arch Priest/Sage)
3-4x Battlecruiser (Corpatis Fanatic/Shade)

Spawn 2

3-4x Destroyer(Corpior Cleric/Friar/Templar)
1-2x Cruiser (Corpum Priest/Sage) Nos
3x Battlecruiser (Corpum Bishop)
1x Elite Cruiser (Shecon Pouta) (Elder Corpum Dark Priest/Shadow Sage)

Blitz
Rush to the warehouse, get the item to retrieve and fly back to your agent. No kills needed to complete.

Loot
~ 900,000 isk in drops from all enemies.
~ 1,800,000 in bounties.
~ 600,000 from salvage with basic salvaging skill.

Total = ~ 3,300,000isk (3 Million)



Mineable Asteroid info
There are 60 Veldspar Asteroids containing 2.27m Units.

Harbinger plenty of ship. Didn't even get through raw (no DC even) shields.

Minor spawns very different from either listing above, but Trigger/likely NOS/Named Elite as listed.

Confirmed weapon tracking disruption in first spawn: 2x (Elder Corpii Seeker). Easily killed. If your tracking is poor, just straight line them.

10MN AB II very useful even on a (slow BC) Harbinger. Gets to warehouse quickly, and the npc's are even slower. The NOS ships spawned out of range due to immediate move to sniping distance during first spawn (briefly used 15km/25km range ammo). At any distance over 30, especially around 38-45km, practically unhit, and was able to maintain distance. Aurora M (Amarr t2 sniping crystals) enough to bring down even the BCs and Shecon Puta, though slow. Drones tested safe after acquiring standard ship aggro (recalled before Spawn 2, then redeployed); however, used guns alone to confirm enough dps/tracking/range (no mods, but correct damage types and t2, no drone dps).

Suggest if early skills, use a damage mod(s) and go light on the tank: But certainly snipe /w AB, use a DC if low skills, my speed was a bit above 385 once it got going. I would doubt that close-range guns would breeze through this mission with a weak tank.
