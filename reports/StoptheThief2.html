Stop the Thief, level 2


Last edited by GideonDelta
Fri, 27 Jul 2012 23:54 UTC

Faction: Mercenary
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space (mwd does work)
Damage dealt: Thermal
Recommended damage dealing: Thermal/Kin
Recommended ship classes: Cruiser
Recommended generic setup: Assault/Heavy Tank
Completed By: Catalyst (Armor Tank /w Thermal Hardeners)

Single Pocket


Single Group: (10KM) (Automatic aggro)

1x The Thief (Angel Interceptor)
5x Mercenary Fighter (Mercenary Frigate)
6x Mercenary Elite Fighter (Mercenary Frigate)
  • Mercenaries exclusively use Light Infernal Missiles (which do a base 75 thermal damage each.)
  • They hit hard and fast. It is advisable to kill the Elite Mercs ASAP if not Blitzing.

Blitz
Kill The Thief, take reports, warp out.

Mineable Asteroids
None

Structures
Cargo Rig - Drops 3-4x Random Ammo
Narcotics Supermarket - Dropped nothing
Information Exchange Booth - Dropped nothing
3x Solar Harvester - Dropped nothing
