Operation Hamstring, Level 3


Last edited by CatMurdoch:
Added screenshot; corrected spelling
Sat, 13 Apr 2013 09:39 UTC

Faction: Mercenaries
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: All
Reccomended damage dealing: Thermal

Single Pocket


No warp in aggro.



Single group present on warp in (20-30km):

- 3 x Mercenary Commander (Cruiser)
- 1 x Mercenary Lieutenant (Cruiser)
- 1 x Mercenary Corporal (Cruiser)

After these are dead, take out the Armory. It will drop assorted ammo.

Blitz

Kill Armory, Lieutenant, Corporal.
