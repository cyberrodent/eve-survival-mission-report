Dead Men Tell No Tales, Level 4


Last edited by CaptBoemer
Mon, 02 Feb 2015 07:44 UTC


Faction: Gallente Federation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gate
Damage dealt: Kinetic/Thermal
Extras: Sensor Damping
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Therm, Kinetic
Recommended ships: Manticore (sniper fit with sensor boosters to counter sensor damping, example fitting)

Single Pocket

Aggro from all ships on warp in.

Distance: 60-65km
4 x Battleship (Federation Covinus / Navis Longa / Proktar)

Reinforcement Wave

It will arrive when the Command Post's armor is down to 50%. Aggro on arrival.

Distance: 30-60km (to be verified)
7 x Frigate (scramble + webbed!!!)
2 x Battlecruiser
2 x Battleship

Blitz:
Kill the Gallente Tactical Command Post and the mission is complete. (Distance 64km at warp in, it has 40K EHP)

Tips
Warp in cloaked and start orbiting the Tactical Command Post at max range (e.g. 65 km) and top speed. When you are at full speed and well away from the warp in beacon (this gives you enough time to warp off in case you get jumped by a WT) de-cloak and activate your afterburner. After you reached full speed with afterburner lock up your target and start shooting. The battleships will not be able to hit you anymore and you can take down your target with ease.
