Operation Wyrmslayer, Level 5


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:17 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace, no gates
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Extras: Damp
Web/scramble: Stasis Towers.
Additional: Missile Batteries.
Recommended damage dealing: Kin/Therm
Recommended ship classes: Dominix/Ishtar with Logistics support (Dominix), speed tanking Ishtar with dual MAR, Active tanked Tengu
Recommended generic setup:

Single Pocket


Direct warp into ungated deadspace. Full room agro on first shot.



You will see 2 groups of ships each consisting of :

2x Frigates, (Coreli Guardian Protector)
6x Battlecruiser, (Corelum Chief Infantry, Coreltis Wing Leader, Corelum Chief Infantry)
4x Battleships, (Core Grand Admiral)

Additionally there are 2 groups of towers each consisting of :

1x Serpentis Stasis Towers
4x Serpentis Heavy Missile Battery

The first timed spawn consists of about a dozen mixed frigates: some guardian, some not.

Destroying the non-elite (not Guardian) frigates of that spawn causes a random Shadow Serpentis Battleship to appear which must be killed to complete the mission.

After the frigate spawn there will finally be another tower spawn:

2x Serpentis Stasis Towers
8x Serpentis Cruise Missile Battery

Note that the time that this second tower spawn occurs is independent of the Shadow Serpentis spawn.


Blitz
Shoot the initial frigs and the Maulus frigs that spawn in order to spawn the Shadow Serpentis BS. Kill this BS to trigger completion.

Notes
  • This mission is easy to solo and the lack of Neut towers makes it active tank friendly.
  • The Shadow Serpentis BS can drop Snake implants and several Shadow Serpentis modules.
  • Bounties from the rats are approx. 20 mil.
  • The Stronghold takes very long to destroy and does not drop good loot.
