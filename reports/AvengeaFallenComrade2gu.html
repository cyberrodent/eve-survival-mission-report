Avenge a Fallen Comrade, Level 2


Last edited by TechReviewsAndHelp
Mon, 03 Jun 2013 23:28 UTC

Faction: Gurista Pirates
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage Dealt: Kin/Therm
Recommended Damage Dealing: Kin/Therm
Recommended Ship Class: Cruiser
Video: http://youtu.be/6Nl7ZeZneMM

Goal: Destroy the pirate Habitat.

Single Pocket

Warp-in 10K from group 1. Groups 1 and 2 aggro upon warp in. Groups 3 and 4 wait their turn to be destroyed.

Group 1: (10km)

4x Frigates (Pithi Arrogator/Imputor)

Group 2: (40km)

5x Frigates (Pithi Infiltrator/Invader)
1x Destroyer (Pithior Nihilist)

Group 3: (40km)

3x Frigates (Pithi Infiltrator/Invader)
1x Cruiser (Pithum Silencer)

Group 4: (60km)

5x Frigates (Pithi Despoiler/Saboteur)

Blitz
Dash in and destroy the habitat, then warp out.
Mineable Asteroids
None
Structures
Radio Telescope, Shipyard, Subspace Frequency Generator - Nothing
Storage Facility, Factory Installation, Landing Pad, Asteroid Colony - Also Nothing

Video
This video shows what you will be dealing with.
http://youtu.be/6Nl7ZeZneMM
