Unauthorized Military Presence, level 3


Last edited by MycroftW
Sun, 06 Apr 2014 22:01 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm
Extras: Minor Dampening
Recommended damage dealing: Kin, Therm
Recommended ships: Ishtar, Curse, Huginn, Drake
Video: http://youtu.be/vlphaq2fTjo

Initial Gate

30km ~ 40km
4x Frigates (Coreli Patroller/Watchman)

Pocket 1

The militants will be in the transport wreck. Only 10 militants are required to bring back to agent to claim the rewards.

Group 1

35km ~ 40km
4x Frigates (Coreli Patroller/Watchman) damp
4x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Guard/Safeguard/Scout/Spy)

Group 2

70km ~ 80km
1x Serpentis Personel Transport (mission objective)
5x Frigates (Coreli Patroller/Protector/Watchman) damp
4x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Defender/Patroller/Protector/Watchman) damp

Group 3

80km ~ 100km
4x Frigates (Coreli Patroller/Watchman) damp
1x Cruiser (Corelum Chief Guard) damp
3x Cruisers (Corelum Chief Scout/Spy)

Blitz
Kill group 1, then approach group 2 and kill those. Be careful with drones after group 1, as they pull aggro from group 3 while working on group 2.

(To complete this mission you must take out the transport, and take it's cargo)

Asteroids
Veldspar - 101 asteroids - 3,702,375 units of Veldspar
Plagioclase - 51 asteroids - 987,594 units of Plagioclase
Pyroxeres - 26 asteroids - 165,944 units of Pyroxeres

About 30 Jetscans are used if you mine all the ore

Bounty
0.7m

Loot&Salvage
1.3m

Standing loss
Killing the Transport Ship incurs a -4.0% standing loss

Video
This video shows what you will be dealing with.

http://youtu.be/vlphaq2fTjo

-- TipzNexAstrum (2009-01-20 00:07:31)
I have taken Hulks and Iteron Mark V through the gates with no issues several times.

Takes a long time to travel through the gates to get to the asteroids, so if you go in to mine all the ore, simply have the hauler ship fitted with a Tractor Beam. Have the miners jetcan the ore as they mine it, marking the cans with the creation time. when the cans are full, use the tractor beam to pull them to the hauler. When the cans are 1hr old, refresh them by "flipping" the ore into a new can.

Once all the ore is gone, BM one of the cans, everyone leaves the deadspace (should have turned in the mission long ago to get the time bonus reward), the deadspace vanishes, and you can warp back directly to the cans to haul them to a station. Mining everything will result in approx 30 full jetcans of ore.

At the current mineral values in my region (tri = 4.10, Pye = 3.88, Mex = 25.61, Noc = 70.21) the resulting minerals would be worth approximately 74mil ISK.
