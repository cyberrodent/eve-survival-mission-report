Hidden Stash, level 2


Last edited by TechReviewsAndHelp
Tue, 15 Jan 2013 04:50 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Therm, Kinetic
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic
Recommended ships: Rifter
Mission: Blow up the warehouse, get the cargo (150 M), then take the cargo back to the agent.


Single Pocket:

Distance: 15-18km
5x Destroyers (Corelior Soldier/Infantry)
Immediate aggro

Structures
Warehouse (Trade goods) (Mission goods)
Cargo Rig (Ammo)
Pressure Silo (Ammo)
Habitation Module (Trade Goods)

Blitz
Warp in, then blow up the Warehouse. Go to the cargo contaniers, and load 15 small sealed cargo containers (150M) onto your ship. Give the items to the agent.

Asteroids
Veldspar Rocks x 35 (549,159 m3)

Rewards and Bouns
Rewards: 62,000 ISK and 113 LP
Bonus: 59,000 ISK if you get it done within 1 hour and 24 minutes.

Bounty
1xCorelior Soldier: 11,813 ISK
4xInfantry: 12,375 ISK
Total: 61,313 ISK

Time
Blitz: 1 min (This high dep on how fast your ship can pick up the items, and take them to your agent.)
No Blitz: 1 min to 3 min. (You can take everyone out quickly with the right drones and/or guns.)
