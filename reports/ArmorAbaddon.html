Abaddon


Role
  • Excellent close range and midrange dps with pulse lasers.
  • Above average sniper.

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Large Pulse Laser Specialization 3
  • Large Beam Laser Specialization 3
  • Controlled Bursts 5
  • Advanced Weapon Upgrades 4
  • Amarr Battleship 4
  • Sentry Drone Interfacing/Heavy Drone Operation 4
  • Energy Weapon Rigging 4

Fitting Notes
  • Ability to swap between close range and sniper configurations without changing (or wasting) rigs is heavily emphasized.
  • DO NOT fit Cap Rechargers when running Vanguard sites! All mid slots should be used for tracking/sensor/stasis web modules.
  • The critical capacitor problem is resolved by fitting Energy Discharge Elutriation rigs.
  • Mega Pulse / Mega Beam fitted.
  • Full gun tachyon variant can be fitted but at the cost of wasting a rig slot. Capacitor time is drastically shortened.
  • Typical rigs : Energy Discharge Elutriation / Anti-Explosive Pump.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • No afterburner (typical).
  • Mega Pulse Laser fitted (typical).

Assault/HQ
  • No afterburner (typical).
  • Mega Beam Laser fitted (typical).
  • Last mid slot is fitted with a Cap Recharger to extend capacitor time.
