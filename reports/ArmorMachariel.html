Machariel


Role
  • Good close range range dps with 800mm autocannon. Average mid range dps (reduced by falloff).
  • Above average sniper (with 1200mm artillery).
  • Utility high slots enable emergency remote repairs and energy transfer cap chain.

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Certificate : High-Velocity Helmsman Improved
  • Large Autocannon Specialization 3
  • Large Artillery Specialization 3
  • Advanced Weapon Upgrades 5
  • Minmatar Battleship 4
  • Gallente Battleship 4
  • Energy Emission Systems 4
  • Remote Armor Repair Systems 4

Fitting Notes
  • Ability to swap between close range and sniper configurations without changing (or wasting) rigs is heavily emphasized.
  • 800mm autocannon / 1200mm artillery fitted.
  • Fitting equivalent 1400mm artillery variant requires Inherent Implants "Squire" Engineering EG-603 and removal of the Projectile rig.
  • Typical rigs : Projectile Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator / Projectile Metastasis Adjuster or Ambit Extension / Anti-Thermic Pump / Anti-Kinetic Pump / Trimark Armor Pump.
  • Typical utility high slots : Large Energy Transfer Array / Large Remote Armor Repair System / Auto Targeting System.
  • Ship's Kin-Exp resistance is given more priority for better defence against more damaging missile hits.
  • Damage Control II + EANM combination avoids triple stacked resist penalty if an OGB bonus is applied.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • Stationary fleets do not require an afterburner.
  • Mobile fleets require either an afterburner or MWD.
  • 800mm autocannon (typical).

Assault/HQ
  • No afterburner (typical).
  • 1200mm artillery fitted (typical).
  • Mid slot requires 2 Sensor Booster to target 150km distance.
