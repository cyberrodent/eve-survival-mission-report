Mother Lode, level 4


Last edited by CuttleBug
Thu, 13 Mar 2014 18:40 UTC


Mission type: Mining
Space type: No gate, warp to beacon only.
Faction: Same as NPC on belts

Acquire 2800 units of Polygypsum (44800 m3)

Exactly 2800 units of Polygypsum to mine.

At the end of the first mining cycle on each asteroid, a massive explosion will occur. You might want to stay 5,000m away from them to avoid its effects.

Availability: Quafe Company
