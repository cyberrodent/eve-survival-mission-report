Labors of War (3 parts), Level 2


Last edited by GenoRed:
Added faction standing change information
Tue, 29 Dec 2015 10:46 UTC


Part 1 of 3

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (mwd does not work)
Damage dealt: Therm
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Explosion
Recommended ship classes: Battlecruiser
Recommended generic setup: Heavy Damage

Warp-in


Group #1 at 20 KM

3x Republic Tribal Baldur (Minmatar Frigate)
3x Republic Industrial (Mission Industrial)
  • No aggro on warp in
  • Industrials have no weapons

Group #2 at 20KM

3x Federation Praktor Belos/Phalarica (Gallente Frigate)
  • Appear as backup 3 minutes after aggression

Blitz
Destroy industrials, which have quite a bit of hp, to complete the mission.
No Faction standing loss for killing the industrial ships.
Mineable Asteroids
None
Structures
Gallente Lookout - Dropped nothing



Part 2 of 3

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (mwd does not work)
Damage dealt: Therm
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Explosion
Recommended ship classes: Battlecruiser
Recommended generic setup: Heavy Damage

Warp-in


Group #1 at 10KM

3x Federation Praktor/Balra (Minmatar Frigate)
1x Republic Industrial (Mission Industrial)
  • Aggros shortly after warp in

Group #2 at 10KM

3x Federation Praktor/Balra (Minmatar Frigate)
3x Federation Pelekus/Arcus (Gallente Destroyer)
  • Appear as backup 4 minutes after aggression

Blitz
Destroy industrial, which has quite a bit of hp, collect workers (30m3) from container to complete the mission.
No Faction standing loss for killing the industrial ships.
Mineable Asteroids
None
Structures
None



Part 3 of 3

Faction: Gallente
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (mwd does not work)
Damage dealt: Therm
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Explosion
Recommended ship classes: Battlecruiser
Recommended generic setup: Heavy Damage

Warp-in


Group #1 at 12KM

2x Federation Insidiators (Gallente Frigate)
1x Federationn Matara (Gallente Destroyer)
  • Aggro's shortly after warp in

Group #2

3x Federation Praktor (Gallente Frigate)
2x Federation Arcus/Pelekus (Gallente Destroyer)
  • Spawn after group 1 is killed

Blitz
Mission complete with just blowing up the Manufacturing Outpost (Large Collidable Structure).
Faction standing loss of -2.4% for destroying (Gallente Federation's Caldari Deadspace Refining Outpost)
Mineable Asteroids
None
Structures
Manufacturing Outpost - Mission objective, Dropped nothing
Workers Quarters and Administration - Dropped nothing
2x Pressure Silos - Dropped nothing
