The Blockade, level 2


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 05:37 UTC

Faction: Blood Raiders
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal ?
Damage dealt: EM/Therm
Recommended damage dealing: EM/Therm
Recommended ship classes: Cruiser, AF.

Note: The waves are random, this is just an example.
Five waves. Auto aggro from each new wave.

Wave 1

4x Frigates (Corpii Diviner)
4x Destroyers (Corpior Converter)
1x Destroyer (Corpior Visionary) -- Trigger

Wave 2

Appears to be three groups

Group 1
2x Frigates (Corpii Collector) (tracking disruption)

Group 2
3x Frigates (Corpii Raider)
4x Destroyers (Corpior Converter)
1x Cruiser (Corpum Arch Reaver) -- Trigger

Group 3
3x Destroyers (Corpior Converter)
2x Cruiser (Corpum Arch Reaver)

Wave 3

Appears to be two groups

Group 1
2x Destroyers (Corpior Templar)

Group 2
4x Frigates (Elder Corpii Seeker) (tracking disruption)
4x Destroyers (Corpior Converter)
1x Cruiser (Corpum Arch Engraver) -- Trigger

Wave 4

Appears to be two groups

Group 1
2x Destroyers (Corpior Visionary)

Group 2
4x Frigates (Corpii Reaver)
4x Frigates (Elder Corpii Raider)
1x Cruiser (Corpum Arch Reaver) -- Trigger

Wave 5

4x Frigates (Corpii Reaver)
4x Destroyers (Corpior Friar)
1x Cruiser (Corpum Shadow Sage) -- Mission Complete

Blitz
Take out the trigger for each wave. Try to identify the three groups in wave 2 so you know which cruiser is the trigger, or just take out all cruisers.

Mineable Asteroid info
Warning: Asteroids are trapped. See comments.
3x large Veldspar asteroids (95K, 130K, 190K)
An entire belt of Pyroxeres, Plagioclase and Veldspar (about 15x Plagioclase, 25x Pyroxeres and 40x Veldspar, asteroids up to 50k)
