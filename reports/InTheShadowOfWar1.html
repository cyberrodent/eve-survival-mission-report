It allows T1 cruisers, T1 frigates, rookie ships and T1 destroyers. Nothing else will go in, although T2 frigates probably should be allowed in on principle.
-- ForteEXE (2009-05-23 18:59:33)
PART I - This mission is good for Caldari Insignia Tags harvesting. For those who are looking for Caldari Navy Captain Insignia I and Insignia IV this is a good easy mission. Fly a Cruiser like a Thorax, kill everything but in the THIRD room do not get close to the wrecked pod or your mission will be considered COMPLETE. If you manage to stay away from completing mission, you can just leave and come back after the daily restart, and so one for 6 days, then turn the mission in. Everyday the ships will re-spawn, granting you the same amount of Insignia.
