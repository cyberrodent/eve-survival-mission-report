Chain Reaction, Level 3


Last edited by MandyMoo
Tue, 14 Jul 2015 12:41 UTC

Faction: Gallente Federation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space with one gate
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Extras: Sensor Dampening
Scramble/Webify: Elite Federation Matara
Recommended damage dealing: kin/therm
Recommended ship classes: Cruiser, Battlecruiser.
Recommended generic setup: Sniper to blitz. Normal tank.


Single Pocket

Overview: Four groups of ships/towers, auto or proximity aggro (~40km).
Mission complete once you destroy the "Starbase Shield Generator" (72km) which will drop the shields and then allow you to destroy the "Starbase Main Reactor" (70km).

Group 1 (60km, auto-aggro)

3x Cruisers (Federation Praktor Centurion/Legionarius)
1x Battlecruisers (Federation Praktor Arx)

Group 2 (35km)

Note: Group 2 and Group 3 may be a single group which aggro's on proximity.
2x Cruiser (Federation Calo)
2x Frigate (Federation Praktor Harpago/Belos)
1x Elite Frigate (Elite Federation Matara) Webify/Scramble/Sensor Dampening

Group 3 (35km)

5x Cruiser (Republic Tribal Manadis/Jarpur)

Group 4 (70km)

3x Gallente Heavy Missile Battery
3x Gallente Light Missile Battery

Blitz
Destroy the Starbase Main Reactor, ~70km from the warp in point.

Structures
Towers drop t1 ammo, the Starbase Main Reactor drops Uranium and trade goods.
