Perimeter Hangar

Last edited by HurriCane
Thu, 24 Mar 2016 21:24 UTC

Faction: Sleepers
Signature type: Cosmic Anomaly
Space type: Normal Space
Damage dealt: Omni
Webbers: None
Scramblers: None
Extras: NOS (Emergent Watchman)
Recommended ship classes: Soloable with passive Drake
Recommended generic setup: Omni tank
Blue Loot Payout: 15.6 Million ISK

As with all Sleeper combat anomalies there are 3 waves each which auto aggress.
Sleeper ships may target switch. Drones and support ships are not immune.

Single Pocket


Initial Group

3x Cruisers (Awakened Patroller) last is TRIGGER
2x Frigates (Emergent Watchman) [neut]
Max Incoming DPS: 144 (EM/The 23%, Kin/Exp 27%)

Reinforcement Wave 1

1x Battleship (Sleepless Watchman)
2x Cruisers (Awakened Escort) last is TRIGGER
Max Incoming DPS: 422 (EM/The 42%, Kin/Exp 7%)


Reinforcement Wave 2

1x Battleship (Sleepless Escort)
2x Cruisers (Awakened Escort)
Max Incoming DPS: 328 (EM/The 18%, Kin/Exp 31%)

Note
  • Second wave spawns 60-80km from initial warpin, might not auto-aggress until it is 75-79km away.
  • The (Sleepless Watchman) will try to out-range you at ~70km away.
