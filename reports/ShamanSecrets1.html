Part 1 of 5



Faction: Serpentis / Angels
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace
Damage Dealt: Therm, Kin, Exp
Extras: None
Web/Scramble: None
Reccomended damage dealing: Kin / Exp


Pocket 1


6x Frigates (Coreli Initiates)

Pocket 2


3x Frigates (Gistii Thug/Rogue/Hijacker)

One of the kills will drop an Angel Cartel Pilot in a container which is required to be brought back to agent to complete the mission.

Type: Courier

Part 3 of 5


Faction: Mercenaries
Mission type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace
Damage Dealt: Mostly Therm/Kin
Extras: None
Web/scramble: None
Recommended damage dealing: Therm/Kin


Warp-in


6x Frigates (Mercenary rookies)

Pocket 1


7x Frigates (Mercenary Rookies/Fighter)

Pocket 2


The second level has Matique Karani which does not aggro and is an optional kill. He will reveal the location of the area needed to complete the next part upon entering that level.

Part 4 of 5




Faction: Mercenaries
Mission Type: Encounter
Space Type: Deadspace
Damage Dealt: therm/kin mostly
Extras: None
Web/Scramble: None
Reccomended damage dealing: Therm/Kin


Part 4 starts at warp-in on a moon with an agent, a blasted neon sign and a locked gateway. Get the mission from the agent, destroy the neon sign to get the key into the gateway(0.8% Minmatar Republic standing loss).

Inside the gateway is a Dari Akell and many habitation modules. Dari Akell will drop herself as a mission item when killed, and the habitation module nearest to her ship will drop 10 Refugees, 10 Dairy Products and 10 Superconductors. Bring Dari Akell back to the agent at the first level using the gateway to get Logut Akell and complete both missions.

Both habitation modules dropped loot.

Part 5 of 5


Type: Courier

Gives a +1 attribute implant.

Be sure to remember the location that Matique gives in Part 3, because in Part 4 you will be asked to fly there yet the mission window does not have a location link that you can click on. For me, it was Eram IV Moon 2.

When you fly to the moon, there will be an agent there. To get his mission, chat him then click the choice that declines (says something like Rightyoo). Then chat him again. I don't know what happens after that because I boobooed and instead clicked the first choice. He would not give his mission after that.

If, like me, you boobooed, do not give up. Instead, blow up the neon sign anyways, loot it to get the key, warp thru the acceleration gate, blow up Dari's ship, loot her, then use the accerlation gate in that pocket to warp back to that agent. Chat him, chose the choice "I have someone interesting" and he will replace the Dari item in your cargohold with Logut. Warp to station, chat Pinala and you're done.

The habitation module drops random trade goods. Mine was seed and wheat.
