The Score, Level 3 (Angel Cartel)


Last edited by MrOden:
update bounty
Sat, 29 Mar 2014 06:47 UTC

Faction: Angel Cartel
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Exp, Kin (Some thermal missiles)
Recommended damage dealing: Exp, Kin
Recommended ships: Retribution, Drake, Hurricane
Video guide: http://youtu.be/vigv6oDUwqE

The exact names of ships may be different, but there should be the same number of frigates / destroyers / cruisers / battlecruisers etc. per spawn.

No hostiles at the initial gate.


Pocket 1

Ships spawn in small groups a few seconds apart, no initial aggro
They all aggro when you shoot at any one.
Acceleration gate at 40km.



Single Group (40-70km)

7x Frigates (Gistii Ambusher/Nomad/Raider/Ruffian)Target Paint
2x Destoyers (Gistior Defacer/Haunter)
8x Cruisers (Gistum Crusher/Defeater/Depredator/Predator/Smasher)Target Paint

Asteroids
Mining causes a few frigs to spawn during the process, 3 may be the most at one time.

Veldspar - 8 Asteroids - 199,996 units - 19,999.6 m3
Plagioclase - 6 asteroids - 173,925 units - m3
Pyroxeres - 6 asteroids - 191,877 units - 57563.1 m3

Structures
No loot

Angel Bronze Tag from one of the wrecks

Pocket 2

Groups appear a few seconds apart, no initial aggro.



Group 1 (60-70km) (left)

6x Frigates (Gistii Ambusher/Raider/Thug)
4x Cruisers (Gistum Centurion/Depredator/Liquidator/Phalanx/Predator)
1x Battlecruiser (Gistatis Primus/Tribuni)

Group 2 (60-65km) (right)

2x Frigates (Gistii Ambusher/Outlaw/Raider/Thug)
2x Cruisers (Gistum Centurion/Depredator/Liquidator/Marauder/Phalanx/Predator)

Tip
Group 1 is the objective. Shooting at it causes full pocket aggro.

Bounty
Total bounty 945,753

Video
The video in the following link shows you where everyone will show up, and their range. This guide also only shows one of many ways to take out the bad guys.
http://youtu.be/vigv6oDUwqE
