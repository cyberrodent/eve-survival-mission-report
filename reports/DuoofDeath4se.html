Duo of Death, level 4


Last edited by KrakilFrostborn
Fri, 04 Jan 2013 15:38 UTC

Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace with gates
Damage dealt: TH, KI (some exp and EM from the sentries)
Recommended damage dealing: KI, TH
Recommended ship classes: Dominix
Recommended generic setup: Short range 7-8km for ships and 35 km for Towers. Very easy mission

One Pocket:


2x Battleships (Core Lord Admirals) (Orbit range 8km)
2x Frigates (Spider drones II) (Orbit range: 7,5km. 100% chance of web. 5% chance of scramble)
3x Tower Sentry Serpentis III (+/- 35 km from warp in point)

Aggro from towers.

The Objective item is dropped from the second Core Lord Admiral Wreck.
