Intercept The Saboteurs, level 2


Last edited by AwwWhatThe
Tue, 30 Oct 2012 13:48 UTC

Mission type: Deadspace
Faction: Minmatar Republic
Damage dealt: Explosive. Some thermal and kinetic.
Web/scram: Chief Republic Isak
Recommended damage: Explosive
Recommended ships: Cormorant, Cerberus.

Warp in


Aggro On Warp (10 sec)
1x Minmatar transport
3x Republic Baldur/Takan

Room 1 (after first gate):


Group 1 (All aggro on warp in 20k)

7x Republic Gleeda/Baldur/Otur
1x Republic Takan

Group 2 (80-100km on warp in)

8x Republic Baldur/Takan/Gleeda/Otur
1x Minmatar Transport Ship
1x Chief Republic Isak/Iflin (Web/Scram)

Group 3 (120k from warp in)

1x Chief Republic Isak/Iflin (Web/Scram)
10x Republic Takan/Baldur/Borman/Otur
2x Republic Austri/Bormin
1x Minmatar Transport

Tip
Don't forget to grab the can of Confiscated Virus Material

Standings
Killing one of the transporters incurs a 2.4% standing penalty

Blitz
Last killed ship drops the can
Final ship of group 3 dropped the viral agent for me, I ignored all of the rest apart from the transport ship in group 2. Unknown whether it is neccessary to kill the transport ship in group 2.
