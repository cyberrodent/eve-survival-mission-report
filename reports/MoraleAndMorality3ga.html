Morale and Morality, Level 3


Last edited by DesmontMcCallock
Sat, 14 Jul 2012 07:54 UTC

Faction: Gallente Federation
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Normal space with one gate
Damage dealt: Kin/therm
Extras: Dampening
Recommended damage dealing: kin/therm
Recommended ship classes: Cruiser, Battle Cruiser
Recommended generic setup: normal.

Overview: Single pocket with 2 groups, one spawn.
Mission is complete once group 2 is destroyed.
Spawn triggered by last ship in group 1.

Single Pocket


Group 1

2x frigate (Federation Praktor Harpago)
3x Cruiser (Federation Praktor Legionarius)
1x Elite Cruiser (Elite Federation Quadrieris)

Group 2

4x Federation Industrial

Spawn 1

This group spawns once last ship in group 1 is destroyed.
It appears at the same location as the Industrials.

2x frigage (Elite Federation Matara) sensor dampening
2x Battle Cruiser (Federation Praktor Auxilia/Arx)

Blitz
Tank the room and destroy the four Industrials.

Tip
Mission is complete once four industrials are destroyed.
