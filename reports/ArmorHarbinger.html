Harbinger


Role
  • Below average close range dps with pulse lasers. Non-existent mid range dps.
  • No sniper role.

Skills
  • Certificate : Turret Control Improved
  • Certificate : Armor Reinforcement Standard
  • Medium Pulse Laser Specialization 3
  • Controlled Bursts 5
  • Battlecruisers 4
  • Energy Weapon Rigging 4

Fitting Notes
  • Heavy Pulse fitted.
  • Typical rigs : Energy Burst Aerator or Collision Accelerator / Ancillary Current Router / Anti-Thermic Pump / Anti-Kinetic Pump / Anti-Explosive Pump.
  • Ship's Kin-Exp resistance is given more priority for better defence against more damaging missile hits.

Site Doctrines

Vanguard
  • Stationary fleets do not require for afterburner to be fitted.
  • Mobile fleets require either an afterburner or MWD to be fitted.
  • Heavy Pulse Laser fitted (typical).

Assault
  • Afterburner fitted (improves survivability).
  • Heavy Pulse Laser fitted (typical).

HQ
  • ATTENTION!! DO NOT use for HQ sites.
