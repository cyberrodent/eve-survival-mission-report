Sansha Forsaken Den


Last edited by ShintuFelper
Sat, 28 Mar 2015 20:18 UTC

Faction: Sansha
Location: Lowsec & Nullsec
Damage Dealt: EM/Therm
Web/Scramble: Centii Loyal Minion/Ravener/Servant
Recommended Damage Dealing: EM (best), Therm(secondary)

Single Pocket


On warp-in everything will agro. Final kill is trigger for next wave.

Initial Group

2-3x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Fiend/Hellhound/Torturer)
2-3x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon)

Wave 1

2-3x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Butcher/Enslaver/Plague)
2-3x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Fiend/Hellhound)

Wave 2

3-4x Elite Cruiser (Centum Loyal Fiend/Hellhound/Mutilator)
4x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon)

Wave 3

3-4x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon)

Wave 4

3-4x Cruiser (Centum Fiend/Hellhound/Mutilator/Torturer)
3-4x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon)

Wave 5

2-3x Elite Frigate (Centii Loyal Minion/Ravener/Servant) Scramble & Web
2-3x Battlecruiser (Centatis Behemoth/Daemon)
