Avenge a Fallen Comrade, Level 1


Last edited by SinSmith
Thu, 20 Mar 2014 22:38 UTC

Faction: Gurista Pirates
Mission type: Encounter
Spacetype: Unknown
Damage dealt: Kin, Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kin, Therm

Video: Corax

Group 1: (48km)

4x Frigates (Pithi Arrogator/Saboteur/Despoiler)

Group 2: (60km)

3x Frigates (Pithi Invader/Infiltrator)

One of the frigates from Group 2 will trigger:

First Spawn: (60km)

5x Frgiates (Pithi Arrogator)

Shooting one of the frigates will trigger:

Second Spawn: (60km)

5x Frigates (Pithi Arrogator)

Blitz:
Destroy the Habitat. Remember it will draw aggro.

Tip: If you do not want to chase the 60km, fire on the Habitat. It will aggro the spawn and they will fly to you.
