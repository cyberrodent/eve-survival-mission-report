Slaves Must be Returned, level 3


Last edited by ElemWiz:
added ISK
Sat, 03 May 2014 12:51 UTC

Faction: Minmatar
Mission type: Hybrid
Damage dealt: EM/Exp/Therm/Kin
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic
Recommended ships: Ferox, Vengeance, Drake

Mission objective drops from named cruiser.

Single Pocket


Single Group: (40km) (Automatic aggro)

1x Freedom Patriot (cruiser)
1x Named cruiser
4x Minmatar Freedom Fighter (frigates)

Named cruiser may be one of the following:
1x Akori (Caracal)
1x Ibrahim
1x Karothas (Augoror)

Structures

Asteroid Station drops no loot.

ISK

Bounty : ~50,000 ISK
Loot : ~2,430,000 ISK (EVE estimated, salvage included)

Standing

2.4% standing penalty with Minmatar Republic.

Mining

279059 units of Veldspar from 32 asteroids - 35-55km from entry point
