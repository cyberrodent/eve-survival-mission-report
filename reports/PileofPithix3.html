Last edited by PaddoSwam:

Mon, 25 Jul 2011 16:39 UTC

Mission type: Mining/Encounter
Space type: Deadspace w/o gate. Warp to bookmark works.
Faction: Rogue Drones
Damage dealt: All kinds
Recommended damage dealing: EM
Recommended ships: Drake (Covetor with T2 Medium drones easily possible with medium drone skills, interfacing, durability, specialization lvl 3)
Extras: Objectiveis to defeat any rogue drones present & mine 4,500 units of Pithix.

Single Pocket

Asteroids present:
1x Pithix roid 4500 units (a Jaspet variant)

First Spawn:

1x Sentient Destructor Alvum

Second Spawn:

2x Sentient Sunder Alvi
1x Sentient Render Alvi

Third Spawn:

1x Sentient Annihilator Alvum

Fourth Spawn:

1x Annihilator Alvum
2x Silverfish Alvi
