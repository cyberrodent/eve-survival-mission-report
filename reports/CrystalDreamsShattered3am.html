Crystal Dreams Shattered, Level 3


Last edited by KirtaadTzash:
Added standing loss info
Sun, 24 Jan 2016 07:53 UTC

Faction: Amarr & Mercenaries
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace
Damage dealt: Kin
Recommended damage dealing: Kin
Recommended ships: Drake
Difficulty: Easy
Hint: Speed tank possible, take out the webbing frig first.

Pocket 1 (no aggro)


All groups aggro on proximity, but independently. Gate is locked.

Group 1 (36-38km)

3x Cruisers (Mercenary Commander)

Group 2 (40-43km)

3x Cruisers (Mercenary Corporal)

Pocket 2 (aggro on warp in)


Group 1 (36-38km)

1x Battlecruiser (Imperial Avenger)

Group 2 (20km, appears when you shoot Group 1)

1x Frigate (Mercenary Wingman)

Group 3 (34km, appears when you shoot Group 1)

6x Frigates (Mercenary Elite, Mercenary Fighter, Mercenary Wingman)
1x Cruiser (Mercenary Corporal)

Group 4 (trigger: none appears after a short time)

1x Battleship (Mercenary Overlord)

Group 5 (trigger unknown when last of group 2 is killed)

1x Battleship (Mercenary Overlord)

Group 6 (trigger when Terrorist killed)

2x Cruisers (Mercenary Lieutenant) or 3x Mercenary Elite

Standing loss:
2.4% with the Amarr Empire

Blitz:
Not possible, must kill all ships
