Dark Lesson, A level 2


Last edited by AsdayXyfu:
Added a space. :3
Fri, 02 Oct 2009 17:27 UTC

Faction: Caldari State
Mission type: Encounter
Space type: Deadspace (Mwd available)
Damage dealt: Kinetic, thermal.

Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic (thermal)
Done in an: Iskur

Mission briefing

"During a routine recon operation inside a nearby contested sector, we discovered a large facility housing thousands of Caldari citizens.
In all likelihood they are assisting State forces in some way, either directly or indirectly. We have an opportunity for to remove the threat,
whatever it may be. Though it is a grim task I ask of you, it is one that must be done. "

Objective
Take out the Industrials that re-supply the Colony.

Single Pocket

Group 1: (Auto Aggro)
41-80Km from warp in point
1 x Battlecruisers (State Kanpaku)

5x Cruisers
4 x State Shukuro Ashigaru
1 x State Shukuro Chugen

4 x Frigates :
2 x State Shukuro Shuriken
2 x State Shukuro Shinai

2 x State Industrial

Wave 2:
4 x State Wakizashi
3 x State Katana


A few seconds after you warp in, the whole room, except the 2 state industrials, aggros you.
Stay at 40km and killed all the cruisers one after another with drones. After killing the last cruiser, wave 2 spawns.

After clearing wave 2, pop the 2 industrials and you will receive the confirmation message:

"The Industrials are down. Try inflicting as much damage to the civilian compound before returning to your agent."
