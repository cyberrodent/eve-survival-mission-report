Faction: Serpentis
Mission type: Encounter
Damage dealt: Kin/Therm
Recommended damage dealing: Kinetic
Recommended ships: Caracal

Note: Spawns and triggers may vary, this is just an example

Initial Group: (20km) (Automatic aggro)

1x Starbase Stasis Tower
5x Frigates (Coreli Initiate/Guard) (Trigger)

Spawn 1: (50-60km)

7x Frigates (Coreli Initiate/Guard/Scout) (Trigger)

Spawn 2: (100km)

7x Frigates (Coreli Initiate/Watchman) (Sensor Dampening) (Trigger)

Spawn 3: (115-130km)

8x Frigates (Coreli Initiate/Scout/Patroller) (Sensor Dampening) (Trigger)

Spawn 4: (15km)

4x Frigates (Coreli Watchman/Safeguard) (Sensor Dampening)

Extra
Mission area contains considerable amount of ores: Veldspar
Structures drop no loot
